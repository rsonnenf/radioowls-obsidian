## Summary of Last Session
---
 After a night's sleep, a snake enters the cabin while The Works In Progress wake up. We discern that someone is looking through the eyes of the snake. It appears to be a familiar. Zos scoots the snake toward the door, and we see a human male on the other side of the door, startled. He notes that he learned that we showed his men mercy. He says he wants to talk along with one Captain Ward. An Elven woman with red eyes walks in after him. They both use mold earth to sit in dirt chairs. The snake slithers up the elf, whose name is Sylera. They are trying to find a cure for their people and propose working together to save the scientists, but they want the ability to use the first wish from the wishing well. After discussing, we tell them that we're looking to get our friends back, and we want the first wish to do so, but they can use theirs afterwards. They provide a plan: we accept the curse that afflicts the Crimson Claw at the keep. This will ensure that we are motivated to find a cure for them at the well along with us. We'll find the scholars, get them out. We counter that we'll go to another homestead after being bitten tonight, and we'll be chased out tomorrow to ensure that the scholars are too scared to leave our sides. They agree. Zos leaves out her arm and allows herself to be bitten, allowing herself to be a weresabertooth tiger Grubby allows his neck to be bitten to become a wereowl. We are to set out tomorrow for the keep. Sylera will guide us underground while Zos tunnels our way there. While we are underground, Sylera will cause a blood moon to occur, allowing the Hunt to start. This will draw some out of the keep. The next morning, we start our journey to the keep from underground. Sylera uses magic to bring about the blood moon. We eventually reach the inside of the keep. Sylera skitters away. We approach the cell and find a white owlin woman and break her out. She runs toward the tunnel, and Grubby grabs a log book while the rest of us follow the owlin.
