## Summary of Last Session
---
 [[The Works In Progress]] and [[Kalinar/Notable People/Moose|Moose]] engaged [[Donovon Ortofola]] and the dead members of their group in an intense battle. Not long after the battle commenced, Barnaby threw his portable hole near Ortofola, then threw his bag of holding into it, creating a gate to the Astral Plane. Ortofola was sucked into the gate and sent to the Astral Plane. The group then focused their efforts on battling their deceased friends. While they were able to knock out several people, Moose fired a crossbow bolt at Fax while he was on the ground, believing him to get ready to make an attack at [[Zos]]. After attempts to figure out how to bring their friends back to life came up fruitless--at least for the time being, the group began discussing their next steps.
