## Summary of Last Session
---
[[The Works in Progress]] inspect the cabin after the battle. Inside, it is musty. There is rotted furniture and a tackle box on the ground. There are some runes on the inside of the door. They are runes of an alarm spell. Someone has been informed that the door has been opened. It looks like only the fireplace and tackle box are being used. [[Grubby]] looks inside the box and finds papers written in charcoal. They are records with dates and time stamps that log who has been through and checked in. [[Zos]] turned into an owl and flew up the chimney to investigate, after having seen a half-burned log in the fireplace. Although much of the roof contains overgrowth, the chimney was clear. We discern that this is a check-in spot on a patrol, and the alarm door was meant to inform others who did not know that the *chimney* was the main entryway. Zos flew above and saw a few clearings and the river nearby. We decide to rest in the cabin and prepare to plan to get the scholars back. Our plan as of now is to triangulate the location of the camp and have Zos tunnel us toward it.
