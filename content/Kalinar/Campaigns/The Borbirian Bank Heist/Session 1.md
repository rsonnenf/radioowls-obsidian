## Summary of Last Session
---
![[Pasted image 20240629090014.png|\right|500]]After [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] encounter an arrow with a message asking them to assist the [[Red Thorn]] with procuring some sort of item from a bank in [[Borbir]], they decided to oblige. After confirming through a court wizard that Zif is still alive, they go to the royal gardens in an attempt to use a tree to travel to Borbir. While attempting to find the Faire B&B, they meet [[Notable People/Moose|Moose]], who convinces them to allow him to lead them to the meeting location. They enter the Faire B&B and eventually meet with [[Rilad]]. She has been keeping tabs on the Works In Progress for some time.  Rilad explains that, in addition to money, [[The Bank of Borbir]] houses artifacts and information dating back to the oldest times of Kalinar, including when gods walked among those in Kalinar. She explains that she wants to move the Red Thorn in a different direction, which requires 5 million gold. She asks the group to place a device in the Bank in order to obtain the gold. Rilad explains that the Red Thorn has been seen as a criminal organization for too long. She wants the group to be a more authoritative, legitimate organization, like the Emperor.  A nonpolitical organization to help the people when needed. She explains that the Borbir bank isn't even on this plane of existence. She explains that she learned this that Moose posed as a teller at the bank who learned that the back room is not within this plane of existence. Eventually, a man walked in--[[Donovon Ortofola]], who attacked the group several weeks prior.
