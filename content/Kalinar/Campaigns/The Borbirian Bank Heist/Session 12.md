## Summary of Last Session
---
 [[The Works In Progress]] returned to the house through the tunnel. [[Zos]] noticed a carving in an indentation on the ground that read "From one lichen to another." Inside is a note that reads, "Don't let the owlin find this," along with a sending stone. The owlin is named Lyra Windmire. She explains that she and the other scholars were sailing along the river when sharks attacked the ship, causing it to go down. They were interrogated. Her friend, Eldrin, who knows the location of the wishing well, went missing some time ago after traveling north to a Dwarven city named Tha'l Durahl in the Tha'Lanel Wastes following a clue to find the wishing well. We make it to the next hideout, a warehouse in a town called Willowbrook. We stayed for the night. The next morning, Barnaby followed Lyra to people watch in the small town. Lyra is new to adventuring and wants to document her experience. While they are out, Zos sends a message via a sending stone to the other lichens asking if we can teleport up to the northern region. They respond that they are suspicious of us teleporting away and would want to teleport with us. We decide to travel up on foot. Our route will take us through Varnithel, and if we do not find any travel aids, we will go to Theel.
