## Summary of Last Session
---
 As [[The Works In Progress|the Works in Progress]] continue moving through the time loop, more people are getting stuck in the loop with us. We speak to the man casting the protection spell, who tells us that he is protecting us from a [[adult-red-dragon|Red Dragon]]. He explained that [[Varnithel]]'s quick sums of gold that have been gathered brought about the attention of the dragon, which seeks to take the gold for itself. Eventually, we decide that Stone and [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] will sprint for the dragon; [[Zos]] and [[Grubby]] will attempt to escape by [[Tree Stride|Tree Striding]] through a tree; and [[Kalinar/Notable People/Moose|Moose]] stays with the man to see what he does. Zos and Grubby are able to escape, but the rest of the group move back to the beginning of the time loop. Stone asks the man his name, and he claims that he is known as the Unlucky Penny. Barnaby darted toward an area where he thought he might find the dragon, and something incredibly large charged toward him. It opens its mouth and blasts fire at him. They return to the beginning of the loop. Zos and Grubby emerge on the far side of Varnithel, which has grown larger with paved roads and additional buildings. The sending stone contains several messages, suggesting we were in the time loop for several regular-world days, from the lychens indicating that they believe the Works in Progress betrayed them. Grubby and Zos sent a message explaining the time loop before they began to brainstorm how to get the rest of the group out of the loop. In the loop, Stone banishes Unlucky Penny, and the world went dark. A dome of fire erupted from the direction of the checkpoint. Time begins to tick normally. Unlucky Penny turned into a [[adult-copper-dragon|Copper Dragon]], determining that he could not stop the Red Dragon, potentially dooming Varnithel.
