## Summary of Last Session
---
 The infiltration of [[The Bank of Borbir|bank]] begins. Moose makes his way to the doors and finds two guards, Alric and Greg at the doors. He communicates with the group via collect call but is inexperienced and outwardly shows signs of distraction. The guards became concerned, and he mimed being sick, drawing both guards away from the doors. The rest of the group made their way through the door. Moose convinced one of the guards to clean up his vomit, induced by old [[Goodberry|goodberries]] given to him by [[Zos]], but while trying to stop the other guard, the bank's wizard, Lynia, approached. Moose used a single-use invisibility ring given to him by the [[Red Thorn]] before the heist. After entering the vault, Lynia and Greg follow, but the group, now totally invisible, do not draw their attention. They eventually leave the vault. The group enters the next room, where they find a table. After leaving valuable items on the table, a door on the other side of the room opened, allowing them to move through to the next room, the main chamber of the vault.
