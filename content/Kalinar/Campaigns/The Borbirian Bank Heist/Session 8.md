## Summary of Last Session
---
[[The Works In Progress]] teleported via tree back to [[Borbir]] in the hopes of getting information to resurrect their friends. Back in Borbir, [[Kalinar/Notable People/Moose|Moose]] deflates the group's plans to us him as a hostage and convinces them to let him ask [[Rilad]] if he can get the other group members back. [[Grubby]] follows. Moose meets Rilad, seeing two other members of the [[Red Thorn]] there, part of another, more brutal, sect. Red Thorn wants to be aggressive. Without the money, she doesn't have any bargaining power. She explains that they have been raiding villages and torturing people to learn information about a rumored Wishing Well. Rilad allows Moose to decide whether he wants to stay or leave to pursue other endeavors, pointing at a map to give him a clue about something. After much deliberation, Moose decides to leave to follow his friends. She gives Moose a red stone as he turns in his Red Thorn signet. It is a flawless ruby worth 10,000-20,000 gp. She then takes a book, "The Wildlife and Dangerous Creatures of Kalinar - The Deer and Moose," Moose's codename book, and burns it. Rilad then announces that an "intruder" is in the base, giving Moose the opportunity to run away. Moose leaves and grabs the bag containing the bodies of the members of The Works In Progress and Cervo and leaves to meet up with the rest of the group. We decide to head toward the southern Misty Forest in order to find the Wishing Well. Moose knows that the Crimson Fangs operate out of southern Misty Forest. While making our way to the southern Misty Forest, Moose was swept up into a trap.
