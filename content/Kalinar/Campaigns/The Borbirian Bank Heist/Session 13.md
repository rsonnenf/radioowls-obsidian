## Summary of Last Session
---
![[bbh_s_13.jpg|right|750]] [[The Works in Progress]] travel along the path to [[Varnithel]] to see if we can find transportation to the Tha'lanel Wastes. Along the way, we speak with Lyra, the owlin we saved, who wants to learn more about the group. As we approach Varnithel, we enter into the forest to avoid a toll. We come upon a guard checkpoint and pay a toll, which is being used for funding for Varnithel. In the compound, [[Zos]] notices some sort of creature--a fox or hawk--which is unusual in this area. We discern that these are the lycan following us. It appears again, a flash of red. We approach another checkpoint. It is the same one we just paid. We think we are in a time loop. At one point, [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] talks to someone and asks for the date. He learns that the date is several weeks old. We try a different approach this time. We try to plow through the barricade, and guards begin to chase us. But the loop restarts. Even so, one of the individuals at the checkpoint, someone who was eating with his horse, informs us that he knows we are in a time loop. Stone calls upon his deity, Moridan, to tell us how to break the loop. Moridan tells us that we are in some sort of protection spell. The old man has an aura of good, and he is casting some sort of protection spell.
