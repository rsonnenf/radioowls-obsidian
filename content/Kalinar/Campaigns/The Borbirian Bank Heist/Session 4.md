## Summary of Last Session
---
After dispatching the undead prior members of [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]], the group put their bodies into a bag of holding in the hopes of bringing them back to life at some point. After doing so, they discussed next plans, and while doing so, they found a map left by [[Donovon Ortofola]]. Upon opening it, they found it to be a map of the interior of the vault in [[The Bank of Borbir|the Bank of Borbir]]. After surveying it and deciding they would attempt to break into the bank vault to look for the gold and hopefully a scroll of resurrection, they decided to try to get to know [[Kalinar/Notable People/Moose|Moose]] more by playing a drinking game: Two Truths and a Lie. Moose continuously lost and passed out. [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] began speaking with [[Rilad]], who told him that if he wanted to seek out his deceased friends, he should go to the Astral Plane. The next day, the party began planning their heist, determining to enter through a bathroom window Moose weakened before making their way to one of the three doors containing the switching statutes that would give them access to the vault.
