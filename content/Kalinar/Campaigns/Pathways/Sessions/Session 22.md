Shiki led you to a den that seeps red light like the type you saw outside of [[Bilell]]. After trying to lure whatever was inside back out, without success, you walked inside to find a bunch of deer. Shiki began laughing, gloating that he had successfully lured you inside to feast upon you. He pulled back his hood, revealing a wolf's head that attacked and killed one of the deer. He attacked you, and [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] threw him through the portal gem and into the realm of [[Dranech]], a graveyard realm. You find Shiki impaled on the sword of a statue in the courtyard. You also saw Bahamut chained up and totally unconscious. Zos attempted to speak with the plants, and they only gave her messages of doom. As you all attempted to free Bahamut with Magic, Dranech appeared. She is the goddess of rot and decomposition, and she looks the part. She has grayish, green skin, and a distinct rotten smell. She explained that she is keeping Bahamut trapped her while the Blight continue their efforts to create a permanent rift into which the Blight can enter your realm and take it over. During this speech, [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] attacked, and a battle commenced!
