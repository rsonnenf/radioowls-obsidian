You walked to the tavern to parade [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] around, in the hopes of drawing out whoever might be trying to harm him or his mother. Throughout the night, though, no harm came to him, and you did not find anyone who looked suspicious. You went back to the Artificer's Guild to sleep, all in the same room, anticipating that someone from the [[Red Thorn]] Syndicate might try and strike. Something did happen, but not quite what you were expecting. A bird-like humanoid individual named [[Deekek]] flew in through the window and woke up Barnaby. He told Barnaby that members of the [[Dire Clan]] were seeking revenge for your party having killed Egart Elderberry, and they were on their way as you spoke. He told you that he could not engage in the fight with you, because it was not his fight to have, but he told you, if you survived, to meet him in the tavern tomorrow afternoon. He has work for an experienced band of adventurers like you, if you are willing to take it.
