Upon seeing [[Zif]] in [[the T'durn]] camp, [[Zos]] reached out to Zif to communcate with him. She learned that he did not seem to be in his right mind. He recognized Zos and seemed to be regretful for his association with the T'durn, but she could sense that he was being compelled to assist them. After the leader of the T'durn rubbed a mass of acorns held by string against Zif's forehead, Zif's eyes went glassy, and he heeded any commands given to him by the T'durn leader. You waited until evening came and, after sending a bird into a tent that Zif had entered and drawing him out, [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] vortex warped him away and to your group, which remained hidden in the foliage outside of the camp. Zif explained that the T'Durn have been attacking villages and that they took him as he was traveling the forest. He was taken for his powers and had been used to augment the raiding parties' abilities, making them stronger when they attacked nearby villages. He explained that the T'Durn were using some sort of unnatural force to twist the forest to their will, and Zos, upon reaching out and sensing the forest, recognized the telltale signs of the Blight aiding the T'Durn and their efforts. The forest seemeed poisoned by the T'Durn's efforts. After agreeing to rid the forest of the T'Durn's influence, you attacked, dispatching much of the force with ease. You captured the leader and attemnpted to question him about Heartwood and what he knew of the individual. The leader noted that he knew Heartwood only by reputation but knew of an Oak Heart. He noted that he reverently held a piece of Oak Heart in his pocket, and [[Grubby]] immediately grabbed it. The leader explained that Oak Heart was a tree that spread his wisdom throughout the Misty Forest, and as he spoke, he said, "Ardenel." Unexpectedly, the trinket flew into the air and transformed into a massive tree that attacked you. You dispatched the tree, but not before [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] brutally shot the leader, as he lay on the ground, with an arrow. Zif, grateful to have been saved, now looks forward to speaking with Zos more to learn of what she has been doing since he has been taken.

The woods are poisoned.
