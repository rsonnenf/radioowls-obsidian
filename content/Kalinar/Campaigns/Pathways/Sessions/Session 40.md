[[Heartwood]] was clearly upset by [[Grubby]]'s mention of dragons and his efforts to gain their abilities to stop the blight. He explained that dragons once roamed Kalinar freely and were common. They had little regard for the forest, though, and allowed their squabbles with other dragons and those attempting to steal from their hoards to cause significant collateral damage to the forest. After significant efforts to convince Heartwood to help you, he agreed to give Grubby information about what is likely the last remaining [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]] in existence in Kalinar and to provide transport to Den Fatum if you rid the forest of the poison of the blight, which is caused by a lesser god that has taken up residence in a cave in the forest nearby. You make your way to the cave to find the god, guarded by two of what Heartwood believed were dragons. The creatures flew out of the cave, and the god emerged ready to attack as two portals boiled out of the ground.
