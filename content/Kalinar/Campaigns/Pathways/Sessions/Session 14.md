After defeating the blood giant blocking your way into a ceremonial chamber, you walked through another portal in that room, which teleported you to some sort of hellish throne room. The leader of this realm, [[Fulad-Zereh]], revealed himself and informed you of his and his companions' plans to take control of your realm--the Overrealm. After an intense battle, you defeated Fulad-Zereh, and a woman and winged dog appeared in a blinding light. The woman introduced herself as Mayari, the goddess of the moon, with her companion, Kaibigan. Mayari informed you that she had been trapped by Fulad-Zereh, like two other gods, Bahamut and Grummsh. She explained that the three of them were members of the Pantheon seeking to thwart the efforts of the Blight, a group of dark gods and goddesses who have launched a plan to take over the Overrealm and rule it with their malice and might. Unfortunatey, she and her companions were captured, but your efforts in killing Fulaz-Zereh freed her. Given your skill in dispatching a minor god, she has asked you to save the other two captured gods and to assist in the saving of the Overrealm. You, who have newly christened yourself [[The Works In Progress]], agreed and learned that Bahamut is likely somewhere in the Frozen Pine, and Grummsh is in the region of Den Fatum. You are promised training, items, and everlasting light for your help. And as personal thanks for saving her, Mayari tells you that she will answer one question you might have, and she will give an answer if it is in her power to provide it. The Works In Progress asked, "Where is [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]]'s mother?"
