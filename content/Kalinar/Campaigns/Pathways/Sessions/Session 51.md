## Summary of Last Session
---
 Continuing through the Mines, [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] come across a chamber containing a Mithril Dragon named [[Aurimis]]. They convince Aurimis to help them rid [[the Mines]] of the Underrealm creatures with the promise that Aurimis will be able to take the newly vacant space as his new lair. After securing Aurimis's help, he leaves to get his father's help as well. The party continues on through a maze of rock where they were able to avoid a scorpion devil and exit without alerting it. On the other side, the party encountered three dwarvish ghosts who asked for their help giving them peace so they could move on. They asked the party to kill the three devils that killed them, and in return, they would tell them where the portal to the Underrealm is. [[The Works In Progress]] agreed and made their way to the northwest, where they found an Underrealm structure apparently built by devils that has been there for some time without the dwarves' knowledge. Once inside, they searched and found a room containing three devils posed and ready to attack.
