Upon hearing that Varnithel is in danger, Fax immediately started packing things at the campsite. You all started making the remainder of the journey to Varnithel. After a day, as you approached the town, you noticed a plume of smoke rising in the distance and saw the glow of flames engulfing several buildings. You continued making your way toward the town, where you saw several druids attempting to bring down the Mayor's Manor. You leaped into action. During the fight, the druids called in two animated trees, which caused additional havoc. Fax attempted to fly Mayor Burnside out of the manor, and he was successful, but one of the druids cast a wall of fire. The ensuing flames engulfed the mayor, killing him. Bo was able to kill one of the druids, and attempted to absorb his soul. Barnaby, however, stole the soul before Bo could wholly absorb it.  Fax was able to save First Lady Alina Fox from the window, and Barnaby cast feather fall on the rest of the staff still in the manor before eliminating the rest of the enemies. After doing so, Barnaby cast revivify, bringing the Mayor back to life. However, the town is still in flames, and you need to find a way to save it!
