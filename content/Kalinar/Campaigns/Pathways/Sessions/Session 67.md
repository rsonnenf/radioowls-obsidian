## Summary of Last Session
---
![[Pasted image 20240215214845.png|right|500]]As [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] fell off the mountain, Thava did what she could to protect him, and though the gods might have extended some semblance of mercy, they stood idle, and [[The Works In Progress]] lost their friend, [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]], who was reunited with his family in the afterlife. The rest of the party emerged on the other side of the portal to a host of demons making their way to the portal. After dispatching them, they learned that a stone tethered the portal to Kalinar as long as it remained intact. While [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] examined this, the rest of the group learned of [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]]'s death upon Eldrid's scrying. With [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]]'s death confirmed, [[Grubby]] began to spiral. He talked of the pointlessness of the mission and of feeling like a pawn in the supposed omnipotent gods' games. In the moment, he resolved to kill [[Mayari]] for what she ended up putting them through, and [[Zos]] tried to refocus him toward escaping the Underrealm for the time being. Whether he was turned away from his new plan of vengeance remains to be seen.
