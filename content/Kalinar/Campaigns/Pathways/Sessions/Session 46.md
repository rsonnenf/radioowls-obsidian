## Summary of Last Session
---
 You walked into [[Den Fatum]] and began taking in the city. You first visited the Great Forge, where Fax made a donation in an effort to get in the good graces of the Dwarves who have been very isolationist the last century or so. Eventually, a man walked up to you, named [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid]]. He tells you that he is new to the city and here on state business. You invite him for drinks at the [[Anvil and Ale]], a tavern you passed, and he gave a partial commit, as he seemed interested on why individuals like you are in the city. You then made your way to a shop, where the wizard owner cast [[RPG Rules/5e SRD/spells/Sending|Sending]] for you. You informed [[Feril]] about the corrupted dragon and eagles headed his way, and he responded that he would keep an eye open. You then made your way to the tavern, where you learned that the King, [[Fargrim Ironfist]], seems to be digging into his isolationist ways as a result of his grief for having lost his husband. You also called upon [[Mayari]], who informed you that she learned that the portal leading to Grummsh appears to be hidden in the mines under Den Fatum. The barkeep, [[Balor Hammerstrike|Balor]], told you that the mines are a sacred place and heavily guarded by the royal guards. He told you that you, as outsiders, were unlikely to be granted entrance, as not even many dwarves are allowed into them. He did note, though, that he has some contacts in the Great Hall, and he might be able to convince them to try and get you an audience with the King for you to plead your case. To do so is dangerous for the barkeep, though, as if word got out that he was helping outsiders into the sacred mines, he might be ostracized, so he asked for 75 GP and Fax's cloak to make the risk worth it. Fax said he would think about it, and you all went to bed after Eldrid did not appear.
