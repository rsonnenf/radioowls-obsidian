Once you gain your bearings after the mercenary attack, you decide to make your way to a magic shop to restock on goods. Before following the group, [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] checks the bodies of the mercenaries. He finds a duplicate contract on his life that he had seen in Councilwoman Forrest's possession. He burned the contracts somberly, them being the last vestigates of his old life. Before [[Grubby]], before serving Emperor [[Fenn]], before the death of his family. He met up with the group and Craigug, and they went to a magic shop where Barnaby obained a scroll of sending and 1200 gp worth of diamonds. You went to the Horned Hen, an inn, where you cast the sending scroll to communicate with [[Feril]]. He told you that he left [[Bilell]] in his airship with members of the artificer's guild. He told you that he is on his way to Theel. Craigug told you all that he intends to stay in [[Borbir]] for some time before eventually making his way to Feril in Theel. You all agreed, and Fax and Grubby gave him 20 gp each. You decided to investigate [[The T'durn]] in the Misty Forest. Since it was near [[Varnithel]], you decided to make your way there to check on the town and get some rest before venturing into the forest. Along the way to Varnithel, you encountered a mass of unnatural vines that attacked you in the middle of the night!
