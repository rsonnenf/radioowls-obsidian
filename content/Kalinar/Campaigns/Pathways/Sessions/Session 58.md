## Summary of Last Session
---
![[Pasted image 20231008113004.png|right|500]]
 [[The Works In Progress]] continued toward [[Skuggahvelfing]] and entered the dead city. Upon walking toward the tower, two incredibly large creatures with gaping maws emerged out of the ground. You fled to the top of a building as its jaws snapped at you. After some close calls, you were able to dispatch it, and you made your way to the [[The Towers of Myrkyr|Tower of Myrkyr]]. You learned that, contrary to [[Heartwood|Heartwood's]] understanding, the tower is, in fact, three towers. Two are somewhat intact, though the third was missing the bottom half, and it was impossibly floating in the air. You entered the tower and encountered four humanoid creatures and a statue guarding the entrance. You dispatched them, and a door to a stairwell leading up opened. Again, contrary to Heartwood's initial report based upon rumors he had heard, it becomes apparent to you that the [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]] lies not under the Towers, but at the top of them.  You entered the tower to face several humanoids and a hostile statue attacking you, apparently guardians of the tower. It is likely that there are more inside.
