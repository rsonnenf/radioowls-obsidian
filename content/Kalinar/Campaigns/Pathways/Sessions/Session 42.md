You continued your battle against the Blighted foes, during which time [[Zos]] and [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] fall unconscious. Despite Barnaby nearly being killed, you all worked together to get everyone back to health and finish the fight, though not before Barnaby hears a voice in his head, originating from one of the dragons, asking for help. All of the creatures are dispatched, regardless, and Barnaby brings the dragon that spoke to him back to life to try and help it somehow. As it lay unconscious, [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] discerned that these dragons were once true, young dragons who appeared to be captured by this lesser Blight god and corrupted by his dark energies. Barnaby asked [[Bo Fortuna|Bo]] for help to siphon out the dark magic from the dragon, and Bo, reluctant, agreed. With significant effort, and much difficulty, Barnaby was able to draw out the dark magic from the corrupted dragon, who remains unconscious on the ground, and into a vial. Bo, seeing great power in consuming this pure Blighted essence, drank the contents of the vial.
