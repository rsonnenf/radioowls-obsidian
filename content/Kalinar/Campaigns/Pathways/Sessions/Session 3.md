After seeing a woman fire a crossbow bolt at the mayor, several heavily armed and armored individuals forced their way into the forest clearing.  They yelled that the Mayor deserved to pay for some sort of transgression and began to attack the gathering.  You all sprung into action and fought off the attackers, taking the leader of this group hostage and unconscious.  The mayor, though shot with a bolt, remains alive but unwell.  You took him back to the manor for healing, and the First Lady, [[Alina Fox]], explained that the attackers were members of the [[Dire Clan]], a wannabe influential criminal organization wanting to gain influence in the region so as to join the larger criminal syndicates growing in the region, taking advantage of the political instability throughout Kalinar.  She asked you to somehow deal with the Dire Clan, which have been attacking Varnithel for several months, and you agreed.  [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] and Zos went to a strange alchemist's shop, who after trying to convince you to buy odd potions, eventually relented and sold you a "boring" potion of healing.  You began to head back to the manor.  [[Bo Fortuna|Bo]] disguised himself and began to head to the shed in the back of the manor where the rest of the group is, and where they dragged the unconscious leader of this group of Dire Clan members.


Bal and his group were dispatched to antagonize Varnithel.  [[Davyd Burnside]] has been pushing back against Dire Clan efforts to grow influence in the area.  He created a militia group to combat Clan.  But paying off militia members/militia members' inexperience, etc. has led it to be ineffective in totally combatting them.  The Dire Clan has taken the opportunity to strike back at the Mayor and the Varnithel to send message about getting in the Clan's way.
