## Summary of Last Session
---
![[Pasted image 20231214212335.png|right|500]][[The Works In Progress]] decided that traveling to Theel to devise a plan for dealing with [[The Blight|the Blight]] once and for all was in order. Zos transported you to Theel, and after some searching, you found Craigug, [[Feril]], and [[Zif]] at [[The Rusty Compass|the Rusty Compass]], a tavern near the Artificer's Guild. After catching them up on recent events, and convincing Craigug that going through with this plan to put yourselves in danger to save Kalinar was the right thing to do, you set a plan in motion. Zif would gather as many druids as he could in order to help in the growth of a massive tree that would allow Zos to transport the Theel army to the portal outside of Coran in time to meet the [[Den Fatum]] army. [[Feril]] and Craigug would work with the Artificer's Guild to get the airship in working order and to outfit it with weapons. The airship will serve as a command center where the three of them and several [[Justicars]] directed the battle. After setting them off to start their tasks, you left for the capitol and were escorted to the throne room in the hopes of meeting with [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid]] to inform him of the plan. You met him there along with several other Justicars: Talia, Nailo, and Troy. You explained the plan to them: The two armies would surround the portal while Justicar generals directed the battle from the air. As the armies attack the Blighted creatures pouring from the portals, you all would make your way into the portal in the hopes of eliminating the Blight, somehow, once and for all. You asked for any sort of equipment to help along the way, and Eldrid told you to go to the armory to obtain equipment typically reserved for Justicars.
