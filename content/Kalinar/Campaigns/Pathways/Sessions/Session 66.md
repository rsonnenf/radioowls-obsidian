## Summary of Last Session
---
![[rpg-art-of-a-green-dragonborn-player-character-falling-off-of-a-cliff-while-the-ciff-is-collapsing--167712306.png|right|500]]Upon exiting [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid's]] teleportation portal at the top of the cliff face, you saw two Blighted portals and creatures pouring out of them a few steps above you. You planned to have Thava throw the gunpowder barrels down the cliff to destroy it after you all made it through the portal, and you all fought past the creatures and barreled your way through the portals. Except for [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]], that is, who was grappled and restrained by two creatures. After everyone else made it through the portal, Fax tried fighting his way out of the creatures' grasps, but was unsuccessful. He ordered Thava to loose the barrels, and they exploded, causing the cliff to collapse. While falling, the creatures attacked [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]], causing him to lose conscious. In a dramatic, last-ditch effort, Thava caught Fax and shielded him from the falling debris as best she could. But the debris continued to fall. Try as she might, the weight of the debris is becoming too much to bear.
