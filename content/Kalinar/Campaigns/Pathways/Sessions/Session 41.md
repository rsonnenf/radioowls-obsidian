The battle with the lesser flame god begins! Two portals began belching out minions from the Underrealm. Immediately out of the cave, the god lands a few very powerful blows against some of you. The winged creatures sent powerful psychic attacks your way, and during some of the attacks, you heard whispers from the creatures asking you to help them. They continued attacking, though. Eventually, you brought down the lesser god, two portals dissipated. Now, you just have to clean up the remaining mess.



If the players free dragon from their current state, (maybe an arcana, charisma check?) They will see ethereal, ghostly apparition of a healthy, beautiful dragon emerge out of the slithering, menacing husk. The dragon's spirit will thank them as the husk is killed, and the dragon's soul can proceed into the afterlife peacefully.
