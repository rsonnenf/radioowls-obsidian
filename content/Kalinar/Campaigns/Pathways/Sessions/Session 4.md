You interrogated [[Bal]], the leader of the [[Dire Clan]] troupe that attacked the show.  He didn't reveal too much more than you already knew, other than that the group is trying to make a name for itself in Kalinar.  He was delirious, believing he was back at the Dire Clan hideout, speaking to its leader, Egard Elderberry.  You obtained the general location of the hideout from him before convincing him to stay in Varnithel. You sent him off to Ilga to become his new apprentice and provided a potion of healing to Mayor [[Davyd Burnside]] before heading out into the Misty Forest to find the Dire Clan's hideout.  Before you were able to do so, you came across a troll-looking creature animating several large mushrooms.  He introduced himself as Fern, but he gave off a very odd vibe.  You attempted to leave when he offered to take you to the place you were looking for, and he attacked with his mushrooms.  You quickly dispatched them, but not before enduring some noxious spores, and a few hits from the group.  When you killed Fern, the mushrooms fled, and you found a note, among other things in a bloody pouch Fern was carrying.  Once you were ready, you continued heading out.

You continue your soggy journey, which takes you several more hours until you see off in the distance a run-down looking wooden shack.  Moss grows on the sides of the building.  

Inside, the building looks ordinary.  You initially walk into some sort of living and dining area.  There is a dining room table.  A few of the dishes on it look like they have been recently used, but otherwise, there are few indications that this is a "lived in" home.

As you head down the steps, you notice that some source of light, possibly torches, are lighting the basement.  You hear shuffling and muffled voices, which you can't make out.
