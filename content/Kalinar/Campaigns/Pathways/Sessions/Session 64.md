## Summary of Last Session
---
[[The Works In Progress]] met with the armorer of the [[Justicars]], who picked out several items he thought would assist the party members in their quest. The armorer agreed, per your request, to try and make 1,000 spears in order to create traps. Thereafter, the group went to [[The Rusty Compass]], where they met once more with [[Zif]], [[Feril]], and Craigug. After a night of drinking, you went to bed. The next morning, you decided to travel to the site of the portal to get a tactical view of the impending battle. Emerge from a tree into a green grassland. In the distance is a valley with a large cliff face. You see a large army of grotesque creatures on the grassland, while deep purple lights shone on the cliffside. Many Blighted creatures emerged from the portal. The portal appeared to be 1,200 feet high, and the army that was already amassed was several thousand strong already. You took this information, returned to [[Theel]], and reported to [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]], who stayed behind while creating a holy water firearm.
