## Summary of Last Session
---
The battle against the three devils begins! Fierce attacks were hurled back and forth against both sides. [[The Works In Progress]] learned that the creatures were resistant to a large significant amount of damage, but after learning what was effective, the group tactically adapted to focus their strongest attacks against them. [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] was briefly controlled and caused to attack Grondar. But [[Zos]]'s lightning and [[Bo Fortunate|Bo]]'s [[RPG Rules/5e SRD/spells/Black Tentacles|black tentacles]] did damage to all three over the course of the fight. [[Grubby]]'s and Fax's arrows along with [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]]'s elemental abilities caused massive amounts of damage. Shayna was very near death, but after significant weakening of the last devil by her companions, she marched forward and slayed the last devil, thereby killing the three creatures that killed the three dwarves who promised to tell the groups how to reach the portal to the Underrealm.
