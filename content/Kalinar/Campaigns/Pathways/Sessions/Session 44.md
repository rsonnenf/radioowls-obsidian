## Summary of Last Session
---
 [[The Works In Progress]] began their flight to [[Den Fatum]]. After several hours, you saw in the distance a bright orange light that turned out to be a town in flames, being attacked by a Blighted creature. Despite your attempts to land, the eagles that were carrying you refused to do so, noting that they were to bring you to Den Fatum, and not put in you danger. [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] and [[Grubby]] decided to jump, believing that saving anyone they could from [[the Blight]] is part of their mandate of eliminating it. The rest of the group reluctantly jumped. As each floated to the ground in their own ways, the Blighted creature, a giant humanoid figure wreathed in flame and glowing red was destroying buildings and harming anyone in sight. You attacked the giant as people fled the city. Eventually, you brought down the giant and, exhausted, hoped to stay the night. The eagles, however, urged you to push on, explaining that this was just one example of the growing influence and danger of the Blight. You agreed to continue through the night, though you were exhausted as you reached the border of [[the Forgotten Sands]] the next morning. Off in the distance, you saw a sandstorm approaching, and despite your attempts to evade it, everyone but [[Zos]] and Fax were engulfed by it. As the rest of the party began a quick descent--their eagles unable to maintain altitude, [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] was [[Shayna's Third Vision|seemingly transported]] to her parents' house as a child. She woke to her parents cooking breakfast until her parents heard creatures approaching town. They told Shayna to go to her room and not leave until told to do so. As Shayna peeked from her room, she saw her parents draw swords, her mother's ornamented with the items she now wears as a pendant around her neck. After Shayna's parents went outside, a ghostly, smoky purple figure with red eyes burst through the door. At the same time, Shayna heard a voice in her head tell her. "Your line has betrayed me, child."
