## Summary of Last Session
---
[[The Works In Progress]] finished divvying up the loot in Myrkyr. After doing so, they noticed [[Grubby]] incredibly distraught. He explained that he dreamt for so long of becoming a dragon, believing this [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]] would help him achieve that goal only to have it literally dissipate in front of him. [[Zos]], seeing Grubby's state, used [[RPG Rules/5e SRD/spells/Divination|Divination]] to ask Bahamut what the gods could do to help. Bahamut seemed regretful but gave little in terms of tangible help. He explained that the gods relied upon your group to save them, and by extension, to save Kalinar. He gave you a talisman allowing an individual to cast Mass Cure Wounds, a spell not without reach for some of you, and gave you a potion that allow whoever drinks it to turn into a dragon for one hour, so long as a life of someone close to that person was paid. Bahamut pleaded with Zos and the rest of the group to help him, [[Mayari]], and Kalinar, if you could, but gave no more assistance. When Zos relayed her conversation and the "gifts" Bahamut gave to you, the group was confused and disappointed. Realizing that Bahamut had given little to you and that Mayari seems to have been stringing you along for a long time, you all were furious with these so-called divine entities who needed the assistance of mortals and could do no better than give next-to-worthless items as help. Grubby threw the potion against the wall, realizing that becoming a dragon might not be his priority any longer. Having taken this blow, wondering whether your efforts thus far have been worth it, or if you have been pawns in a divine game, you leave Myrkyr and [[Skuggahvelfing]], and find a tree, where Zos teleported you back to the Misty Forest.
