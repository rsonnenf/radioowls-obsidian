## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Pasted image 20231019211228.png|right|500]][[The Works In Progress]] continue their way up [[The Towers of Myrkyr|the Towers of Myrkyr]], making it to a room containing two large brutish individuals. After a long battle, they dispatched the two, but not before taking significant wounds. You see that the door on one end of the room leads to a rope bridge that attaches to the other tower, which is missing its bottom half and floating via magical means. You try to make your way across but are attacked by several [[Fey]] creatures with humanoid bodies. You send in Thava, who becomes trapped between and inside the mirrors until you break them. She is released and, without discerning the significance of these mirrors, continue on. On the next floor, you encounter a fey individual lamenting his being cast out of favor with the fey queen. He explains that he failed to protect the tower. You mention that you were looking for an orb, which piqued his attention, and when you noted that you wanted it, he attacked in the hope of regaining the favor of the queen.
