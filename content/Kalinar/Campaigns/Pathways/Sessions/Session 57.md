## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Pasted image 20231008112316.png|right|500]]
 [[The Works In Progress]] made their way out of [[Den Fatum]] after securing a treasure trove of potions. They made their way out of the mountain after securing assistance for the coalition Kalinarian army. The group made their way down the mountain and into [[The Forgotten Sands|the Forgotten Sands]]. Eventually, you came across a sphinx and two ogres guarding the way to [[Skuggahvelfing]]. The sphinx informed you that he would only allow you to pass if you solved three riddles, and you solved all three immediately. The sphinx, angered, set his ogres against you, and you quickly dispatched one and the sphinx. You chased the remaining ogre, which attempted to flee, and it informed you that Skuggahvelfing is a dead city inhabited by dark creatures and ruled over by an evil demigod. After speaking with the ogre further, you allow him to go on his way, and you continue your trek to the dead city. Eventually, you make your way there, and in the distance, you see the single fully intact and well-maintained structure jutting into the sky: the tower, Myrkyr.
