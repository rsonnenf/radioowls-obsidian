After waking up in the Artificer's guild, you decided that you would put on a show in an effort to get [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]]'s name out into the city. You hoped that by doing so, you would draw out whoever is trying to hunt him or whoever might be affiliated with him. You convinced [[Feril]] to allow you to use the guild hall as the venue for a show showing off new inventions created by artificers, and Feril was excited to show off his airship that he had created. You took to the streets to advertise. Your street advertisement included jousting, barrel repairs, decapitating Bo, and a horse. Eventually, several guards approached after hearing panicked screams from a crowd that had gathered following one of your stunts. You convinced them that it was part of your street show advertising the event later this evening, and the guards cited you for putting on a street show without a permit. 100 gold pieces to Barnaby, Bo, Grubby, and [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]]. Once you finished advertising, you returned to the guild hall and put together the stage. Eventually, the sun went down, and you began the show, describing Barnaby's and [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]]'s experience of dealing with vampires they encountered together. The show incorporated Cervo and Feril's airship idea with moderate success, and the show was mildly received. You announced rather loudly that you would be celebrating the……successful show at a nearby tavern, then head to bed back at the Artificer's guild.
