On your travels, you find three dwarven brothers, tipsy in the middle of the road. They were initially arguing about a creature they had seen, but could not come to an agreement about what it looked like. Darkus, Larnus, and Orvus Meidfom explain that they are famous brewers, hoping to distribute their beer to towns on the western side of Kalinar and generate additional sales. They, having been enjoying some of their product, were in no state to protect themselves, and they asked you to protect them until they can get to the next crossroads, about 25 miles away, or so. You agreed. Eventually, you stopped for the night. In the evening, [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] sees a [[Shayna's Vision|vision]]:  After having an apparent nightmare of herself as a child, Shayna's dream gives way to a vision involving a cloaked humanoid individual. He tells Shayna that her father told him that she might be able to help, then repeated "You must find us. You must free us. The fate of the world is at stake." As she wakes up, startled a creature, approaches, apparently attempting to attack or take the dwarves. It looks like all three of the dwarves were correct about what it looked like. The creature was a chimera--it had three heads, a lion, goat, and dragon. Upon being engaged, it immediately attacked, killing two of the three dwarven brothers. After an intense battle, though, you were able to kill it.
