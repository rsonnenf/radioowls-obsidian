You spent some time determining whether to take Captain Bird up on his offer, but you didn't come to any determination. Before settling down for the night, Grubby and Fax wrote a contract hoping to make everyone in the party sign that would bind them to each other as friends for the rest of their lives. A few have signed already, but Grubby has decided to be tactical in how he approaches the rest of the group, waiting for the right time to ask. As the group went to sleep, Zos experienced a [[Zos's Vision|vision]] She was enveloped in gray mist that began to part. As it did so, the mist turned to tree bark. From the center of the parting bark emerged a moose or deer with eyes that, while initially, were rolling incessantly and wildly, focused on Zos. The deer opened its mouth, and the chirping of thousands of birds erupted before coalescing into a single voice that said, “You won’t forsake me, will you?” While this was happening, Fax had awoken and helped Bird with repairs on the deck. He was explaining ghost stories he had heard on the high seas, including ghost ships that consume the souls of crewmen on captured ships. As this was happening, Galen saw a [[Lost Sailors|lifeboat]] with five people aboard, and they reeled them in while the rest of the group emerged from below deck. After treating them, you asked a few questions. They claimed that their ship had sunk without notice, and they escaped. [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] discerned that they were not being completely truthful. They were invited below deck to sleep in the captain's quarters, and while most everyone went back to sleep, screams erupted from the rescuee's room. Moving in that direction, you found that one of the individuals was dead, and Grubby, utilizing a scroll of detect evil, determined that one of the rescued sailors was, in fact, undead. It revealed itself to be a vampire and attacked! You were able to dispatch it, but not before it killed the other three sailors. After [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] eliminated the vampire with a mighty swing of her new sword, Barnaby cast revivify on two of the downed sailors, bringing them back to life.
