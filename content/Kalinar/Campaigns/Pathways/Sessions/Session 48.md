## Summary of Last Session
---
A fierce battle erupted in the rubble in [[The Mines|the Mines]] against several worm-like creatures. Although you were able to dispatch them with relative ease, the Mines continued to collapse around you. Just before the chamber crushed you, [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid]] teleported you out of the rubble, where you all came face to face with a squad of dwarves ready to arrest you. You convinced them that you were here on a mission from Mayari after asking her to provide a sign. She provided that sign by causing the rubble behind you to float in the air briefly before crashing back down. The dwarves agreed to help move the rubble, and during that time, Eldrid returned with King [[Fargrim Ironfist]] and [[Grondar Deepdelver]], the Master of Mines. After tense discussions about the disrespect you may or may not have shown to the Mines and to Den Fatum, King Ironfist ultimately heard your pleas to enter the Mines and search for the portal housing Grummsh, apparently being moved by the corroborated news of supernatural attacks happening throughout Kalinar. He allowed you and Eldrid two days to find what you were looking for in the Mines, and he instructed Grondar to assist you.
