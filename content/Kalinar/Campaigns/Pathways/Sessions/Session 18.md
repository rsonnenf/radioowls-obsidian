After the creatures you met in the forest finished repairing the airship, [[Feril]] flew it to Hornsmeet. Upon landing, he told you that he was going to try and figure out a way to repair it and that you should go about your business. You went to the Whimsical Pig, a tavern, where you met a drow bartender. He told you that he knew of the elf, [[Lovel]]. He told you that [[Lovel]] was invited to a state gala held by Mayor Stonehammer. The gala is meant to draw heads of state to the town to boost its influence in the region, and Lovel, as someone who is well-connected and knowledgeable, has an invitation. The barkeep also told you that Lovel might be in trouble--he gave a client some information that was embarrassing to one of the heads of state who would be in attendance at the gala, and there are whispers that he might be apprehended. He could be walking into a trap. After learning this information, you began to figure out how to get into the gala and prepare for it. You obtained formalwear and obtained an invitation that was inadvertently left at the clerk's office in city hall. You all are about ready to attend the gala tomorrow.
