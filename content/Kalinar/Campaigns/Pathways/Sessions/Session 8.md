Following the chimera attack, Darkus was in deep mourning over the loss of his brothers. Thankfully, several members of the group comforted him, and he was able to lay his brothers to rest before you all continued on your way to [[Bilell]]. Eventually, you reached the crossroads, where Darkus determined that he would leave you and continue his efforts to sell his and his brothers' ale, in memory of them. He thanked you and left you behind, and the rest of you made your way to Bilell. Upon arriving, you saw an orc trapped in a cage, and after speaking with him telepathically, you learned that he was a member of the [[Red Thorn]] Crime Syndicate who was captured. He confirmed that the Red Thorn has a presence in Bilell, but didn't provide much more than that. You deceived him by telling him that you would break him out later that evening, then you went to the Cyan Shrine to seek the help of one of the priests to bring Egart Elderberry back to life briefly. With a modest contribution to The Cyan, you were able to bring back Egart's spirit for two minutes, while [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] asked him questions. You learned that Richard L. was a contact he had--the nature of their arrangment was that Richard L. would provide information from neighboring towns that would be of use to the [[Dire Clan]] in their effors to gain influence with the Red Thorn, which is based in Bilell. Barnaby asked about who was after him. Egart explained that someone was after Barnaby for, apparently, potentially attempting to imbue an item with a Will-o-Wisp, which is against Artificier Protocol. It is an odd thing to get upset about, but maybe there's more to it. After learning this, you went to the market, where you were able to find a potion powerful enough to cure Zos and [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] of their ailment from the mushroom spores they have been suffering from. Grubby, in turn, found goggles that help him against the oppressive sun, then you all went to the Artificer's Guild to spend the rest of the evening. You met a dwarven artificer there, [[Feril]], who explained that he is making a magical airship, and provided a little more context for the person hunting Barnaby. Apparently, this person is a fellow artificer who is seeking revenge for something that happened as a result of Barnaby's apparent attempt to imbue some item with a will-o-wisp. After learning this, you all decided to take the rest of the day to rest, and went to your rooms.
