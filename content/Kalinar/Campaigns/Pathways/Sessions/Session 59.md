## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Pasted image 20231008114420.png]]
 
 [[The Works In Progress]] continued up to the second floor where they encountered more fey humanoid/insectoid creatures. After a tense battle that involved [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] briefly falling unconscious, you defeated the fey creatures. You continued up to the next level after a brief moment of gathering your strength, and you encountered a pedestal with a mote of gems atop it. You felt it calling to you, as a magical force beckoned you closer. A few of you approached not of your own volition, and [[Grubby]] and [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] were the first to reach it. As they approached, you observed spikes in the ceiling. Clearly, removing the gems would cause some sort of trap to trigger. As Fax and Grubby thought of taking the gems, they felt the rare feelings of jealousy and resentment toward the other. Grubby attempted to use magic to swipe the gems, but before he could cast the spell, Fax pushed Grubby out of the way and maneuvered the gems of the pedestal with his bow. The party braced for the inevitable falling of spikes, which never came. Instead, taking the part off guard, the gravity in the room shifted, and everyone fell toward the spiked ceiling, which now constituted the floor.
