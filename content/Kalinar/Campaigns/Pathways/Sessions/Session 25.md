Upon speaking with the captive bandit you left alive, you learned that he was just some poor soul who was trying to survive and made some bad choices about how to do that. You pitied him, and convinced [[Feril]] to take him as part of his new crew for the airship. He agreed, and you eventually made your way into [[Bilell]]. After landing, you convinced Craigug to take the group to whoever placed the [[Bounty on Fax]]'s head so that he could clear the air and free himself of any future pursuers. Upon being led into the Bilell Council Chamber, you saw the council, including Councilwoman Eliana Forest, who let show that she was an Emperor Fen sympathizer. She was an individual [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] had left behind while he was tasked with escorting her back to Bilell when he was working under Emperor Fen. Eliana Fax noted that, after Fax left, she and her cohort were attacked by marauders, who killed her husband. She has been searching for Fax to obtain revenge ever since. Fax, in an attempt to test her, mentioned the Blight and saw Eliana give away that she was familiar with the evil gods. She ordered Fax imprisoned and to be interrogated by [[Copi]]. Fax was led to the dungeons, and his friends followed. Copi met Fax and began to interrogate him in an attempt to learn as much as he could about what Fax knew of the Blight and the honorable gods who opposed them. After using magic to hurt Fax and attempt to draw out answers, he caused Zos to revert back from her mouse form, and Copi yelled that intruders were in the dungeons. The split party now deals with the dungeon's myriad enemies.
