You wake after being knocked unconscious from the crashing of the ship and saw [[Feril]] frantically attempting to repair the damage. As you came to, you heard a noise out in the forest and several creatures trying, poorly, to keep hidden. Eventually, they emerged and approached the ship. They were stout creatures with large noses and covered in fur. They had gentle eyes and did not appear to be particularly dangerous. They began to touch the ship, smell it, and place their ears to its hull to obtain a full sensory experience. One of them informed you that they were attempting to ensure that it was safe. They continued and eventually, once they apparently determined that it was safe, began to repair it. One of them told you that there is a town nearby that you could go to to obtain supplies, and you considered going there. Grubby and [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] talked about Fax's new wings and his concerns and insecurities about finding his brother Craigug. You all went to bed, and the next morning, Fax asked one of the creatures, who were continuing to work on the ship, if they had seen anyone who looked like Grubby. The creature responded that they had, with a few other individuals, near the border of the forest. You all decided that, since you had a contact who might be able to point you in the right direction to Bahamut in Hornsmeet, you would head to that city, which was a few days to the south. Feril told you that, once the ship was repaired, he would take you there.
