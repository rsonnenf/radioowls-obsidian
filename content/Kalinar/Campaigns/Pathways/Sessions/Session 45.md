## Summary of Last Session
---
[[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]]'s [[Shayna's Third Vision|vision]] continued. During the vision, she saw a neighbor and family friend, Orthin, kill the creature that had approached Shayna. Her parents arrived as well and decided that it was best if they left Shayna with Othin while they found the source of the attackers. Shayna's mother, Jaylen, gave Shayna the pendant on her sword, which Shayna now wears around her neck. Jaylen told Shayna that there was much she did not know about her family, and they had to hunt whoever was causing the attack in order to keep her safe. They told her to go with Othin to safety, and they would find her as soon as they could. Little did Shayna know, this would be the last time she saw her parents. Shayna awoke, continuing to fall, and after hitting the ground with the rest of her companions, [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] survived by finding shelter near a large rock formation, though not without losing one of the eagles in the process. Eventually, you made your way to the entrance of [[Den Fatum]]. Outside, you asked the Eagles to take the corrupted dragon you had been carrying, who seems to be slowly recovering, to the city of Everfire, which is steeped in dragon culture. You then made your way toward Den Fatum. You encountered two guards who prohibited you, as outsiders, from entering. But by explaining your quest and noting your regret about Kalinar's treatment toward Den Fatum, they relented and allowed you in. You entered and made your way through a massive hall toward the Commons of Den Fatum.
