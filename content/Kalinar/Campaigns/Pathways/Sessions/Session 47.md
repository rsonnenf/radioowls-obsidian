## Summary of Last Session
---
 [[The Works in Progress]] had a good night's rest and in the morning started making their way to [[The Mines]]. As they did so, they made themselves invisible and attempted to sneak in. As they arrived, however, they saw two guards and a gate blocking the way. You had your familiars attempt to cause a distraction, which was mostly successful, but you still had issues opening the gate. Zos turned herself into a mouse and squeezed through the gate but couldn't figure out how to unlock it and ultimately fell victim to a pit trap in the mine's entrance. [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] made short work of the lock with her sword, and you all forced your way in as the guards came running back to see what the commotion was. [[Grubby]] created an illusion of the entrance of the Mines caving in, and you all continued deeper into the mine. Eventually, you made your way into a large chamber, where [[Eldrid Withers]] was waiting. He revealed himself as a [[Justicars|Justicar]] and seemed upset that you apparently made his efforts to draw the dwarves to assist in the battle against [[the Blight]] more difficult. As you spoke with him, the mines began to [[Collapsing Chamber|collapse]] again, which is apparently a common occurrence. You were trapped under a pile of rubble. You attempted to burrow your way out, but while doing so, two creatures, apparently drawn to the chamber by the commotion and collapse, squeezed their way into the rubble and began to attack.
