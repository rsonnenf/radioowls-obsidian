## Summary of Last Session
---
 Once [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] wake the next morning, Barnaby sends off a letter to place in a publication in Theel to inform Bo's next of kin that if they desire, they are free to collect Bo's contractual reward. Thereafter, Bo and Grubby go to the apothecary to obtain silver shavings for use in a Ceremony spell to pay their respects and send Bo off to the afterlife. After several failed attempts to steal the shavings, Grubby buys them, then they head back to the [[Anvil and Ale]]. The ceremony takes place, and the Works In Progress say their final goodbye to Bo. They then make their way to [[The Hall of the Council|the Hall of the Council]] where they convince [[The Council of Seven Hammers]], now Six, to recommend to King [[Fargrim Ironfist]] that [[Den Fatum]] send troops to assist the coalition of Kalinarian armies against [[The Blight|the Blighted]] creatures pouring through a portal to the south of the city of Corun. Having succeeded in that regard, you returned to the apothecary to obtain what potions you could in preparation for what lies ahead. Zos turned into a squirrel to distract the shopkeep, and Grubby started filling a bag with stolen potions. Barnaby convinced the shopkeep that he could control the "squirrel" with a spell, and after "commanding" the squirrel to do a flip, aided by an invisible Grubby, and leave the shop, the shopkeep set his store, now in shambles, ablaze to collect the insurance money. The Works In Progress now stand outside the apothecary with their newly stolen potions.
