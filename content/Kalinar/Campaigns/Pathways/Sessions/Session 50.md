## Summary of Last Session
---
 [[The Works In Progress]] continued along in [[The Mines|the Mines]] until they came across a room containing a small brewery. It was clear that this chamber was created in secret, and there wasn't much to be obtained from here. [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] found a pair of magical gloves that allowed for the creation of any alcoholic beverage in an immensely abbreviated amount of time, and Fax found a note detailing that the two brewmasters were affiliated with a third brewer somewhere in Den Fatum until creative differences caused them to grow apart. The two brewers here started the brewer in the Mines knowing that it would be difficult to find, and you learned that they did so because they had heard of three Dwarven brothers who intended to follow their dreams to brew what would become the most famous beer in all of Kalinar. These dwarves followed in their footsteps, but it is unknown whether they were successful or whether they even still live. You continued through the Mines until you came across a chamber containing a large concentration of volatile and unstable explosives. Several Underrealm creatures attacked and attempted to set off the explosives, but you fended them off. [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid]]  and [[Grondar Deepdelver|Grondar]] argued over whether you all should attempt to disable the explosives, and you ultimately fell on Grondar's side. Grubby was able to disable all of the explosives, though clumsily, and you all continued your way through the Mines.
