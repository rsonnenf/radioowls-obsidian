## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Destroyed Portal-webp.webp|right|500]]Your fight with [[Dregath]] continues, but you are able to hold your own against the devil. [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid]], in a last-ditch attempt to end the conflict, casts a powerful magic missile that kills him. After he dies, a pillar of flame emerges where his body was. Through the flame, you can see a glint of red as a pure, flawless red gem like those you destroyed earlier emerges. This gem, though is different. You can sense the power within it. It tells you that it has the power to save Kalinar, protect the realm against those who would do it harm, give you the opportunity to protect your friends, make those who would harm you pay with their lives, to take vengence, to kill gods, and to Rule Kalinar for yourself. [[Grubby]], enticed by the promises of this gem, takes it. he feels the power within it vibrating, and [[Zos]] uses plane shift to return you to Kalinar. Upon returning to that realm, you see destruction before you, and the armies of [[the Blight]] and Kalinar warring. Behind you the portal to the Underrealm is collapsing, and you must make your way to the far side of the battlefield to avoid danger.
