Grubby and Fax flew toward the Council Chamber to deal with Councilwoman Eliana Forrest. While they did so, the rest of the group attempted to escape [[Bilell]], but were blocked by several lich hounds and guards bearing the signature red glow of the Underrealm. They fought against their enemies while Fax and Grubby encountered the Councilwoman in the chamber. She summoned crimson mist creatures that attacked and placed a spell on Grubby, causing him to attack Fax. Fax was overwhelmed by the enemies and friend attacking him and succumbed to his wounds briefly, but in a fit of determination stood up and fired an arrow that went directly through Councilwoman Forrest's heart, breaking the spell on Grubby. You telepathically communicated with the rest of the group, who, upon dispatching their own enemies, went to a nearby boat to escape Bilell with two crewmen. Grubby and Fax are on the way to meet you.
