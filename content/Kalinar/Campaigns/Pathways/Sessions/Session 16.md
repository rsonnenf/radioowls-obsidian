You took to the skies, as [[Feril]] revealed his new Airship invention. You all are amazed at this new mode of transportation and revel in the opportunity to see Kalinar from this height. Your joy and excitement are quelled pretty quickly, though, as you noticed a humanoid creature flying its way to you. It speaks in your heads, informing you that it is a servant of the Blight who has come to inform you that, no matter what efforts you attempt, Kalinar will fall. As the creature approached, it flew through one of the engines of the airship, setting it ablaze. You fought the creature and attempted to fix the engine as you quickly fell from the sky. You were able to kill the creature after an intense battle, and [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]], seeing that Grubby had fallen off of the ship, jumped off to save him only to learn that he now has wings. He flew back to the ship to the relief of everyone aboard. [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] attempted to fix the engine and was able to do so just before the ship hit the tops of the trees of the Frozen Pinewood.  Given his quick ability to fix the engine and Zos's ability to call upon nature to grow the trees beneath you to cushion your fall, you were able to minimize the injuries you might have otherwise received from falling out of the sky and minimized the damage the airship too as it crash landed.  You blacked out and now awake to a steady, quiet snowfall in the middle of a forest of pine trees.
