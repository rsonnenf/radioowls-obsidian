[[The Works In Progress]] decide to head back to [[Bilell]] to allow [[Feril]] to get back and to tie up some loose ends there. You ask Mayari to conduct some surveillance, whatever she can, to help give some information about the strength of whatever god of the Blight is holding Grummsh. She told you she would do her best, although, like with Bahamut, she can't sense where she is. You hope that she will be able to gain information somehow to guide you on your way, and she tells you that she will meet you when you make it to Den Fatum. You return to Feril and Craigug in Hornsmeet, and take off. There were some mechanical issues, as an imbalance in the magical engine caused the ship to rise higher than you anticipated, causing a scary episode in which many of you suffered from hypoxia, but [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]], again, was able to save the day by putting in place a quick fix that should stabilize the ship and get you to Bilell. You continued along before several people riding Griffons attacked. You were able to dispatch all except one of them, who you took captive.

Captive

Dorn Charlton

-   Description: Dark-skinned half-orc. Shorter at 5'5". He wears a tattered red shirt and stained pants. He wears his hair in dreadlocks. He has a dull look in his green eyes.
-   Personality: He seems absolutely devoid of it. He stares off into space and will respond with overly literal and minimalist answers to questions. He is used to getting what he wants.
-   History: He was born on a small isolated farm. His mother took him hunting and taught him how to think like creatures. He got away from his former life and has fallen to alcoholism and joined with the rest of his group, who are now all dead. They were:

-   Adrik, a male elf;
-   Ilanis, a female half-elf; and
-   Leaf in Woods, a female tabaxi.
