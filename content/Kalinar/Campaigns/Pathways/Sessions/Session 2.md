Several adventurers met in the town of Varnithel looking for work.  You noticed that some sort of festival was going on at that time, and you resolved to find out what exactly was going on.  You went to the tavern, the Sucking Cobbler, where you met Grubby and [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]], two individuals who apparently had a spot in a show that was occurring that evening.  They tried to promote their act to you, and after several attempts to convince them to come, they agreed.  [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] went to the barkeep to see if there was any work around town for you to pursue, and she said that the organizer of the festival was feeling overwhelmed and could probably use help.  You sought him out, and he tasked with you with obtaining lanterns from the mayor, Mayor David Burnside, along with his wife, and the First Lady, Alina Fox, which you did.  A few hours later, you went to the show in the forest clearing where Fax and Grubby were performing. They told the enthralling story of Ashardalon that had terrified Kalinar and gifted one of his scales to individuals who had challenged him, which contributed to a potion that had the power to transform anyone into a dragon.  After their performance, a woman took the stage and engaged in a martial arts display before Zos noticed a small crossbow emerge from her sleeve and fire a bolt at Mayor [[Davyd Burnside]].



You hear a *flick* as a bolt loosens from the small weapon in the direction of Mayor Burnside.  Shocked and terrified yells erupt from the crowd, and at the same time, you hear screams toward the southern area of the theater area.  Looking on, you see five heavily armed individuals walking toward the stage.  "The mayor and his wife!" one yells.  "An example is to be made of them!"  Many in the crowd are shocked into silence.  "Let this be a lesson to anyone who dares to cross the Dire Clan again.  The punishment is elimination."

. . .

Party will learn that Dire Clan is a gang that has been terrorizing Varnithel for several months.  Mayor Burnside, in an effort to stop the attacks, put together a militia (albeit, ultimately unmotivated) that challenged the Dire Clan on one occasion, leading to the arrests of several members and the deaths of others.  Ever since the transition of power in Theel, criminal organizations have been trying to take advantage of the political shakiness to gain influence, hopefully to consolidate several of the organizations and lead a single, unified syndicate.  The Dire Gang is a lowly, inexperienced group.  Nothing compared to the more organized groups that might be found in larger cities.
