You spend a few days at sea after the vampire attack, helping with chores on the ship. Grubby's efforts to get the rest of the crew to sign his contract are relatively fruitless, aside from getting [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]]'s signature. Much of the time is relatively uneventful. However, one night, Bo sees a [[Bo's Vision|vision]] in which the blanket he is using pins him to the deck where he is laying, and his patron appears before him in a form he does not recognize. His patron tells him that Bo is dishonoring him by continuing with his companions against the Blight. Bo's patron has struck a deal with the Blight, and believes they deserve to be worshiped. He tells Bo that he expects him to stop hindering the Blight and threatens to cut him off from his powers if he does not. He tells Bo that he has the power to cause him to go mad and even to end him if he does not abandon his companions. Bo's patron strangles him with his sheets until he is startled awake. That morning, Zos begins to discuss her vision with the rest of the group. [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] remembers that a deer or moose is the sigil of a clan or town in one of rhte forests he visted when he was working for Emperor Fen. Captain Bird approaches the group and informs them that Lambert, one of the rescuees, has been bitten by the vampire and does not look well. He is pale, weak, and no healing has helped. The group bring him to the top deck and tie him up there. While doing so, Bo comes to the top deck, and his eyes are now different colors. One is sliver, and the other is crimson. After casting mirror image and acting odd, he retreats back to the lower deck. As he does so, Fax sees that Lambert has turned into a bat and flown away.


Are there any options for the party to get to Den Fatum other than by walking?
