## Summary of Last Session
---
 After returning from the grassland area and seeing the extent of the Blighted army, [[The Works In Progress]] decided to transport several druids who made it into the city of Theel to the portal so they knew where to transport the armies in the future. After Zos took charge and showed them where the entry point would be, she saw that the army was going exponentially. She returned with the druids, and [[The Works In Progress]] slept for the night before deciding to accelerate their plans to attack to stop the tide of the armies. They obtained their four barrels of gunpowder then met with Feril and Craigug, asking them to take the airship toward Coran now so that they could arrive in time for the battle. They were reluctant to launch the attack before everything was ready but ultimately agreed. You then gathered Eldrid, two Justicars--Harriet Underbough and Roondar Lingenhall--as well as two druids and Zif and teleported, via tree, to just outside of the portal. You emerged far closer to the army than you had hoped, however, seeing that the army had grown even larger overnight, and you immediately tried to fend the Blighted creatures off until Eldrid could teleport you away. As Eldrid was only able to teleport him and the party away, one of the druids created a portal to teleport the remaining individuals back to Theel. We now find [[The Works In Progress]] toward the top of the cliff where the Blighted portal sits.
