## Summary of Last Session
---
 [[The Works In Progress]] returned to the chamber containing the ghostly dwarves, and they informed you that there is a false wall inside the structure built by the Blighted creatures. After doing so, they disappeared, finally at peace, and [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] prayed to [[Mayari]] to determine whether there was enough time to rest. She appeared and told you that, time is running even shorter, but that the next challenge would likely be the most difficult one you've faced yet, so getting a full opportunity to rest was in your best interest. You did so and made your way back into the structure. Along the way, [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid]] and [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] spoke. Eldrid seemed curious about Fax's wide ranging knowledge of various cultures and languages and asked about it. He learned that Fax had worked for "several emperors," which Eldrid seemed to take to mean that Fax had affiliated himself with the evil Emperor [[Fenn]] before he was overthrown. When Eldrid criticized Fax about this, Fax retorted that [[Christopher]] did not seem to be doing much better to help the realm. Eldrid left, displeased with Fax, and you entered the structure. Eventually, you found a false wall on the western side of the structure and broke it down, finding a portal to the Underrealm. After Eric conducted some reconnaissance, you all entered to find [[Gruumsh]] chained to a stone outcropping in the middle of a pool of lava in a massive chamber. A winged devil, whom you discerned is named Titivulus, stood near Gruumsh, noticed your presence, and charmed [[Grondar Deepdelver|Grondar]] into jumping into the lava, where he was killed. The Works In Progress then engaged Titivulus. During the course of the battle, Fax was charmed by Titivulus, and Barnaby was made to fear him. As things continued to look more and more bleak, out of nowhere, two dragons: [[Aurimis]] and his father, Argentius, appeared in the chamber, apparently by taking the portal, and joined the fray!
