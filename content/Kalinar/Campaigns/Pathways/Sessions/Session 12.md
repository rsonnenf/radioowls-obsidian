The fire and salt-based creatures next to the altar-like structure attacked you! During the tense battle, Grubby decided to run toward the gem giving off the red glow at the center of the altar. As he did so, he disappeared to a completely different area. You all continued with the battle, and after a tense fight, you were able to dispatch them. Once you regrouped, you took a few minutes to figure out what happened to Grubby. [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] pursued Grubby toward the gem and also disappeared. Bo resummoned Eric and sent him toward the gem with a note, and he disappeared, appearing near Grubby and Fax. After Eric was resummoned he conveyed to Zos that Grubby and Fax were in a dark, firey, and cavernous area. Eventually, you all decided to follow and walked toward the gem. You were transported to a dark, cave-like area with jagged rocks and glowing, sinuous rock. Corridors jutted off in every direction, but Bo noticed that a pair of footsteps wandered off down one particular cavern. You followed and eventually came across a sword embedded in the rock and holding up a skeleton. I had a crimson glow. [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] had Cervo extract it, and Cervo gave it to Barnaby before you all decided to continue on.
