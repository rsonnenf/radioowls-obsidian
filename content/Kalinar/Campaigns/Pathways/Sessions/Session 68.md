## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Pasted image 20240321182230.png|right|500]]After much trial and error, [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] discern that to destroy the gem holding this part of the portals anchored to Kalinar, you had to attack it with a melee attack. A swift cone of cold that cooled the lava enough allowed for a melee attack against the gem, which caused it to shatter, and a quake occurred. The party ran toward the portal, and you moved through it, returning incredibly briefly to the Overrealm before flying into the other portal to the Underrealm. Upon reentering, you saw a vast lavafall with a gem once again floating in midair above a pool of lava. Several creatures guarded it.  You approached and surprised the creatures, and during the ensuing battle, [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid]] unexpectedly caused the gem to shatter, via a magical spell, and the realm began to shake again, this time more violently. You quickly dispatched the remaining demons guarding the gem, and as you tried to planeshift out of the Underrealm, a voice in your head noted that you have caused much difficulty in his plans to overtake Kalinar. You were violently teleported to some sort of throne room. Upon a throne sits an intimidating, though simple looking creature. He reiterated that you have placed many obstacles upon his path to rule Kalinar.
