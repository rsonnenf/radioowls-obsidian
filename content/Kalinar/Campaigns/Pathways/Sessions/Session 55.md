## Summary of Last Session
---
As the dust of the battle settled, [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] turned toward Mayari and Eldrid, clearly angered at their leading them into this situation which caused Bo's death. After hearing your anger, Mayari pleaded with you to find it in your hearts to help with one more task. She explained that, with Gruumsh now dead, her plans to stop the Blight needed to adapt. A massive portal has opened to the south of the city of Coran, and [[The Blight|blighted]] creatures are pouring out of it. She explains that Emperor [[Christopher]] of Theel is sending a coalition army comprised of allied provinces to the portal to fend off the creatures. The plan is for Mayari, Bahamut, and any allied gods they can convince to fend off the Blighted gods while the armies hold back the creatures pouring out of the portal. She asks if you would convince [[The Council of Seven Hammers|the Council of Seven Hammers]] and [[Fargrim Ironfist|King Ironfist]] to send their forces to aid the coalition army. Eldrid told you he would deliver a report to King Ironfist, and after saying your goodbyes to Aurimis, Mayari opens a portal that brings you back to [[The Mines|the Mines]]. As you make your way to the exit, you hear tapping in the wall. Upon closer investigation, you find a near-imperceptible seam. You discern that this portion of the wall is actually a hidden door. Upon opening it, you find a dwarf inside named [[Stonehead Rockface]]. A quirky figure, you spend some time getting to know him. He explains that he was trapped behind the wall for three days because someone at the temple he studied at became tired of him and intended to leave him for dead. After getting to know him more, you make your way back to your room at the [[Anvil and Ale]]. After Stonehead falls asleep, you discuss what your next steps are, and you decide that, tentatively, you will try to obtain the [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]] so Grubby can provide aid against the Blight as a dragon. Then, you would head to Theel to check on Feril and others waiting for you there. Thereafter, you all went to sleep.
