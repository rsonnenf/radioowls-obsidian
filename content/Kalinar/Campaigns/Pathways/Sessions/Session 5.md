After regaining your composure post-combat with some animated mushrooms, [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] found a squirrel and began talking to him, asking where the [[Dire Clan]] hideout was.  The squirrel answered that the hideout was nearby and that he and his brood were plotting against them.  Apparently, they threw rocks at the squirrels, which was a grave disrespect to the One Acorn, an apparent deity to the squirrels, for whom they serve as some sort of extension.  You followed the squirrel's direction and came across a house.  Inside, you found a hidden chute that led to a basement.  Inside were several guards and Egart Elderberry.  After several starts, stops, and tense moments, you learned several things:  The Dire Clan are attacking Varnithel because they want to gain the approval from the [[Red Thorn]], a criminal syndicate based in Billel; Egart and the Dire Clan were driven out of their original hideout by ghoulish, horrifying creatures they have never seen before; and Egart knows something about [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]]'s [[Vomilla Baffletop|mother]], who is apparently in some sort of danger.  Negotiations, however, broke down, and a fight broke loose between the Dire Clan and our group.
