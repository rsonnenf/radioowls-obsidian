You dispatched the vine creatures after a fierce battle. After doing so, [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]], wanting to discern how these creatures came into being, asked the grasses what news there is in the forest. The grasses, answered proudly that the forest is being allowed to grow and flourish faster and stronger than it ever has before. Fax discerns that something is wrong with the grasses--it is as if the forest is being influenced and tricked into being the twisted, overly powerful iteration that it is now. The grass proudly points you in the direction of the source of its power--to the southeast. Fax takes this information and turns to Thava, asking her to fly to [[Varnithel]] to ensure that the town is safe. [[Bo Fortuna|Bo]] asks the same of Eric, and the two of them fly off for the evening. During the remainder of the evening, [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] looks through the book written by his [[Vomilla Baffletop|mother]]. He learns that she was learning how to imbue constructs with magical essence so as to emulate the very magic the constructs were imbued with. The last chapter provided some hint of what she hoped to study thereafter: if magic could be imbued in constructs, what of essences? Could one's memories be permanently implanted? Could souls? Pasages seem to indicate that she hoped to study whether creatures could have eternal life by having their souls imbued in constructs. During [[Zos]]'s portion of the watch, she also learned of the twisted nature of the forest, which angered her. Finally, during Fax's portion of the watch, he obtained several phesants while hunting, which he cooked before the rest of the party woke. After everyone did, they ate and saw two figures flying toward the camp: Eric and Thava. Once they landed, Fax asked if Varnithel was safe. Somberly, Thava shook her head, "No."

On approach to Varnithel:
*A few days more of travel along the edge of the forest leads you to a familiar road. You travel along it for several hours, and as you cross over a hill, expecting to see the town where your adventure began, you are surprised to see a plume of smoke rising into the sky. In the distance, you hear faint screaming.*

In Varnithel:
*The town is in chaos. Flames lick at several buildings, and townspeople are panicking, running for their lives. In the distance, toward the town center, just in front of the Mayor's Manor, you see several humanoids standing in front of it. They are concentrating, reciting some sort of chant. Just beyond them, your eyes focus on the manor. You see vines crawling up it, squeezing the house. The humanoids appear to be attempting to crush the manor. The prize inside, no doubt the Mayor and First Lady, are too valuable to set the manor ablaze. You see that pieces of the building are beginning to crumble. If you don't do something quick, the whole building will collapse with the Mayor and First Lady inside!*
