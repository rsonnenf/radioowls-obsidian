Your searches in the library were fairly fruitful. [[Grubby]] found books showing him that Potions of Dragon Majesty probably exist, but for some reason, they do not match up with his understanding that they are permanent. The stories couldn't have been wrong, could they? He learned that there has been some discussion among alchemists to indiciate that some believe the potion can be made permanent. Perhaps it has something to do with the dragon orbs he has heard about? Maybe mixing it with another component? Zos learned of [[the T'Durn]], a group of shadow druids who worship nature and its violent tendencies. They have been attacking small villages near the Misty Forest in an attempt to help nature take back what was taken from it. [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] found a book written by his Mother, Vomilla, indicating that she was styding infusing magic essences with constructs. Barnaby also found a touching acknowledgement in that book. He ended up checking it out and taking it with him. As you made your way out of the library, you came across a small child being escorted by a guard for stealing fruit. He convinced the guard that you would pay is fine such that he would be set free, and to his surprise, you did. He told you that he is an orphan just trying to get along and find food, and he asked you to show him how to steal more effectively. After some convincing, you agreed. He led you to a back alley where two mercenaries--likely the last two hunting [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]]--were waiting. Craigug was also there, shackled. The mercenaries paid off the boy, and they tried to take Fax in. A battle ensued during which Craigug was killed. But so were the mercenaries and their goons. Barnaby ran forward at the last moment, and with a well-placed revivify spell, he brought Craigug back to life.



Craigug will explain that Feril fled with the Artificer's Guild. Craigug was with them until they were ambushed by the bounty hunters that were just killed. Feril did not have a plan when they fled, so Craigug does not know where they went. Craigug wants to stay in Borbir where it is relatively safe.

What is one piece of information that Craigug can give to the group? Maybe that he heard of a bounty hunter's guild that is trying to rival the Red Thorn? So they encouraged these mercenaries to take on the job to gain influence?
