## Summary of Last Session
---
After drinking the Blighted essence, [[RPG Rules/5e SRD/spells/Darkness]] engulfed Bo. Once it dropped, two Bos stood before you. One wore lighter clothing with a smoother complexion. The other was darker and looked corrupted. After trying to determine what happened to [[Bo Fortuna|Bo]] for some time, the darker Bo attempted to flee. The lighter Bo told you to eliminate him because he could sense malice from him. As you attempted to subdue the darker Bo, he cast [[RPG Rules/5e SRD/spells/Wall of Fire]], attempting to harm you all and escape. Before he could do more harm, you killed the Dark Bo, and his body turned to vile, black ichor. After resting for some time and investigating the cave to make sure there were no more portals, you tied up the formerly corrupted dragon to make it seem to limit any negative repercussions from [[Heartwood]] and started heading back. Once you arrived, you convinced Heartwood to allow you to keep the dragon and take it to safety. As payment for ridding the forest of the Blight, Heartwood told you that the last known [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]] is located in catacombs beneath a tower, known as Myrkur, in a dead city known as Skuggahvelfing in [[the Forgotten Sands]]. Heartwood also enlisted six massive eagles to take you to your next destination. You then climbed atop them and began your journey to [[Den Fatum]] in the forgotten sands.
