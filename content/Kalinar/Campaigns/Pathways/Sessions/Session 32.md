Despite the mime (Colben) and his accomplice's (Holabelle) best efforts to get away with stealing your gold, you were able to thwart them. You convince them to show you around the city, and after they show you the markets you part ways with them but not before [[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] is able to grab hold of Holabelle’s invisibility cloak. You visited the blacksmith in the markets, where [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] obtained a +1 shield  by trading many of his alchemical ingredients and agreeing to be available for a favor to be redeemed by the shopkeep. [[Grubby]], in turn, stole a crossbow on the way out. You then made your way to the tavern where you had some drinks and dinner. Grubby identified the crossbow as a magical crossbow, a Corpse Slayer Hand Crossbow. Barnaby, in turn, attempted to identify Shayna’s necklace. Crimson magical energy rocketed out of it and shocked Barnaby, making him temporarily blind. Shayna, on the other hand, was drawn into another [[Shayna's Second Vision|vision]]. She was immersed in dark fog that slowly transformed crimson red. A disembodied voice repeated to her “If the blind one is made to see, there will be ruin.”  Eventually she returned to consciousness in the bar, confused as to who the blind one is. After telling the rest of the group of her vision, the group went to sleep. The next morning, you went to the library to find any information on a number of questions you had. Barnaby and Grubby looked for information on dragon potions, and Barnaby intended to look for information on the blind one. [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] and Shayna looked for history on Kalinar and Bilell. Zos looked for information on the creature she saw in her vision to see if any sigils reflected it like Fax had mentioned. After some time, Zos found the sigil in the book she was reading and excitedly began making her way to anyone who could read it to her.

The book that [[Vomilla Baffletop|Barnaby's mother]] writes is called "[[On the Infusion of Magics and Constructs]]."
