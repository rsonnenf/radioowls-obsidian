Despite [[Copi]]'s best efforts to get information out of [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] about what he knows of the Blight, you were able to break free, and a massive prison break battle ensued. Copi's powerful magic was able to briefly take down Grubby, but your teamwork was able to bring Copi down, get Grubby back on his feet, save several prisoners, and eliminate the guards who were, unfortunately, corrupted by Councilwoman Eliana Forrest's influence. You made your way out of the prison to find that the city of [[Bilell]] was on high alert. You went to the Artificer's Guild to find it in flames, but fortunately, it looks as if the airship has fled. Fax and Grubby, both of whom were surveying the city from the air, decided to make their way back to the Council Chamber to confront Councilwoman Forrest. The rest of the group began to ran and are now being chased by guards and Underrealm hounds summoned by guards who appear to have been corrupted by Underrealm influence.
