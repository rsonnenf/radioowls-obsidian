## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[fantasy-concept-art-of-an-orb-of-dragonkind-with-a-black-dragon-bursting-out-of-it-579350417.png|right|500]][[The Works In Progress]] eliminated the fey creature protecting the fey queen's horde. After traversing to the top of the tower, you found the last [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]] in existence sitting on a pedestal  in the center of the room surrounded by a slew of magical artifacts. Grubby was immediately drawn to the Orb and picked it up. As he looked into it, he could feel a consciousness seeping into his mind. The prismatic dragon, Vorakathrax, charms [[Grubby]], showing him visions of power and grandeur, convincing him to release him from the orb. Upon his release, he attacks [[The Works In Progress]]. The party, however, bests the dragon, killing it. He leaves behind a crystal object, and the party loots the horde at the top of the tower.
