## Summary of Last Session
---
[[The Works In Progress]] continued running from the failing Blighted portal, fighting horrible Blighted creatures along the way. While fleeing, Barnaby was knocked unconscious, but Zos was able to save him after help from Stone in bringing him back to consciousness. To ensure that the group was able to escape, Eldrid sacrificed himself, holding off a hoard of enemies while the rest made it to safety. As they did so, the Blighted portal was obliterated, destroying the rest of the mountain. All of the Blighted creatures in Kalinar immediately turned to dust. As the battle ended, [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] assessed their circumstances. They took the time to grieve the loss of their friend Fax, as Grubby placed a small crossbow in a makeshift grave dug by the party. Zos used her druid powers to create a beautiful setting atop the grave of vibrant flowers and other plants. ![[Pasted image 20240514215112.png|center|500]]Eventually, Feril and Craigug landed the airship, which was flying above the battlefield. The group reunited with them, with incredibly big hugs as the group processed their incredible achievement and devastating loss. As this happened, Justicars gave their reports of the battle to generals on the ground. Despite Zos's questioning, no one knew Zif's fate. He opted to assist the druids on the ground rather than take to the skies. Eventually, the group was given the all-clear to take the airship back to Theel. As the ship journeyed to Theel, they spoke about their next steps. Grubby, clearly upset, reiterated his wish to find and eliminate Mayari for the pain she caused by placing the group on this quest. Barnaby and Stone suggested perhaps trapping Mayari in some sort of cage or convincing the gods to leave Kalinar alone for the rest of time. Grubby seemed amenable to that option, and though the group's decision seems close, there is still much to learn.
