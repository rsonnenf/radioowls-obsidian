## Overview
---
A group of Dragonborn worshipers of Bahamut are praying in the street. They try to convince the party of Bahamut's benevolence and omnipotence.

## Encounters
---


**Setting:** The party strolls through the crowded streets of a city when they encounter a group of fervent followers of Bahamut. Clad in white and gold robes adorned with dragon motifs, they approach the party with radiant smiles, eager to share the teachings of the Platinum Dragon.

### Scene 1: Benevolence in the Streets

The followers, led by a charismatic and kind-hearted cleric named Seraphina, approach the party with an air of gentle persuasion. They express their deep faith in Bahamut's benevolence and omnipotence, aiming to counter the party's potential displeasure with the Platinum Dragon and Mayari.

_Skill Check: Insight to gauge the sincerity of the followers, Charisma to engage in diplomatic conversation._

### Scene 2: The Convincing Sermon

Seraphina passionately delivers a sermon, highlighting the benevolent acts attributed to Bahamut throughout history. She speaks of divine interventions, miraculous healings, and the protection bestowed upon the faithful. The followers present stories of lives transformed by the Platinum Dragon's grace.

_Skill Check: Religion to counter with alternative beliefs, Wisdom to discern the emotional impact of the stories._

### Scene 3: Offer of Healing

In an attempt to showcase Bahamut's purported omnipotence, the followers offer to perform a healing ritual. They explain that through Bahamut's divine grace, wounds can be mended, and ailments cured. This is a demonstration of the Platinum Dragon's compassion for all beings.

_Skill Check: Insight to discern the authenticity of the healing, Arcana to understand the magical aspects of the ritual._

### Scene 4: Addressing Doubts

The followers attentively listen to the party's grievances and doubts, acknowledging the complexities of faith. They provide thoughtful responses, attempting to reconcile the party's concerns with the teachings of Bahamut. The conversation becomes an opportunity for both parties to find common ground.

_Skill Check: Persuasion to express doubts diplomatically, Charisma to maintain open communication._

### Scene 5: Parting Words

As the encounter concludes, the followers express gratitude for the conversation, extending an invitation to join one of Bahamut's upcoming ceremonies. They believe that through communal worship and understanding, even those with reservations can find solace and purpose in the Platinum Faith.

_Skill Check: Insight to determine the followers' true intentions, Charisma to gracefully decline or accept the invitation._

### Resolution

The encounter leaves the party with a choice: either to further explore Bahamut's teachings or to maintain their existing perspectives. The followers of Bahamut part ways amicably, leaving a lingering sense of goodwill in the air, regardless of the party's decision.
