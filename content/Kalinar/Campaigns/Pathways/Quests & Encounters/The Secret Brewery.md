## Overview
---
The secret brewery in the mines was run by a group of renegade dwarves who were once employed by the Anvil and Ale, but left due to disagreements with Balor over the quality of the ale being produced. The renegade dwarves set up their own operation in a hidden section of the mines, where they have been perfecting their craft.

They heard of Darkus, Larnus, and Orvus Meidfom, who were talking about traveling Kalinar after creating an incredibly good beer. They decided to do the same.

Inside the secret brewery, the party will find a large chamber filled with copper kettles, barrels, and sacks of grain. The air is thick with the smell of hops and yeast. The renegade dwarves will be hard at work, brewing various types of ale and experimenting with new recipes.
