## Overview
---
As the party moves through the Mines, they suddenly see the translucent figures of several dwarves appear before them. These are the ghosts of the dwarves whose bodies lie nearby. The ghosts are unable to move on until their deaths are avenged, and they plead with the party to help them seek justice.

The ghosts explain that they were ambushed by a group of dark creatures from the Underrealm. These creatures came through the portal and attacked the dwarves, killing them and taking their valuables. The ghosts beg the party to find and defeat the creatures responsible for their deaths, so that they can finally rest in peace.

The party can agree to help the ghosts, and they are given a spectral map that shows the location of the Underrealm portal. However, the ghosts warn the party that the creatures they are seeking are dangerous and powerful. They also tell the party that the creatures have been using the dwarves' stolen goods to bribe and corrupt other miners in the area.

As the party makes their way through the Mines, they are occasionally accosted by spectral apparitions of the creatures that killed the dwarves. These apparitions are not solid, and cannot be damaged by normal attacks. However, they can be repelled with radiant damage or by using spells that affect undead creatures.

The party may also encounter living miners who have been corrupted by the stolen goods. These miners are fiercely loyal to the creatures who have been bribing them, and will attack the party on sight. The party must choose whether to fight or try to reason with the miners.

Ultimately, the party will need to find the creatures responsible for the dwarves' deaths and defeat them. When this is done, the ghosts of the dwarves will appear before the party one last time, thanking them for their help and finally finding peace.
