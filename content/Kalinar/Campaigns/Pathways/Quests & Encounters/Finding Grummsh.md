## Overview
---
As [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] make their way through the mines looking for the portal to [[Gruumsh]], they encounter several setbacks.

Eventually, they find the portal to the Underrealm. Inside, Gruumsh is near his breaking point.

## Encounters
---
#### Approaching the Portal 

>*As you enter this chamber, which you can tell has been closed off for a very long time, you start to hear a low rumble that gradually grows louder. Inside the chamber, you see a swirling red portal that crackles with intense heat, and you realize that this must be the gateway to the Underrealm. You can feel the heat emanating from the portal, and the sound of churning lava becomes deafening as you inch closer to it.*

#### After entering the Portal 

>*As you step through the red portal, you are immediately hit with a wave of intense heat and the smell of sulfur. Looking around, you find yourself in a vast chamber surrounded by flowing lava. The only things that break up the fiery expanse are the outcropping you stand upon and a large stone platform in the center of the chamber. You can just barely make out the form of a hulking figure bound to the stone with chains. As you approach the platform, the figure begins to thrash and bellow in a rage that seems to shake the very foundations of the chamber. You have to act quickly before he is consumed by his fury and lost forever.*
