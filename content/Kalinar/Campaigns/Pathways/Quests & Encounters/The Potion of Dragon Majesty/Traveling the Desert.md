## Overview
---
[[The Works In Progress]] leave [[Den Fatum]] and make their way toward [[Skuggahvelfing]] in the hopes of finding the last remaining [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]]. 

## Encounters
---
### Beginning the Journey

>*Leaving the ancient halls of Den Fatum behind, the Works in Progress sets forth on a new chapter of their quest. Their journey takes you back out the mountain into the frigid cold and snow, down until you reach the bottom of the mountain. Now, far ahead of you, you see the expanse that is [[the Forgotten Sands]], a vast desert that stretches beneath the unyielding sun. The horizon shimmers with mirages, an illusionary dance that seems to mock their every step.
>The air is heavy with the scent of sun-baked earth and the promise of adventure. Sand dunes rise and fall like the swells of a restless sea, their golden crests kissed by the sun's fiery embrace. The sky above stretches in an endless azure expanse, a backdrop to the Works in Progress' determined march.
>As the sun arcs across the heavens, the desert's duality becomes evident. In the day, the sands sear under the sun's blistering touch, the air wavers with heat. The party's footfalls leave imprints upon the shifting surface, a fleeting testament to their passage through this unforgiving landscape.
>But as night descends, the desert's face changes. The temperature plummets, and the dunes cast long shadows under the moon's pale glow. The Works in Progress wraps themselves in cloaks and huddles around the warmth of their campfire, the desert's nocturnal symphony alive with the sounds of unseen creatures.

### Encounters Along the Way

* **Quicksand Quandary**: While crossing a seemingly stable stretch of sand, the party stumbles upon a treacherous patch of quicksand. They must quickly react to avoid being pulled under, requiring teamwork and creative solutions to escape its grasp.
* **Oasis Enigma**: A seemingly inviting oasis appears on the horizon, surrounded by lush vegetation. However, it harbors a magical enchantment that distorts reality. The oasis is actually a mirage, and as the party approaches, it seems to shift further away, leading them deeper into the desert's heart.
* [[Desert Sphinx]]: The party must solve three riddles in order to continue along their journey.



>They encounter ancient rock formations, weathered by the sands of time, and traverse dry riverbeds that hold secrets of bygone waters. Each landmark brings them closer to Skuggahvelfing and the tower of Myrkur that awaits.
>With determination etched upon their faces, they push forward, driven by the weight of the realm's fate and the quest to retrieve the sole remaining Orb of Dragonkind. This artifact, buried beneath the dead city, represents their beacon of hope—a light to guide them through the darkness that enshrouds the world.
>The desert is a realm of extremes, where survival and discovery are intertwined in a dance of challenge and reward. The journey is arduous, but the Works in Progress stand united, their shared purpose forging bonds that can withstand even the harshest trials. Ahead lies the desolation of Skuggahvelfing, a city caught in the clutches of shadow, and beyond that, the labyrinthine catacombs and the vault that holds the key to their destiny.*
