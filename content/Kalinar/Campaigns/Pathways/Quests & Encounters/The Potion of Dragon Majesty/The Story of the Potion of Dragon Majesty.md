Majesty. They say one night while Cyan was sleeping Adrex snuck into Cyan’s layer and stole a single scale off of the tip of his tail. Being suspicious of other dragons, Cyan called other great dragons to a meeting to see who had stolen his scale. Adrex used this opportunity to steal a scale from 10 different dragons. He spent years mixing these scales with different chemicals until he profected a potion that could turn anyone who drinks the potion into an adult dragon of the same color as the scale that was stolen.” A potion to help accelerate the growth into a dragon, AND an orb that would help release the dragon within??? These were extremely interesting to Grubb’rhun, and he knew that he had to have one if not both of them.

The Orbs of Dragonkind have been lost. Many wonder whether they even still exist. Unbeknownst to many, the Dwarves of Den Fatum possess one. They took it from a dragon 1,000 years ago as revenge for the dragon's stealing much of the material they had mined in the caverns beneath their city. Since that incident, they have been incredibly reluctant to allow any outsiders near the caverns.
