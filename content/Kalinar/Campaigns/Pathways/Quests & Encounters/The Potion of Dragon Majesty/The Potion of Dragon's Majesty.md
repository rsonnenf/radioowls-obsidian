[[Grubby]] finds books in the [[Borbir]] library noting the possible existence of Potions of Dragon Majesty outside of the stories he was told. But the books state that the potion is rare, and even if it can be brewed, many alchemists have only been able to brew a potion that lasts for about an hour. 

Apparently, the potion requires the use of a dragon scale and also maybe an [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]]. 

Party is told to meet with [[Heartwood]] in the forest. He is an old tree being attuned with nature and might be able to help with obtaining ingredients necessary for a permanent potion. But Heartwood will be hesitant to help them. Red dragons have engulfed his kind in flames before. He is afraid that, by allowing Grubby to become a dragon, he would be endorsing their return and the slaughter of his kind.

They must complete a task for Heartwood to be willing to assist them. A lesser Blight god, the Devilflame Juggler, has taken up residence in the Forest. He has been corrupting the forest and causing more of the Blight to enter the realm through portals he is creating. Heartwood asks the Works in Progress to rid the Forest of the Blight.

He is guarded by two catonoctrix, which were two dragons in the forest that had been exposed to the Juggler's powers and corrupted by the Blight.


If party asks Heartwood to help them concoct a potion of dragon majesty, he becomes angry.  

>*"What business do the likes of you have with such a potion? Do you wish to sully this Forest even more than the Blight already has?"*

Heartwood will explain that, while dragons are relatively rare in Kalinar now, they once roamed throughout Kalinar. Some had little regard for the forest. Great battles between dragons and those who sought to steal from their vast hoards caused significant collateral damage to the Forest. 

>*"All manner of dragon was guilty. Their acid poisoned our mothers and fathers. Their lightning struck down our brothers and sisters. Their flames burned our saplings alive. You aim to facilitate our suffering further?"*

If party is persuasive, Heartwood will tell them that he will consider assisting them if they purify the Misty Forest.

>*"There is a darkness in the forest. Whispers suggest that a minor god has settled in a cave to the northwest. From there, his poison spreads. All manner of dark beings erupt from cracks in the soil and boil into existence from pools of darkness. I hear that two contemptable dragons have allied themselves with this god. They protect him while he calls forth his minions, which pour into this realm."*

Dragon's name is Vorakathrax (Vor-ah-ka-thrax)

**Charisma DC 20**. On a success, you are not charmed. On a fail, you become charmed (cannot attack the charmer or target the charmer with 
harmful abilities or magical effects). 

The orb will cast [[RPG Rules/5e SRD/spells/Suggestion|Suggestion]].  
**Charisma Save DC 20**. On a failure, the dragon urges you to work toward the evil ends it desires. It seeks freedom from the orb and to spread suffering in the world as it had done before.

Upon failure, Grubby will agree and attempt to summon the dragon within.
