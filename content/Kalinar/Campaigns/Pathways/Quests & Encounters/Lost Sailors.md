You begin to hear yelling above deck. "Hurry up, you sad excuse for a pirate, the life float, the life float!" You hear a set of footsteps above you running across the deck, while Galen simultaneously yells, "Oh, Papa, I'm going as fast as I can! Do you think they are all right?" "We'll find out soon enough. Reel them in, lad."

Walking up the steps, the hot, salty wind blows past you. You look off the port side of the boat and see a small lifeboat carrying five souls. Even from this distance, you can see that they are hungry, weak, and dehydrated. They've sweat through their clothes, and their eyes lack any spark, replaced with the realization of the horrors that the sea is capable of.

These individuals were sailors on a ship. They claim that the ship sank and they are not sure why. They scrambled to the nearest life boat and were the only ones to make it away.

This is untrue. In reality, the sailors feared something that was on their ship. They would awake, and every night, another member of their crew was found dead. They believed a dark sea spirit was taking the soul of one sailor every night. They abandoned the ship and sunk it in an effort to prevent the spirit from following them. They were mistaken. In fact, the culprit was a vampire knight, sent by Lord Donovan [[Donovon Ortofola|Ortofola]] to scout western Kalinar in the hopes of Rhuie gaining influence in the region. Fabien, that vampire, got on board with the escapees, intending to use the boat as a means to make his way back to Rhuie.  At night, however, he needs to feed. He seeks a victim and sucks their blood.
