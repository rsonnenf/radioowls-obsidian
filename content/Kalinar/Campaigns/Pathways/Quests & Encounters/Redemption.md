## Overview
---
After [[The Works In Progress]]'s failure to save Grummsh, plans need to adapt in order to stop [[The Blight|the Blight]]. [[Mayari]] has learned of a massive portal opening in a secluded area of Kalinar. Underrealm forces are amassing as they enter this world. Mayari asks the Works in Progress to make one last stand to save Kalinar and to avenge their fallen friend. Without Grummsh, a Kalinarian force must be amassed to counter and eliminate the Blight once and for all. Theel and a few regional powers have been organizing a force, but more will be needed to have a fighting chance. The Dwarves of [[Den Fatum]] and their skill in metalworking will certainly serve a role in turning the tide. Then, once the force is amassed, confronting the Blight at their portal and eliminating them for good will bring peace back to Kalinar.
