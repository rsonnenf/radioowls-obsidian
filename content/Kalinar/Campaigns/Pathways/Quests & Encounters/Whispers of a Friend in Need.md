The party will hear whispers from travelers passing through from near the Misty Forest that a threat is growing in the woods. Folk who seem to adhere to a brutalistic view of nature are ransacking nearby villages and taking captive anyone who comes through the forest. They apparently use many of the captives to work for them as they continue their efforts to conquer.

If party does not seem inclined to take this path, maybe have Zos experience another vision.


If the party elects to pursue this quest, they will find that [[Zif]] is one of the captives. He was sending Zos visions , attempting to get her to reach him.
