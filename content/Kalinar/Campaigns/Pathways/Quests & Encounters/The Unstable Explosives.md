## Overview
---
As the party enters the room, they notice that the floor is covered with a layer of dust and debris. At the center of the room, they see a pile of unstable explosives that are so sensitive that the slightest movement could cause them to detonate.

As the party cautiously enters the room with the unstable explosives, they suddenly hear the sound of claws scraping against stone. A group of Underrealm devils, attracted by the party's presence, have entered the room as well.

The devils, sensing an opportunity to cause chaos and destruction, begin darting around the room with their fiery claws and sharp teeth. The party must navigate the room carefully, trying to avoid any sudden movements that might trigger the explosives, while also fending off the aggressive devils.

The devils dart around the room, attacking the party from all angles, while the adventurers struggle to maintain their balance and avoid the unstable explosives. One misstep could result in a catastrophic explosion that would surely spell their doom.

The party must think strategically and work together to take down the devils while avoiding any sudden movements. It's a tense and dangerous encounter, but if they succeed, they may find themselves one step closer to their ultimate goal of finding Gruumsh and saving the realm from the Blight.

## Encounters
---
If the bombs go off, everyone in a 25-ft radius takes 9d12 points of fire damage.

## Notes
---
Party will receive an infernal bomb: As an action, a character can light this bomb and throw it at a point up to 60 feet away. Each creature within 5 feet of that point must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take 3d6 fire damage and 1d6 necrotic damage.

They could also receive a soul shard, which is necessary to enter the Underrealm. Faint screams of pain can be heard from within the shard.
