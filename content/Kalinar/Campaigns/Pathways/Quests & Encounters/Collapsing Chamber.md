## Overview
---
The party is exploring a chamber deep within the Mines when the ceiling suddenly collapses, trapping them inside. They must find a way to escape before they are buried alive.

## Encounters
---
>*As you delve further into the mine, you come across a chamber that is large and cavernous. Several pillars and stalactites are scattered throughout. The ceiling is high. As you enter the chamber, you feel the ground below you begin to shake. Soft, at first. Then violently. Your guide becomes incredibly exasperated. "Damn! The chamber is collapsing! Find cover!"*

**DC 14 Athletics or Acrobatics** to avoid tripping or falling as you move through the unstable terrain while attempting to dodge falling rocks.
- On failure, take 4d6 bludgeoning damage.

>*Eventually, the rumbling stops. The dust settles, and all you hear is the heavy breathing of your companions. Eventually, your eyes adjust to the sight around you. You are trapped. Rubble is piled all around you as you lay on the ground, and only by some miracle has the small space in which you are now confined not been fully crushed by stone. Some of the pillars and stalactites have fallen, blocking your path into or out of the chamber.*

- **DC 15 Strength or Dexterity** to clear a path using tools such as a pickaxe, crowbar, etc.
- **DC 17 Strength or Dexterity** to clear a path without tools.
- If party takes too long, the rubble will begin to shift, pinning them to the ground and causing 4d6 bludgeoning damage as they are buried alive.

- **DC 16 Perception** to hear a [[The Blight|Blighted]] creature approaching, which has been attracted by the sound and commotion.

After defeating the creatures, a squad of guards (10) appears in the chamber to arrest the intruders.

>*Making your way out of the rubble, you squeeze your way out to the top of the rubble. You are relieved to have survived the collapse, but your relief is short lived. As you look up, you see the blade of an axe at your throat and a squad of guards behind it. The dwarf who holds it says with disdain, his eyes never leaving yours, "Arrest them."*

 [[Eldrid Withers|Eldrid]] will not be arrested due to diplomatic immunity, and he will spend his time trying to convince King [[Fargrim Ironfist|Ironfist]] to send aid to Theel. [[The Works In Progress]] will be [[Imprisonment in Den Fatum|imprisoned]].
