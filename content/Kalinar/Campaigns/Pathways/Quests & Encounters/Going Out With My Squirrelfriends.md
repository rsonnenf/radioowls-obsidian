[[The Works In Progress]] encountered a squirrel in the Misty Forest who told them that Dire Clan minions had thrown rocks at his brood, which was a grave disrespect to the One Acorn, an apparent deity to the squirrels, for whom they serve as some sort of extension.

The squirrels are planning to take their revenge on those who disrespected them and the One Acorn.
