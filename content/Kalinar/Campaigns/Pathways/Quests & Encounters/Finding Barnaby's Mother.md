[[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] grew up in mountains near Courdney. Went to school. Grew up there. Mom left dad 25 years ago. (Barnaby is 50, relatively young) She was a gifted artificer. Like most talented people, she left town, and dad had family so wouldn't go with. Barnaby grew up to be an artificer as well. Vomila Baffletop was mother's name. She left and went to Hove. Lived a successful life. And so did Barnaby. Working for Artificer's Guild. He "just wants that." Before they are best friends, they are co-workers with a job. It is his mission to finish contract right now. Respects his coworkers right now, but he's tired of putting it off.

[[Vomilla Baffletop|Barnaby's mother]] is named Vomilla. She was an artificer in Rhuie. She was set on creating magical inventions and meticulously documented her experiments. This took time away from Barnaby, who was young enough not to remember much. Eventually, Ethel became consumed by her work, and Barnaby was raised elsewhere. He had one of her journals and read it, which inspired him to become an artificer himself. Ethel, though, became obsessed with merging spirits with mechanical objects to give them lives of their own, and she pushed the limits on what she could create. She created a Nimblewright imbued with a shadow, but she was unable to control it. It broke loose and killed many in the town. She was devastated that her creation caused such damage, but she needed to complete pushing the boundaries of her work, so she fled.

Dissenter, whose family was killed captured Ethel. He learned that she has a son, Barnaby, who is apparently also an artificer. He seeks to draw Barnaby out for two reasons. First, to eliminate another risk of an artificer of the Baffletop family to cause more damage. Second, to cause Ethel pain by eliminating Barnaby as Ethel's construct had harmed his family.
