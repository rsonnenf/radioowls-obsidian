## Overview
---
With the death of Grummsh, the efforts of Mayari and the allied gods must adapt. Mayari asks [[The Works In Progress|the Works in Progress]] to convince  [[Fargrim Ironfist|King Ironfist]] and [[The Council of Seven Hammers|the Council of Seven Hammers]] to lend aid to Theel and its allies.

Mayari explains that Theel and its allies have amassed an army. Emperor Christopher has been in contact with her, and she has suggested that the Kalinarian forces focus their efforts on [[The Blight|Blighted]] creatures emerging from a massive portal that has recently appeared in the plains south of Coran. 
## Encounters
---
#### Introduction
>*Amidst the weight of sorrow for [[Bo Fortuna|Bo]]'s fate, the party gathers once more in somber contemplation. In the dimly lit chamber, Mayari, the wise and enigmatic seer, takes center stage, her eyes reflecting the burden of the future that lies ahead.*
>
>*"My companions," Mayari begins, her voice measured and resolute. "Though our quest to rescue Gruumsh from the clutches of the Underrealm's dark grip has not borne fruit, our journey is far from over. Fate has revealed another path, and our duty to protect our realm and its inhabitants remains steadfast."*
>
>*She draws a deep breath, her gaze locking with each member of the party, as if imparting a shared understanding of the responsibility that now lies upon their shoulders.*
>
>*"Beyond the loss of our beloved god, a new threat looms—a colossal portal, gushing forth Blighted creatures from its depths. These foul entities have set their sights upon the city of Coran and its surrounding lands. The Theel army valiantly holds the line, but the relentless tide of darkness threatens to overwhelm them," Mayari continues.*
>
>*Her words carry the weight of urgency, and the gravity of the situation permeates the room. The Works in Progress, seasoned adventurers bound by a shared sense of camaraderie, now find themselves facing a pivotal moment—a chance to rally the forces of Den Fatum and King Fargrim Ironfist to stand as one against the encroaching malevolence.*
>
>*"We must seek the aid of the Council of Seven—the ruling council of Den Fatum—and King Fargrim Ironfist, for only through their combined might can we hope to repel this onslaught," Mayari implores. "Blighted creatures are already laying waste to the fields outside of the city of Coran and the innocent lives that dwell near it."*
>
>*She pauses, allowing the weight of her words to sink in, before continuing with unyielding determination. "Bo and Gruumsh may not walk among us now, but their legacy must not falter. We must strive to preserve this realm, its people, and the bonds that tie us together."*
>
>*"We have faced trials before, and we have emerged victorious. The Works in Progress, a beacon of hope in these dark times, can become a force of change."*
>
>*With her final words, Mayari's gaze burns with unwavering conviction, and a sense of determination envelops the Works in Progress. Their hearts are heavy with the memory of their fallen god, yet they stand ready to face the challenges ahead, knowing that their actions may shape the very course of destiny itself. The quest to protect their realm takes on new purpose, and they prepare to set forth on this perilous journey with hope in their hearts and the memory of Bo's vision firmly etched in their souls.*
