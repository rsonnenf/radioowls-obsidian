[[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] has seen two visions (below). They are warnings. Shayna's amulet was her father's, a devotee of Grummsh, and his worship imbued it with divine characteristics, allowing it to sense Grummsh. Grummsh, whose rage has always made him volatile, is reaching his breaking point in captivity. The amulet is picking up Grummsh's rage, as his anger is breaking through the magic barrier the Blight put in place to keep him and the other gods hidden.

[[Mayari]] fears that Grummsh will soon be too engulfed in his anger and rage that he will go rogue. Mayari will relay that she has located the portal to the portion of the Underrealm where Grummsh is being hidden. It is in the depths far below the underground city of Den Fatum. But there is an issue. The dwarves who run the city are very protective of it. They allow no outsiders into the caverns below their city, where they mine. The players will need to figure out how to make their way into the caverns beneath Solitude.
