## Overview
---
The lost caravan is a group of merchants traveling through the desert to reach a distant city. The merchants are lost, running low on supplies, and in desperate need of help. The party may encounter the caravan while flying over the desert, or they may come across their camp while searching for a place to make an emergency landing. The merchants are seeking a guide to take them to the nearest city, and they offer to pay the party for their services. The journey to the city is filled with danger and adventure, as the party must navigate through the desert, fight off hostile creatures, and avoid treacherous sand dunes. The party must also be careful, as the merchants may have valuable goods that attract the attention of bandits or sky pirates.
