## Overview
---
The entrance to [[the Mines]] is heavily guarded by dwarven soldiers who are responsible for keeping outsiders out. The party might need to persuade or deceive the guards to let them pass, or they could try to sneak past them. However, the guards are highly trained and observant, so getting past them won't be easy.

## Encounters
---
>*You arrive at the entrance to the Mines to find it heavily guarded by a group of dwarven guards. Behind the guards, you can see that the entrance is carved into the stone of the mountain. It is blocked by a gate. On either side of the entrance sit two torches. As you approach the entrance, one of the dwarves holds up his hand to stop you from going any further. "Halt. State yer business, outsiders. These sacred Mines are off-limits."*

If the party seeks to enter the Mines despite the rebuffs of the guards, they must attempt to persuade the guards to let them in, sneak in, or attack.

## Notes
---
The dwarven guards will be on high alert and will attack immediately if they suspect the party is hostile. Otherwise, they will challenge the party and allow them to speak before taking any action.

Persuading the guards to allow them to enter will be very difficult, as they have orders directly from the [[Grondar Deepdelver]] not to allow anyone in.
- **DC 25 Persuasion** to be allowed in. If the party succeeds, the guards will allow them to enter the Mines, but they will be closely watched and will need to be escorted by a guide.
