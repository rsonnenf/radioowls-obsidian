[[Kahn]] tells the group that [[Oswald]] owes her 10,000 GP. 
- She is offering 20% if you can find him and return the money. 
	- Players can convince her to bump up to 40% with **Persuasion/Intimidation DC 15** and 60% with **Persuasion Intimidation DC 20
- Kahn tells you that Oswald was especially fond of [[Aenalys]], one of the courtesans upstairs.
	- Aenalys will initially deny knowing Oswald. Try to kick out party. **Persuasion/Intimidation DC 11**, and she will inform players:
		- Oswald was sentimental with her and visited her to listen to old Kalinarian epics. He started behaving very paranoid in recent days, though, and eventually, he gave her his [[Oswald's Journal|journal]] for safekeeping, then disappeared.
		- If players fail, they will have to figure out that they will need to find Oswald's journal.
			- Journal is in Aenalys's armoire.
- Taking the journal back to Kahn, she will read it and wonder what has happened to him. She comes to the conclusion that he has been taken, but, initially, she is not sure where.
	- **Religion DC 14**  - recognize the symbol as that of [[Chernobog]]
	- Kahn will recall that there is a temple nearby dedicated to the worship of Chernobog. 

- Group must make its way to Chernobog's Temple.
	- Inside the temple, they will come across a group of Chernobog cultists led by Inquisitor Lodwyn. Oswald is chained to a pillar. The top of the pillar is carved into the shape of a bear's head with horns on it. In place of the bear's eyes are two coals. Before him are seven Chernobog cultists (Warlocks of the Fiend). One announces that Oswald has broken an oath and sentence him to death.
- If players save Oswald, they learn that he has no money. 
	- He will offer them a Nightfall Pearl instead, which he found in the temple. Only has 3 charges.
- Upon return to Kahn, she will be upset to learn that she will not be repaid, but thank the group for their assistance.
