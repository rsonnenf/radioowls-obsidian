## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Pasted image 20240609091921.png|right|600]]You arrived at [[Courdney]], and after spending a few minutes to get acquainted, you went to the tavern, [[the Reliable Meadery]]. The barkeep told you that odd things are happening in the city right now. First, he tells you that the city is in the midst of a political upheaval as Lady Lanthe struggles to keep her power while a group of sorcerers attempts to challenge her authority. Second, you learn that several disappearances have been happening around the city. Livestock and people are vanishing without a trace, and odd puddles of blood are found intermittently. The barkeep notes that the guards clearly seem unequipped to help. You asked him about [[Thrandunir]], but all he has to say is that people have been coming in and out of the city since the unusual political situation started, but that's all he knows. After buying a room, [[Grum-Grum]], [[Nailo]], and [[Troy Staari|Troy]] head to the market district. They find a general store and buy a pair of goggles for [[Talia]] that may or may not help her with being in direct sunlight. Grum-Grum now intends to head to the Temple of Grummish. Talia leaves with [[the Guardian]] to head to the library. She asks a librarian if she can direct her to where she might find information on [[Justicars]], and the librarian clearly becomes exasperated, explaining that such information is no longer discussed publicly. Once Talia gained her trust, the librarian takes her to the restricted section of the library. Talia learns that the Justicars were an order of wise, seemingly divine advisors to the Emperor of Kalinar, [[Emperor Dalran|Dalran]], after he united the realm 350 years ago with the help of his friend and Counselor, [[Brondur]]. She learns that, after years of Dalran's heirs as empresses and emperors and Brondur's heirs as Consulors with the Justicars guiding them over the years, the situation changed 30 years ago. Counselor Brondur's heir, [[Fenn]] became displeased with his role, and sought to take power from the then-[[Empress Hayden]]. He waged a campaign for 5 years, known as [[the War of Ash]], to overthrow Empress Hayden and to eliminate and erase all knowledge of the Justicars in the process. At the conclusion of those 5 years, Fenn succeeded. He overthrew Empress Hayden and took the title for himself. He serves as Kalinar's false Emperor to this day.
