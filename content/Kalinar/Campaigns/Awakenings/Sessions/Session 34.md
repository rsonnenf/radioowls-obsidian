## Summary of Last Session
---
After emerging from the cave, you send N[[Nailo|ailo ]]off in hawk form to scout out the area.  She tells you that she sees a camp with several odd lights emanating from various points near the cliff.  You walk your way forward to find several knights and [[Valador]], a man who was torturing [[Christopher]] at [[Fort Gloomwind]], watching over several individuals in deep concentration next to bright red columns of light.  From one of the columns emerged a winged creature that flew off to the battle, but another one emerged from a second portal.  You began the battle by throwing a bead from the necklace of fireballs, and after doing so, all hell broke loose.  Creatures emerged from the portal, screeching from the creatures paralyzed some of you with fear, and a mysterious purple haze seemed to empower any of you who stepped into it while making you incredibly volatile. As a result of the odd power, Grum-Grum became a sheep and later turned blue. Eventually, after killing several of the creatures, Talia sent Valador careening off the cliff to his likely death, but not before Grum-Grum and Carnell were rendered unconscious.  Eventually, you triumphed and healed Grum-Grum.  Turning, you see Carnell unconscious on the ground.  You also notice that the purple mist has not dissipated.
