## Summary of Last Session
---
After reviving [[Grum-Grum]] and [[Troy Staari|Troy]], the four of you traversed the rest of the cave until you came across more snake-like creatures standing over a man holding an empty vial. You attacked in the hopes of saving him, but in the heat of the battle, the man's skin turned scaly, his pupils turned to slits, and he gradually morphed into one of the creatures and attacked. You dispatched the rest of them, and after making your way back to the entrance of the cave, you returned to the village. You found Mayor [[Fenbir]], who thanked you and rewarded you with 600gp, and he directed you to the nearest intact inn, which was to the north of town. He told you to take any four horses from the stables next to the inn, and you went to purchase a room for the night.
