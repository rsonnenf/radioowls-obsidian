## Summary of Last Session
---
Making your way up from the cellar after obtaining some valuable items, you decide to take the night to rest. In the morning, you start pondering the note you found on the dark elf bearing the initials B.R. and eventually come across a man named [[Carnell]] who knows of him.  After prying it out of him, you learn that B.R. is Baron Redfell, a thief and swindler who goes throughout Kalinar stealing valuable items to sell on the Black Market.  Carnell tells you that he's from Solitude far to the west, but he might be planning something big at one of the museums in the capital, [[Theel]].  He also notes that it's possible that someone might be directing Baron Redfell, but he doesn't know for sure.  You take the information, and eventually make your way to the palace to see if there is a plan to meet the Imperial army.  The queen tells you that she would like you to go behind enemy lines to attack the general leading the army, but she doesn't know whether that is set in stone yet because she doesn't know what the numbers of her forces looks like yet.  But you agree and decide it might be worth trying to recruit Carnell.  After some time walking the city and relaxing at the inn, you hear the [[Courdney]] force arriving.  You make your way to the top of a wall and watch as the force from Courdney makes its way toward the city. You also see a force of elves emerge from the woods, some constructs headed toward the palace, and Fey and Karen carrying some health potions toward the army.  You made your way back to the inn, where a goblin saw you exchanging items, including the Necklace of Fireballs, and [[Troy Staari|Troy]] promptly cut off his arm when he attempted to chat.  After things settled down. [[Thrandunir]], [[Allana]], [[Wir]], and [[Christopher]] walked into the tavern.
