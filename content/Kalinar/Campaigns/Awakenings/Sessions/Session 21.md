## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Pasted image 20240725211911.png|right|750]]After traveling for a few more days, you came across a flickering orange light off in the distant field. As you approached, you noticed that it was a town in flames. You launched into action, assisting the town as several scaly humanoid creatures killed some villagers and apprehended others attempting to whisk them away. You were able to kill off several of the creatures, assist in putting out several fires, and provide aid to wounded villagers, who told you that the town was called Altivin. Afterwards, you approached and met the mayor of Altivin, Andrew Fenbir, and after watching him provide guidance and reassurances to the disoriented and distraught townspeople, you asked what you might be able to do to help. Mayor Fenbir said that the creatures have been raiding [[Altivin]] for the last two or three months and kidnapping townspeople, but this is the first time that they launched an all-out attack. He asked you to see if you could rescue any remaining villagers that might still live, explaining that there have been sightings of the scaly creatures near a cave system about a mile northwest of town. He offers you whatever monetary reward they might be able to put together and offers you four horses, which will save you several days of travel you would have otherwise had to make on foot. You agreed and began making your way toward the cave.
