## Summary of Last Session
---
You left the town of [[Altivin]] after a good night's rest, taking a four of the horses from the stables. After a day of travel, you camped for the night and had an opportunity to talk with each other for a bit. Talia revealed to [[the Guardian]] that she left the underground due to friction with the way her people tended to conduct themselves. The Guardian revealed that he is a soul-infused object that was sworn to protect and seeks to continue doing so. [[Nailo]] reveals that she was an elf raised by animals. [[Troy Staari|Troy]], on the other hand, was sleeping soundly in the tent. As [[Grum-Grum]] finished his portion of the watch, a large yeti snuck up on him and attacked, and over the course of the battle, he was beaten bloody and knocked unconscious before the group was able to subdue it. You traveled about a mile away where you found the yeti's den and obtained 80 gold pieces, a shiny black statuette, and a ceremonial-looking dagger before finishing up the last leg of your trip to [[Twindolen]]. You met with guards who were clearly frantic at the gate of the city, and they explained that they feared an impending attack from the Capital of [[Theel]]. One noted that they are very outnumbered. You mentioned that you bring word from [[Courdney]], and they brought you to the Royal Palace where you met with [[Queen Jamil]], who was happy to hear that you were bringing good news.
