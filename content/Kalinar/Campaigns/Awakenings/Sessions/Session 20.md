## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Pasted image 20240725211032.png|right|700]]Once you made your way through the ravine, [[RPG Rules/Pathfinder 2e/Mechanics/Backgrounds/Trailblazer|Trailblazer]] led you to a large river, expecting you to cross it. You cut down a tree and used it to float across. After reaching the other side, Trailblazer led you to a portion of the forest he claimed needed to be culled because it was not "flourishing" and was failing to produce anything. You noticed that, as it appeared to you, this part of the forest was plenty green and active. Trailblazer began berating you to burn down the forest, and when you tried to reason with him, he became increasingly angry until he attacked you. After an intense battle, you killed Trailblazer, and [[Troy Staari|Troy]], after examining him, deduced that he was likely bewitched. You all concluded that the "curse" that was rumored to permeate the forest had manifested itself in Trailblazer and led him mad--to the point that he wanted to burn the forest down.
