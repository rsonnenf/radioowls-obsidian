## Summary of Last Session
---
 After hiding evidence of your battle with the guards in the forest clearing after meeting with the Contact, you made your way to the weapons cache. You found an opening in the bluff that the cache was built into, and snuck in. You fought your way through several guards, and eventually find a polished-stone door. The face of a dragon emerged from a circular pendant on the door, and it explains that it was tasked with guarding the tomb of a great dragonborn warrior, but failed when the authorities at Fort Gloomwind broke in to use the chamber as a storage unit for weapons. After telling the dragon that you would remove the sullied weapons from the tomb within, it agreed to let you in if you would explain three things to it about the world outside, which it hoped to see one day. You explain, and the dragon is moved. It lets you in, and you take the weapons. [[Grum-Grum]], looked for a way to take the dragon pendant to see the rest of the world, and the dragon, which explains is known as "[[The Guardian]]" agrees to go with. You gather the weapons and The Guardian and make your way out of the cavern.
