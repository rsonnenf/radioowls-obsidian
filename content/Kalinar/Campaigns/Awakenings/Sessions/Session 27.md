## Summary of Last Session
---
You engaged in an incredibly perilous battle with a Frost Giant that left [[Grum-Grum]] unconscious, but with the help of a woman who also fought against it, you were able to bring it down.  The woman reveals that she is Evalyn, and you note that you are [[Justicars]] sent to find her and explain your experience dissipating the magic of an orb held by Administrator Haera in [[Fort Gloomwind]], she explains that the Emperor uses a form of dark magic that only Justicars were capable of fending off. She says that it seems the Emperor has been using this magic to call forth otherworldly creatures and undead monsters to secure his hold on the realm. She notes that, with some training, you will be able to assist in retaking the Throne.  You make your way to King Faeren, the King of [[Bysaes Tyl]], and convince him to send 2,000 troops to aid in the defense of [[Twindolen]].  He wished that he could send more, but notes that he needs the troops he can to assist in the rebuilding of the town, which was ravaged by the attack from the undead creatures.  You made your way back to Twindolen to give a report to [[Queen Jamil]], and Grum-Grum convinces her to send laborers to assist in the rebuilding of Bysaes Tyl in the hopes that King Faeren will send additional troops.  Queen Jamil informs you that the force from [[Courdney]] is on its way and will arrive in several days, and the force from the Capital of [[Theel]] will soon be departing. In the meantime, you have several days to regroup, so you went to a tavern to sleep for the evening.  You awake the next morning to find that Grum-Grum is laying down in his bed where he was, but he's wearing beautifully crafted plate armor, and [[the Guardian]] looks totally different.

Grum-Grum's experience:  After saving Molly, he agreed to help you perfect some plate armor you recovered and to reforge the Guardian if you went to Barney's old shop and convinced him to leave him and his business alone moving forward.  You agreed and went to Barney's old, seemingly abandoned shop. Inside, you came across several of his constructs, but you were able to convince Barney to call them off. You then convinced him that, instead of terrorizing Molly, he should redirect his efforts toward building constructs to assist in the defense of the city from the impending attack from the Capital.  He agreed, and you returned to Molly. He helped you perfect the plate armor, and despite his initial worries, you and him were able to completely reforge the Guardian into a dedicated shield that will allow him to continue to fulfill his oath to protect.  As a result, he took on the new name The Oathkeeper of the Dragon.  After completing this gargantuan and complicated task, you returned to the Mischievous Goat as the sun was about to rise and went back to bed for a good 30 minutes more.
