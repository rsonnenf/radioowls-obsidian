## Summary of Last Session
---
After [[Thrandunir]], [[Allana]], [[Christopher]], and [[Wir]] arrived in [[Twindolen]] with the force from [[Courdney]], you had an opportunity to speak with them after their audience with the queen.  They told you that they brought 2,000 troops and that the force from [[Theel]] will be arriving in the next day or so.  They also brought bad news.  [[Rilad]] snuck her way into the force from Courdney, apparently wanting to assist in the fight, but during the trip, the force was attacked by a small group of raiders.  Rilad was taken, apparently to the Gulf of [[Bilell]], and due to the gravity of the impending battle, the Courdney force was unable to mount a rescue.  After speaking with them, you learned that the plan is to sneak through the caverns under the fields to a point on the battlefield the Queen's military advisors believe the leader of the Imperial army will post up.  They think it will be on a cliff overlooking the battlefield.  The next morning, you head out.  You make your way into the cave system as the battle begins, and come to learn that it is riddled with traps.  Spiked pits, falling logs, and nets that trapped you in fire impeded your way, but you have successfully made your way through so far.
