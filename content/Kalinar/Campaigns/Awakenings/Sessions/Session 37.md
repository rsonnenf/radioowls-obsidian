## Summary of Last Session
---
After you passed the tests with Evalyn, she informed you that [[Queen Jamil]] received an excellent opportunity to have you test your skills.  Evalyn conveyed that the town of Hornsmeet to the Northwest has been experiencing troubles, and the Queen wants you, as [[Justicars]], to test your skills and alleviate the situation.  You agreed, and Evalyn gave you white cloaks, symbols of Justicars.  She sent along her protégé, Orsik, to help guide you and protect you until your powers are under control and you are comfortable with them.  You head out to Hornsmeet and meet an elf in a tavern.  Eventually, he reveals himself to be a firbolg named Leo.  He explains that wolves have been attacking the town frequently, and they have stolen the Mayor's prized possession, the Horn of Valdune, which he believes might be found in Icewing Fortress, an abandoned military installation.  He explains that he advises the Mayor, and before you are able to retire for the evening, wolves attacked the tavern.  You were able to fend them off, but not before discovering that several kobolds accompanied the wolves.  You're unsure as to why.  After killing the kobolds and fending off the wolves, Mayor Stonehammer arrives at the scene to examine the damage.  He explains that the horn is a prized ceremonial object and that the attacks have been relentless.  He then leaves to inspect the damage, hoping that in the morning you will head to Icewing Fortress to obtain the horn.
