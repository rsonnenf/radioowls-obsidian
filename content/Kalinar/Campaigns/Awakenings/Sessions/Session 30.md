## Summary of Last Session
---
After bringing the tumor moss back to Karen and Fey, or Madam Meech as you initially thought, [[Nailo]] asked for any advice she might have on concocting potions. Karen pulled a tome off of a bookshelf and gave it to Nailo, who gathered that, with study, she would be able to learn how to brew healing and greater healing potions.  You left and learned that the force from [[Courdney]] would be arriving in about a day and a half.  In the meantime, as you were walking the streets, you found a note hidden under a loose cobblestone indicating that certain items that a certain person with initials "B.R." hid in the cellar of the Mischievous Goat would be found there.  You made your way to the tavern, and after several unsuccessful attempts to make your way past the bartender to make it into the cellar, you were able to do so by announcing that everyone could get a beer on [[Talia]], which caused a commotion.  Nailo gave you extra cover by casting Pass Without a Trace, and you made your way into the cellar of the Inn.  Inside, you found a locked chest, and [[Grum-Grum]] was about to smash it open when you heard someone coming down the stairs.  You hid, and when the person came around the corner, he readied for a fight.  After an intense battle, including poison fog, entangling vines, and firebolts, whoever this individual was succumbed to the fight, and you found a note on him.  It was charred by the effects of the firebolts, but you can see it was a note intended to be left and given to "B.R.," whoever these items were left by.
