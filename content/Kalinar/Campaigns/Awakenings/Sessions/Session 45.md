## Summary of Last Session
---
After waking up in the morning, you find that the imperial guards have left, and you made your way to the capital city of [[Theel]]. You see food wagon approaching the city gates and charm the individuals into allowing you to enter the city with them. You do so successfully, and eventually make your way to the shop of Arietta an acquaintance of Falmore.  After being promised a ton of gold, she agrees to give you incredibly convincing disguises and to allow you to stay in the room above her shop for the night.  You agree, and decide that before you can rest for the evening until the army arrives, you should scout out the sewers, which Arietta has explained would be a good entry point into the city for [[Thrandunir]] and [[Christopher]], to make sure it is safe.  You make your way into the sewers and, after realizing that the Dark Magic of the Underrealm penetrates the area, find a cockroach-looking being appearing from a portal, which you gather must connect to the Underrealm.  After a fierce battle, the cockroach looking being succumbs to you, and you realize that the distance between the Underrealm and this realm is closer than you would have thought and is dangerously close to breaching this realm based upon the dark magic wielded by the Emperor.
