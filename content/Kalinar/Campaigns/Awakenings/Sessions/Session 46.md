## Summary of Last Session
---
You made your way through the sewers and dissipated the Underrealm Magic long enough to send a message to Christopher and Thrandunir, telling them where they can enter the city.  After a few hours, they arrived and you all caught up before heading to Arietta's shop, where she had a room waiting for you.  You spent the night there before heading back into the sewers in the morning to send a message to Evalyn, telling her that you are prepared for the army to approach.  She responded that the army would be arriving in a few hours and that they would wait outside the city, drawing out the Imperial army but waiting to attack until they are attacked.  She tells you that now is the time for you to attack the Capitol and overthrow the Emperor.  You agree to do so, but decide to stop by a tavern, the Guilded Dragon, until the Army arrives.  Inside, you see several Imperial guards drinking amongst the citizenry.  A table of unruly guards begins to harass Orsik, requiring him to buy ales for the table.  They begin to threaten him until Talia causes a drink to fly into a guard's face.  When they roar, asking who threw it, Talia defuses the situation by offering to buy them a drink.  They calm down, and everyone goes back to their drinks.
