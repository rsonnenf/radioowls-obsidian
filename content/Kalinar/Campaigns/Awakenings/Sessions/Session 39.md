## Summary of Last Session
---
After speaking with the two wolves who helped you kill the kobolds in the canyon, you learn that their pack leader has been acting odd.  He, for some reason, allied with the kobolds, and the rest of the pack does not know why.  You agreed to figure out why the leader has had this change of heart, and the wolves lead you to Icefang Keep.  It is littered with traps, and you came across a Kobold operating a ballista.  He says he was there to guard against intruders, and he seemed to give off the impression that he didn't want to be there. While Orsik was conversing with him, [[Troy Staari|Troy]] entered two separate rooms filled with kobolds.  After failing to convince them that he and the rest of the group were sent by the wolves to help them, they attacked, and a massive fight broke out.  Eventually, though, after Craig was dazed by a blast of eldritch energy from [[Talia]], he accidntally fired the ballista into one of the kobolds, killing him.  Upon coming to and realizing what he had done, he immediately fled, and the rest of the kobolds, ran after him, presumably to rip him apart.
