## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[mountain_road_by_allrichart_dccmpyu-fullview.jpg|right|700]]You woke up in the camp, and [[Allana]] tells you to travel ahead to Courdney to wait for them and get some information on where they might find [[Thrandunir]]. Allana, Christopher, and [[Wir]] went toward Zala, where she said she had allies there she wanted to get in touch with. You head out, and after several days of travel in the forest, you came across a bard named Brandwick who sought to become big in the Capital of Kalinar. He was practicing because people disliked his music, and when you asked him to sing, you found out why as he sang songs that made fun of you. A few days later, you came across a log in the middle of your path. Two ogres, who had apparently set a trap, attacked you, but you fought them off successfully. After several more days of travel, you camp for the evening, and noticed several men watching your camp from afar. After offering them food, one of them suddenly stabbed [[Nailo]], apparently hoping to kill you all and steal your belongings. You killed three of them off, and after pinning the leader to the ground, Grum-Grum force-fed him stew until he stopped gurgling and fighting, having choked to death.
