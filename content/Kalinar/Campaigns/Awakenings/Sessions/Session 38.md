## Summary of Last Session
---
After talking with some of the locals at Hornsmeet, you determined were not worth your time given their….poor worldviews. You left the next morning for Icewing Fortress on your quest to find the Horn of Valdune for Mayor Stonehammer.  The road was treacherous, but you were able to make your way past icy stairways built into the mountain and an icy field laden with traps.  Eventually, you saw the keep before you, but you had to make your way through a canyon with several boulders in the middle of it.  You began making your way through, but encountered several kobolds and wolves.  You began fighting them, but after Leo convinced the wolves to stop fighting you and to turn on the Kobolds for taking advantage of them, they seemingly agreed and began helping you.  You all were able to take out the kobolds, and you now stand with dead kobolds at your feet and exhausted wolves standing before you.
