## Summary of Last Session
---
 After having killed [[Administrator Haera]], you began your attempt to escape the keep as the flames in the room began to grow. Commotion in the keep began to intensify as the personnel began realizing that something is amiss. As Talia, [[Christopher]], Grum-Grum, and Thea walked down the first flight of stairs, a guard in an impeccable uniform stopped [[Troy Staari|Troy]] and Nailo. As he attempted to arrest them, they fought back and killed him. [[Talia]], who had stolen one of the Administrator's uniforms, convinced the guards at the entrance of the keep that she was Haera, and the group walked out into the Fort. They freed Thea from her shackles and gave her her sister's medallion. The group met at the rendezvous point with Jelen, [[Rilad]], and Devin, and the group safely escaped the Fort. On the way back to camp, Christopher was very interested in how they were able to dissipate the magic of the Administrator's seeing stone when they walked into the room. When the group made it back to camp, [[Allana]] and [[Wir]] expressed their gratitude to the group for their help, and Christopher asked to speak with Allana. Talia overheard that Christopher suspects the group might be Justicars (which she recognized as a group that was active several decades ago). The next morning, Allana asked the group if they would be willing to travel to Courdney to meet with Thrandunir, who is apparently affiliated with Christopher, and whom she learned is staying there. She explains that it will be an opportunity to figure out what the next steps will be, and the group agreed to do so.
