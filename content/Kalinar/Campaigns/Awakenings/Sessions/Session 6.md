## Summary of Last Session
---
 You leave the cache and make your way back to the Camp, where [[Allana]] looks over the map of [[Fort Gloomwind|Gloomwind]]. After a night's rest, you leave with about 20 members of the group, and arrive at Gloomwind. You part ways with Allana's band, and after making your way through the caves and into the Fort, you made your way to the Salty Gull, a tavern, where the barkeep, Devin, lets on that he knows of Allana and her plan to rescue [[Christopher]]. As Allana's group draws guards away from the Fort, Devin directs you to a shadowy figure in one of the corners of the tavern, and you find that it is [[Rilad]]. She tells you that she knows of no plan to rescue anyone, but Devin, whom she befriended after arriving a few days before, seems to speak highly of an "Allana." She decides that it's likely worth helping you, so she takes you to a tailor named Jelen. Jelen tries to sell you some of his atrocious-looking clothes, and Troy receives an awful pair of pants, but after a time, Jelen sells you some convincing guard uniforms which you might use to get into the Keep.
