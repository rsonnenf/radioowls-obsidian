## Summary of Last Session
---
 Tasked by Mayor [[Fenbir]] to go to the caves to the Northwest of town to rescue kidnapped townspeople, you found and entered the cave. You were immediately attacked by several large snakes, but were able to quickly dispatch them. Continuing through the caverns [[Grum-Grum]] feel victim to a pit trap, falling 40 feet and having difficulty getting out until eventually the rest of the group was able to pull him free. In another chamber, you found a glowing orb made of a clear gemstone orb. From a far distance, you [[Talia]] used Mage Hand to retrieve it, but it turned out to be a trap. The ceiling caved in, and you lost the orb. Later, you approached a door that read "An Offering To Pass." After attempting to place several items inside, you determined that sliding a sword into a slot in the door unlocked and opened it, and inside, you saw several of the scaly creatures and a large snake-like creature getting ready to thrust a dagger into two humanoids bound to an alter in front of it.
