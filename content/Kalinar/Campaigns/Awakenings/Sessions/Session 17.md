## Summary of Last Session
---
 You left the throne room after reporting our findings to Lady Lanthe to meet with [[Christopher]], [[Allana]], [[Rilad]], and [[Wir]]. You encountered three women who were oddly adamant about having you guess how a shopkeep disappeared. They explained that a reddish pink powder was found throughout his house. You investigated and learned that due to an accident involving the powder, which you learned was incredibly flammable, the shopkeep was incinerated in his home. You reported your findings to a guard before meeting Allana, Christopher, Rilad, Wir, and [[Thrandunir]] at the Green Cask Inn. Allana informed you that she convened the Resistance Council in [[Courdney]], and Thrandunir explained that their efforts should be focused on saving [[Twindolen]], another resistance-friendly city that has been targeted by Emperor [[Fenn]] for a military strike. Thrandunir also notes that another [[Justicars|Justicar]] lives in Twindolin who can train them. All agree that the group's next step is to convince the Council to launch an offensive against Fenn, and that the first step should be to assist Twindolin. Thrandunir toasts to your agreement.  and officially begin a campaign to retake the throne.
