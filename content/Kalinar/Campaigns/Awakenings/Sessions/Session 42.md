## Summary of Last Session
---
After making your way out of Frostwind Keep, you made your way, via magic courtesy of Leo, back to Hornsmeet.  You told Mayor Stonehammer that you gave the Horn of Valdune to the Vilyomah and that the wolves intend to negotiate with the Mayor about taking back their land.  Mayor Stonehammer is upset that you did not bring him the horn, but he nevertheless reluctantly gives you half of the promised reward, believing that there's a good chance that he'll get the horn back during negotiations.  He seemed amenable to giving the wolves their land back in exchange for the horn.  You made your way back to [[Twindolen]] and gave a report to Evalyn, who affirmed that you have completed your training.  She explained the last portion of the plan to take [[Theel]]:  You will head out and attempt to dissipate the dark magic surrounding the city, allowing the resistance army to approach and cause a distraction while you enter the throne room and deal with Emperor Fen.  You will head out in a few days' time, and she tells you to visit Glint, a shopkeep with rare wares in the Markets District.  You head that way and run into a Half-Elf named Falmore.  He is a little odd, and tells you stories about how he came to Twindolen:  He was a member of the guard at Theel, and he somehow obtained odd powers that you recognize to be the very dark magic you are tasked with eliminating.  He seemed oblivious to the dark nature of his newfound powers.  He told you that he was headed to Twindolen because he heard rumors that [[Justicars]] were back. He seeks their assistance in cleansing Theel.  You are offput by his dark powers, but he accompanies you as you try to head to Glint's store, where you entered and bought several rare magical items.
