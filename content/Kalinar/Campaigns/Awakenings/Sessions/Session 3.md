### Beaches Outside Fort Gloomwind

You awake feeling the heat of the sun beating down on you, and you hear the sounds of crashing waves nearby and gulls overhead. You think you must be close to the water, and your suspicions are confirmed when a wave crashes against you, once again soaking your already-soaked clothes. You realize that you're lying on a white-sand beach. Palm trees dot the shoreline, and the beach is pristine except for some debris that washed ashore. You don't see the ship in sight. As you sit up, you see your companions start to stir as well. You're relieved, and it immediately strikes you that you know next to nothing about them.

Perception DC 6 - notice a wooded area some several hundred feet from the shoreline.

Perception DC 12 - to notice footprints in the sand that lead toward the brushline. They do not appear to have originated from the shoreline.

You follow the footsteps, which lead you to the brushline and into a light maritime forest. You can still hear the waves of the ocean crash against the shore, but tropical trees, boulders, and foliage overtake the sand dunes. As the sand recedes to the littered soil of the forested area, the footsteps become less and less easy to follow, and after making your way further into the tropical forest, you lose them altogether.

Perception DC 13 to hear an almost imperceptible rustling of leaves about 80 or so feet to your right.

Before you can react, an arrow goes flying nearby, and—thunk—it embeds itself in a tree about three feet from Nailo's head. You hear a voice call out "Nobody move!" Stepping out from behind a tree, you see a male dwarf aiming an arrow, notched in a shortbow, at your group. "Who are you?" he asks.

Players follow [[Wir]] to camp. "Wir leads you through the tropical forest. Meeting Contact

You take the evening to rest. You awake in the morning just as the sun begins to rise, which burns off the bed of fog that settled early morning. You feel well-rested. You check in with Allana once more before you leave, and she gives you directions to where the contact said he would meet, which is about an hour and a half's hike away. After she bids you good luck, you head out. [Do anything?] After about an hour and a half of traveling, you come across a clearing in the woods. Standing on a stone ahead is an older man. He's wearing white robes with a hood over his head. He looks up to see you, and as you approach, he asks, "Were you followed?"

Contact: He is not attempting to betray the players. He was inspired by the group's ability to escape. He has felt guilt for enabling the holding of the prisoners and wants to do what he can to remedy that.

- Knows where Christopher is held.

The contact holds out his hand. In it, you see two folded pieces of parchment. "The maps of the weapons cache and the prisoner's location inside Gloomwind. I must warn you. The cache is heavily guarded from the entrance. But there is a secret way in. Through the caves. The complex is an underground warehouse built into bluff. A cave abuts the back of the chamber. "This rescue attempt is sure to be no easy task, but I wish you the best. I hope this prisoner is worth the effort."
