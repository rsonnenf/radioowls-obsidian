## Summary of Last Session
---
 You trudged your way through the cave, coming across cultists and fiendish-looking creatures you've never encountered before. After fending them off, you made your way into a large chamber at the end of the cave, where you saw four cultists and a bound man around an altar. One of the cultists cut the man and, using the blood from his wound, summoned a shadowy creature, which emerged from a purplish portal. Despite never having seen anything like it before, you were able to fend it and the cultists off, and you rescued the man, who you learned was Theodore before he passed out with exhaustion. You carried him, out to the entrance of the cave, where Bethany and Ellie were waiting, and after they reunite with him, you carried Theodore to his home. Bethany thanked you and gave you food and shelter for the evening after Theodore was laid in the room upstairs.
