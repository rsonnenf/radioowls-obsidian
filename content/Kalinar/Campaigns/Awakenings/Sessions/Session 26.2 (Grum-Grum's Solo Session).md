## Summary of Last Session
---
After waking up to find your companions still asleep, you decided that you would set out to find Molly so that you could forge [[the Guardian]] into a shield.  You made your way to the library, where you found out that all of the books on blacksmithing and forging have been checked out by Molly--you made your way to the Sphinx's Armor and Arms, Molly's store, and found a gnome there acting suspiciously.  While you thought he was Molly, he suspiciously knew very little about actual forging and even forgot the term blacksmith when pressed.  He seemed to be much more interested in constructs when they were brought up.  Eventually, you looked around and asked him to open a door in the back.  When he did, you heard him run away, and you made your way down the steps of the new room.  Inside, you saw a construct wearing plate metal that took a humanoid form and began to attack you. After a long-fought battle, you brought it down. Eventually, you heard knocking on the other side of one of the walls downstairs, where you found the true Molly on the other side after figuring out the secret way into the room.  Molly explained that he had been taken and trapped in his basement for quite some time. You explained that an imposter was upstairs claiming to be him, and Molly deduced that it was, in fact, Barney, a rival armorer who Molly drove out of business.   When you showed him the Guardian, he was amazed.  He told you he would help you perfect the plate armor you recovered from the construct and help with anything you wanted regarding the Guardian if you could find Barney.

Molly has been studying soulforged objects for a long time. He has been wanting to discern the art, and he has checked out many books from the library on the subject, but he has yet to crack the code.  He has figured out, however, that the soul is very difficult to destroy, and the vessel it is contained in can be remolded without damaging it--so long as the reforging process goes according to plan.  Molly wants you to find Barney and convince him to stay out of Molly's business affairs moving forward and leave him be.  He could call the guards on him, but he has decided to give Barney one last chance to be rational.
