## Summary of Last Session
---
 ![[Pasted image 20240611211418.png|right|700]]After leaving the cave, you made your way to the Bartons' home. Bethany allowed you to stay for the evening, and in the morning, you woke Theodore to ask him some questions about what happened. He told you that hooded figures took him to the cave to conduct grotesque rituals that summoned creatures he had never seen before using a language he had never heard. But he noticed a word being said over and over, which sounded like Garrodar, or Garrodir. Afterwards, you met with Captain Bearor at the guard post in front of [[the Great Palace]], and when you recounted what happened, Bearor was flabbergasted. He had never heard of summonings, and you got the impression that it was a very unnatural thing. He told you that you should report your findings to Lady Lanthe herself, and he took you into [[the Great Palace]] and to the Throne Room. After recounting what happened, Lady Lanthe ordered Bearor to gather more information about what was happening at the cave, and while the conversation was occurring, three assassins broke into the throne room. As one attempted to run past to attempt to kill the Lady, you stopped them and began to fend them off. A cloaked individual entered the throne room and gave assistance, and after you all killed the assassins, the cloaked individual introduced himself as [[Thrandunir]], the individual you were sent to [[Courdney]] to meet. He told you that he intercepted a letter from the three sorcerers who have been attempting to sow distrust and dissatisfaction in the public against Lady Lanthe with an attempt to take power. He explained that they apparently sent a letter to the assassins, contracting with them to kill the Lady when the public failed to support them.
