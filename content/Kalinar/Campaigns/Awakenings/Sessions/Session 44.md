## Summary of Last Session
---
You made your way back to the Throne room to hear the final plan.  You learned that the resistance army will be following in your footsteps to head to the capital.  They will draw out the army from the city so that the [[Justicars]] can enter the city and capture the Emperor.  [[Thrandunir]] and [[Christopher]] will also  be following you and meeting in the Throne Room so Christopher can take over as you eliminate the Emperor.  You travel away from [[Twindolen]] and, after making it into the woods, you encounter two Imperial guards.  They initially seemed skeptical of you, but you convinced them that you were just lost in the woods, and despite their skepticism, they let you sleep around their fire.  While you pretended to be asleep, you overheard them talking to each other, as they expressed concern about the Emperor's ability to call forth devils and demons from the Underrealm.  Eventually, though, you fall asleep and awake in the morning to find that they have gone, apparently leaving you asleep to allow you to get a few more hours of rest.
