## Summary of Last Session
---
 After spending a little more time over drinks with each other you see [[Thrandunir]] enter the tavern. He sits with you and over the course of the conversation, you reveal that you know [[Christopher]] and [[Allana]]. He explains that he met Christopher, who had no parents, as a boy and took him under his wing. They worked as mercenaries, and met Allana during their travels. Allana is the Captain of the Guard of the city of Twindolen, which is to the north. During the conversation, you also reveal that you heard you may be [[Justicars]]. Thrandunir is taken aback by this and somewhat excited. He explains, first that there were rumors that [[Fort Gloomwind]] is the prison camp set up for people Emperor [[Fenn]] believes has the potential to become Justicars, but that the real Justicars could very well be the four of you. He explains that several hundred years ago, the realm of Kalinar was splintered until it was united by two individuals, one of whom took the title of Emperor, while the other took the title of Counselor to the Emperor. Both were hereditary positions. He explained that the Justicars were divine military, political, and magical advisors to the Emperor and protectors of the realm, and over several generations, Kalinar thrived in peace. He explained, though, that after several generations of Emperors and Counselors to the Emperor, Fen, who was the Counselor at the time, became dissatisfied with his "second class" position to the Empress, [[Empress Hayden]]. Thrandunir explained that Fen overthrew Empress Hayden, eliminated the Justicars, and took the throne for himself. Thrandunir explained that, because you show potential to be Justicars, there is now hope that the throne can be retaken and Emperor Fen's reign of oppression can be terminated. He asks for your dedication in assisting bringing order to the realm, and you agree. He explains that a resistance to Emperor Fenn's illegitimate and tyrannical rule has been growing, and that Christopher and Allana likely went to Zara to convene the Council of Resistance leaders, who will likely meet here in Courdney because Lady Lanthe is a resistance sympathizer. He explains that, based on the timeline of events you described to him, Christopher and Allana should be arriving in the next few days. After discussing this a little longer, you watch as the sorcerers enter the tavern. They rent a room and go upstairs, likely to wait for the assassins who are supposed to meet them, and you follow. You break into the room and, after an intense battle, kill all three, but Talia brings one back to life to present to Lady Lanthe before knocking him out cold. You began discussing making your way back to the Great Palace.
