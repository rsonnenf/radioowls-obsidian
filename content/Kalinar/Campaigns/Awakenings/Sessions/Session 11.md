## Summary of Last Session
---
 [[Talia]], after learning of the [[Justicars]], left the library, but not before finding a Scroll of Delectibility. She met up with [[Grum-Grum]], [[Nailo]], and [[Troy Staari|Troy]] at the Temple of Grummsh. After an orc accosted Grum-Grum, you all left for Dragon's Farthing, where you saw the exterior of [[the Great Palace]] before being stopped by guards at a post in front of the structure. You told them you could help with the disappearances, and they led you into a small building to meet with Comandor Bearor. He told you that Theodore Barton has gone missing but that his daughter, Ellie, might have seen what happened. You went to the Barton household on the outskirts of the city and spoke with Bethany, Theodore's wife. She agreed to let you in to speak with Ellie about the disappearance, and Ellie told you that she followed a group of "old" people wearing "dresses" into a cave to the northeast of the city, in a cave that led under one of the mountains. She showed you there, and after leaving the Guardian with Ellie and Bethany outside, you entered the cave to find a group of ghouls. They were eating some sort of dead humanoid until they noticed you and attacked. You were able to hold them off, however, killing them. Now you look around to see the chamber empty of any additional creatures.
