## Summary of Last Session
---
 After successfully killing two of the sorcerers who hired assassins to kill Lady Lanthe and taking one into custody, you made your way back to [[the Great Palace]], but not before [[Troy Staari|Troy]] was pickpocketed by a woman claiming to be a fortune teller. The sorcerer was taken to the dungeon, and Lady Lanthe asked you to interrogate him, since her security and police forces are so thin. You agree, and after interrogating the sorcerer, you learn that he and many others who remain in the city are Imperial sympathizers who seek to keep Emperor [[Fenn]] in power. They view his ruthless and oppressive rule as instrumental to keeping the realm together. He explained that he and other sorcerers kidnapped citizens in the city to use as sacrifices to underworldly demons to call forth dark creatures to this realm in order to build a force to assist in their aim to keep Fenn in power. He explained that they sought to delegitimize Lady Lanthe because they know she is a member of a growing resistance to Emperor Fenn's rule. You brought this information to Lady Lanthe, who reveals to you that she is, indeed, the de facto leader of a resistance group that opposes Emperor Fenn and the injustices of his rule, and she explains that she received word via raven that a Resistance Council consisting of other opposition leaders in the realm will convene here in [[Courdney]] to discuss next steps. She thanked you for your assistance and invited you and [[Thrandunir]] to the Council meeting, and you are free to spend your next day as you wish until meeting with Christopher and [[Allana]], whom Thrandunir says will arrive in town tomorrow evening, at [[the Reliable Meadery]].
