## Summary of Last Session
---
You travelled to a potion shop where you met a woman named Madam Meech. She told you that her supply of Tumor Moss has run low because the sources have been drying up, but that she knew of a couple places where the ingredient might be found. She asked you to retrieve some so that she could continue her potion making.  You agreed and went to a lakeshore she directed you toward. You gathered some Tumor Moss, but afterwards, you realized that you scraped it off of a stone golem. After an intense battle, you brought down the golem and returned to Madam Meech.  While talking with her, [[Talia]] noticed that something was off and pulled down the woman's hood, which revealed that she was two Kobolds, Fey and Karen, sitting atop shoulders.  After reigning in [[Troy Staari|Troy]]'s initial reaction to attack, you calmed everyone down and asked if they would be willing to provide potions to the soldiers for the upcoming battle. They agreed.
