## Summary of Last Session
---
You engaged in an incredibly perilous battle with a Frost Giant that left Grum-Grum unconscious, but with the help of a woman who also fought against it, you were able to bring it down.  The woman reveals that she is Evalyn, and you note that you are Justicars sent to find her and explain your experience dissipating the magic of an orb held by Administrator Haera in Fort Gloomwind, she explains that the Emperor uses a form of dark magic that only Justicars were capableof fending off. She says that it seems the Emperor has been using this magic to call forth otherworldly creatures and undead monsters to secure his hold on the realm. She notes that, with some training, you will be able to assist in retaking the Throne.  You make your way to King Faeren, the King of Bysaes Tyl, and convince him to send 2,000 troops to aid in the defense of Twindolen.  He wishe that he could send more, but notes that he needs the troops he can to assist in the rebuilding of the town, which was ravaged by the attack from the undead creatures.  You made your way back to Twindolen to give a report to Queen Jamil, and Grum-Grum convinces her to send laborers to assist in the rebuilding of Bysaes Tyl in the hopes that King Faeren will send additional troops.  Queen Jamil informs you that the force from Courdney is on its way and will arrive in several days, and the force from the Capital of Theel will soon be departing. In the meantime, you have several days to regroup, so you went to a tavern to sleep for the evening.  You awake the next morning to find that your companions are still asleep. In a flash of independence, you decide to set out on your own for the time being.
