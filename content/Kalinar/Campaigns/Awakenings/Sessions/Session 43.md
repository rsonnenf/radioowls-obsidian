## Summary of Last Session
---
You left Glint's store and headed to the throne room to see if Falmore's story about him having met the queen holds up.  As you arrive, you see that it does.  You asked how you might be able to help as the preparations for the final attack on [[Theel]] finalize, and she notes that her associates have been ahving trouble getting information about the prisoner you captured from the battle.  You agree to help and make your way to the dungeon.  You see the man, who recall is named [[Valador]], in one of the cells.  You question him and try to figure out what his place is in all of this.  He explains, and Falmore recognizes him as, a high military leader in the Imperial Army.  He explains that he was not as high up in the ranks as he was, and he pretty reluctantly went along with the position because he believes, in the loss of [[Empress Hayden]], this Emperor is the only thing holding the realm together.  In the absence of an emperor, a power vacuum might engulf the realm, splintering it as it was before Empress Hayden took power.  As you try to figure out how Falmore is tangled up in all of this, he inadvertently teleports into the cell and begins to glow that deep, purple that you recognize to be the dark magic you need to eliminate  Falmore reveals that he doesn't quite know where the magic came from or why it is inside of him, but he explains that it has engulfed the capital.  Valador explains that his main goal is peace in the realm, and you reveal that [[Christopher]] is the rightful heir, Empress Hayden's secret son. He agrees to provide whatever information he can to Evalyn in order to help bring peace.  You report back to Evalyn, and Orsik reveals to her that Falmore, whom she has apparently not met yet, emanates the dark magic, which clearly disturbs her.  She vows to look into this after questioning Valador, and you head to the Whimsical Pig for a drink and to chat before ending the night and planning to meet at the Throne Room the next morning.
