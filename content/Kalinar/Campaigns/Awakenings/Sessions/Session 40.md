## Summary of Last Session
---
You continued making your way through Icewind Keep, which included finding an ale storeroom.  Also in the storeroom, though, was an odd jelly-like substance that attacked you. You easily dispatched it and continued making your way. Eventually, you came across a large, 30-foot door with a large, impenetrable lock on the door.  [[Talia]] entered a nearby room to find three sleeping kobolds. She successfully snuck by to find a bedroom in the back, where she found a key in a footlocker next to the bed.  She made her way out, and after a close call with a kobold sleepily placing his hand on her shoulder, she was able to sneak by with the help of Orsik.  You all closed the door behind you, opened the lock on the large door in front of you, and made your way through, where you found a massive frozen chamber.  Ice coats the floor, and in the center of the massive chamber sits a white dragon atop a large pile of gold.  In front of the dragon stands a kobold and a large wolf, undoubtedly the Vilyomah.  The dragon, kobold, and wolf are speaking to each other, and the wolf snarls at the kobold and dragon, while the dragon laughs.  You all plan to enter the chamber and attack the dragon, but just as you walk in, you hear the dragon laughing as it turns in your direction and says, "You do not belong here."
