## Summary of Last Session
---
After defeating [[Valador]] and his forces, you loot the bodies and find 1,000 GP, some half plate, and a scroll of modify memory.  You also found an orb similar to the one [[Administrator Haera|Administrator]] Haera had at [[Fort Gloomwind]]. You communicated through it, giving an order to retreat that confused some of the Imperial army.  Eventually, the generals on the ground determined that the orb had been compromised, and the orb shattered, taking the purplish haze along with it.  The Imperial army, though, didn't figure out the vulnerability before the Allied army took advantage of the confusion and began to overrun their enemies.  While this was occurring, [[Nailo]] travelled down the cliff and found that Valador was still alive. She carried him up, and you all interrogated him, learning little before taking him back to [[Twindolen]].  You brought him before the Queen, who ordered Valador imprisoned, and she informed you that your [[Justicars|Justicar]] training would begin the following day, and that the battle to retake the realm was close at hand.  You all took this in, left the throne room, and paid Carnell who gave you what little information he knew about the Gulf of Bilell before leaving.  Afterwards, you headed back to the Mischievous Goat, and over drinks, talked about your future.  Do you continue in this political tug of war you didn't ask to be a part of?  Or do you head out to the Gulf to rescue [[Rilad]] in support of your companion, Grum-Grum, who has become close with her?
