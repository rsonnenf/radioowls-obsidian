## Summary of Last Session
---
After defeating the massive snake and its minions, you freed two individuals who appeared to be close to being sacrificial victims. Once dispatching your enemies, you searched around and found a Dagger of Venom, which [[Talia]] took, before escorting the halfling and human out of the cave. They agreed to tell the mayor that you helped them and intended to look around the cave a bit more for any survivors. You searched the cave and found a pair of boots (Boots of Elvenkind), which [[Troy Staari|Troy]] took and put on, but none of you were sure what they were. After finding them, Talia spotted several more scaly creatures . You surprised them with an attack, and they fought back, putting up a good fight. Troy and [[Grum-Grum]] were taken out until [[Nailo]] was able to kill off the last of them. The party was able to stabilize Grum-Grum and Troy before their untimely deaths, but the party is now battered and injured, realizing that you're not all invincible. You catch your breath and once again look toward the darkness of the winding caves before you.
