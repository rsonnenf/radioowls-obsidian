## Summary of Last Session
---
 After a night of planning for the [[The Council of Resistance|Resistance Council]] meeting in the morning and getting to know Christopher and Allana a little more, you headed out in the morning toward [[the Great Palace]]. Along the way, you see a cart carrying Senator Erden, a member of the resistance council, making its way to the Palace. You try to make a scene to stop the cart and make contact with him so as to get on his good side, but you were unsuccessful, and the cart continued to the palace. Eventually, you made your way inside, where [[Thrandunir]] tasked you, as [[Justicars]], to convince the Council to assist Twindolin and save it from the military force being prepared in the Kalinar capital, [[Theel]], which is on its way to attack the city. After a lengthy meeting with the Council, you convinced them to reveal the Resistance and send aid to [[Twindolen]], and in addition to learning that Thrandunir is a Justicar himself who guided [[Empress Hayden]] during her reign, you learned that [[Christopher]] is the rightful heir to the throne and legitimate Emperor of Kalinar.

The Council concludes, and your orders are clear. You are to leave for Twindolin as soon as practicable. The Emperor's army is likely to be ready to be dispatched within the next few weeks and will likely reach Twindolin within the month--a month and a half if you're lucky.

You know you'll have to leave [[Courdney]] very soon, as it'll take you about two and a half weeks to reach Twindolin, but you have a couple days to tie up any loose ends you might have.
