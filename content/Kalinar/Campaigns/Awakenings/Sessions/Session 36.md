## Summary of Last Session
---
As you continued drinking in the tavern, Troy learned from the barkeep that the Stew cookbook he possesses is actually a very rare item that chefs of royalty used in service of their respective Kingdoms.  Apparently, each of the recipes contained some sort of magical ingredient, and the chefs would use these recipes to create stews that allowed their kings and queens to influence guests to the realms when it was convenient.  Troy takes this knowledge and thinks it over before retiring, and Nailo and Talia follow suit.  In the morning, you find that Grum-Grum has left the room.  He left behind 750 pieces of gold and three gold pieces that are blue and contain engravings of a handshake on one side and a carving of a dragon that looks like the Oathkeeper on the other. You notice that he took Oathkeeper with him, and in a note he left with you, he says that fighting a war isn't what he signed up for. His loyalty is to his friends, and you determine that he left to find and rescue Rilad in the Gulf of Bilell.  While you're sad to learn that he's gone, you power forward, knowing that you have training that must be undertaken.  You head to the palace, and on the way, you encounter a beggar trying to take money from your pockets.  You caught him, and he seemed very desperate for food, and you all take sympathy with him and ask offer him some gold if he leads you to wherever he intends to go that day. Surprisingly, he leads you to the Throne Room in the Royal Palace and transforms back into his original form, which is Evalyn, who was apparently testing you.  After informing the Queen of Grum-Grum's departure, you follow Evalyn to a training room where you meet her assistant Orsik, who is enthralled at the opportunity to meet more Justicars in person.  Evalyn quizzes you on your history and values. After finding you capable of representing the Justicar's values of Courage, respect, and humility, among others, she tests your magical ability.  Through three magical tests, Nailo and Talia are able to learn three potent spells, and Troy, two of them, that can counter the dark magic that you have encountered through your journey thus far, which you learned to be the source of Garrodan, the Dark One and Ruler of Orgodath, the Underrealm:  Sunbeam, Globe of Invulnerability, and Magic Circle.  Evalyn stands before you, having dubbed you official Jusitcars.  She turns to Orsik and says, "Orsik, if you would please go to my quarters. In a box under my bed are three capes. Would you please fetch them?"
