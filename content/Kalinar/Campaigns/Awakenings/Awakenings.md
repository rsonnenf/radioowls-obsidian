## Overview
A group of adventurers escapes a prison camp and learns that they are Justicars, guardians of the Realm of Kalinar, which were destroyed many years ago. They meet with a man who is the heir to the Realm and are tasked with gathering forces who will assist in retaking the throne from a tyrannical emperor.
