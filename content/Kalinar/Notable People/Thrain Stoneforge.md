

## Description
---
Armorer in the Justicar's armory in the Theel.

Thrain hails from a long line of skilled blacksmiths, and his family has served the Justicars for generations until they were wiped out. His family went into hiding until after Emeperor [[Christopher]] retook the throne an reestablished the Justicar Order. His dedication to perfection in every piece he forges has earned him the respect of the Justicar order and the reputation of being one of the finest armorers in the land.

The Justicars hold Forgemaster Thrain Stoneforge in high esteem. His armory is a vital resource for the order, ensuring that its members are well-equipped to face the challenges of upholding justice in the realm. Thrain's creations are often adorned with the sigils and symbols of the Justicar order, reflecting the unity and purpose of those who wear them.

## Appearance
---
Thrain is a stout and sturdy dwarf with a grizzled beard that reaches down to his chest. His calloused hands bear the marks of countless hours spent shaping metal at the forge. Dressed in traditional blacksmith attire, Thrain's piercing eyes reflect the wisdom and experience acquired over years of honing his craft.
## Personality
---
Thrain is known for his stoic demeanor and unwavering commitment to his work. He takes great pride in arming the Justicars, viewing his craft as a crucial contribution to the defense of justice and order in the realm. Despite his gruff exterior, Thrain is passionate about his creations and values the camaraderie he shares with the Justicars.
