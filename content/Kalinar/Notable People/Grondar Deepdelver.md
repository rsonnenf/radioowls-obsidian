## Description
---
Grondar Deepdelver was a dwarf who had dedicated his life to the art and science of mining. He was a master of extracting precious metals, gems, and minerals from the earth, and was skilled in managing the kingdom's vast network of mines and quarries. He was a respected and influential figure in the dwarven kingdom, as the wealth generated by the mines is critical to the kingdom's economy and security. Grondar accompanied [[The Works In Progress|the Works in Progress]] on their journey into [[The Mines|the Mines]]  to locate a portal to the Underrealm to free and save Gruumsh. Grondar was killed when, upon finding the portal and stepping through it and into a chamber containing a vast pool of lava, a devil, Titivulus, charmed him into throwing himself into the lava.

## Appearance
---
Grondar has rugged and weathered features, with calloused hands. He wears leather boots under his council robe, but you know that he would feel more comfortable wearing heavy work pants with mining tools on his belt.

## Personality
---
Grondar is a no-nonsense, pragmatic individual who is focused on results and efficiency. He is highly organized and detail-oriented, with a keen eye for identifying valuable mineral deposits and developing strategies to extract them. He also has a deep respect for the earth and the forces that shape it, and may see his work as a sacred duty to uncover the riches that lie hidden beneath the surface.
