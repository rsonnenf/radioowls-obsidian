Was Empress Hayden's most trusted Justicar during her reign thirty years ago. When [[Fenn]] overthrew her, Thrandunir took [[Christopher]], Hayden's son and the rightful Emperor, to safety. He raised [[Christopher]], and they worked as mercenaries. He has not told [[Christopher]] of his heritage for his safety. Not until allies can be gathered.

After an attack on their camp several months ago, [[Christopher]] was taken hostage. Thrandunir does not know where. He seeks to find him.

After several days of searching, Thrandunir learned that he could be at Fort Gloomwind. Thrandunir went to Courdney, seeking to obtain the help of Lady Lanthe, whose mother, Lady Vestra was a close ally to Empress Hayden. While attempting to gain access to the Lady, though, he learned of three Sorcerers who publicly challenge Lanthe's political authority, and privately, are planning a coup. He intends to thwart the plot.
