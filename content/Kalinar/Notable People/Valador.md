## Description
---
![[Pasted image 20240607084113.png|right|700]]A citizen of Theel and royal guard who has sworn his loyalty to the Emperor of Kalinar. Upon that oath, he is loyal to Emperor Bram, despite his belief that Bram's line is questionable.

- He was captain of Theel army before it was taken by Fen. 
- Fen placed him in charge of new army forcefully; he does not believe in Fen's ways, but feels that continued unification of the realms of Kalinar requires a single head maintaining order. While Fen is not ideal, it maintains the precarious peace among the realms.

During the Battle of Theel, Valador was thrown off of a cliff to his seeming death by Talia. However, the group located him in a cell, apparently captured after the battle.
