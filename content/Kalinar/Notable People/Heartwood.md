# Heartwood


An Aawakened tree in the Misty Forest. He cannot recall the druid who woke him from his deep slumber. He believes that "the kind fellow was an elderly halfling."

Heartwood is generally mild tempered, but he has little tolerance for those who would do harm to his forest. He fears that the recent Blight corruption of the forest will irreparably change and damage his home.

Because he dislikes those who would do harm to the forest, he is very antagonistic to dragons. Though rare now, they roamed the world centuries ago. Some had little regard for damage they caused to the forest.

If party asks Heartwood to help them concoct a potion of dragon majesty, he becomes angry.  

*"What business do the likes of you have with such a potion? Do you wish to sully this Forest even more than the Blight already has?"*
Heartwood will explain that, while dragons are relatively rare in Kalinar now, they once roamed throughout Kalinar. Some had little regard for the forest. Great battles between dragons and those who sought to steal from their vast hoards caused significant collateral damage to the Forest. 

*"All manner of dragon was guilty. Their acid poisoned our mothers and fathers. Their lightning struck down our brothers and sisters. Their flames burned our saplings alive. You aim to facilitate our suffering further?"*
