
## Description
---
A renowned blacksmith, Grunhilda is a fixture of the Market Quarter. She's known for her work creating beautiful and functional pieces of armor and weapons, and is always happy to chat with fellow artisans and admirers.

## Appearance
---
Grunhilda is a stout, muscular dwarf with rough, weathered skin. Her face is set with a determined expression and her piercing blue eyes seem to take in everything around her. She has a long, wild mane of black hair that falls to her waist and a thick, curly beard that is well-groomed and intricately braided. She is dressed in a heavy leather apron and a simple tunic, both stained with soot and the evidence of her work at the forge.

## Personality
---
Despite her rough appearance, Grunhilda has a surprisingly quick wit and a warm smile, which she uses to disarm even the most wary of patrons. She is a force to be reckoned with in both the forge and at the tavern, where she can be found regaling the patrons with tales of her adventures and the latest gossip from the city's market quarter.
