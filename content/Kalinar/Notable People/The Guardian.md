## Description
---
![[Dragon2.jpg|right|700]]Product of soulforging, a practice utilized by Dragonkin in Eastern Kalinar millennia ago. The mystical practice transformed everyday objects into sentient beings. However, this was typically reserved for individuals of high status and military leaders, and the practice was utilized less and less in the last several hundred years. Few soulforged objects remain. With permission of the soulforged object, they can be reforged into new, powerful objects. In the past, some have created soulforged weapons.
