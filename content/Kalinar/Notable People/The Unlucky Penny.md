## Overview
---
The Unlucky Penny was an old man who [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] encountered while stuck in a time loop near the settlement of [[Varnithel]]. A good aura emanated from him as he attempted to protect all those who came into the forest from a Red Dragon that has been drawn to the settlement because its inhabitants had been hoarding gold.
