
![[Christopher.png|right|500]]Christopher is the rightful Emperor to Kalinar. Sensing treachery from Counselor [[Fenn]], Christopher's mother, Empress Hayden, hid her pregnancy and, upon giving birth, handed him over to her most trusted Justicar, [[Thrandunir]], who watched over and raised him as his own. [[Thrandunir]] has not told Christopher of his royal lineage but has instilled in him a sense of justice and resistance to the traitors [[Fenn]] and Bram. Christopher believes his immediate family was a farming family killed by [[Fenn]]'s soldiers and that [[Thrandunir]] was a benevolent person who found and decided to care for him. Christopher was raised by [[Thrandunir]], and the two work together as mercenaries.

A band of reawakened [[Justicars]] assisted Christopher in rediscovering his royal roots and retaking the Throne of Kalinar.
