## Description
---
![[Pasted image 20240714121854.jpg|right|500]]A mithril dragon encountered by [[The Works In Progress|the Works In Progress]] while venturing through [[The Mines|the Mines]]. Aurimis is unexpectedly arrogant yet meek. He is upset that his father is attempting to usher him out of the lair to find his own and make a life for himself. Aurimis agrees to assist the Works In Progress make their way into the Underrealm with the understanding that he will be able to keep the lair for himself once the Underrealm creatures are vacated.

## Appearance
---
His scales shimmer with a brilliant silver hue, reflecting ambient light in a mesmerizing display. Although still growing into his massive form, Aurimis carries himself with an air of confidence and grandeur that befits his draconic heritage.

With an endearing mix of arrogance and a touch of innocence, Aurimis exudes a charismatic charm that often elicits both exasperation and fondness from those who encounter him.


## Personality
---
Despite his impressive stature, Aurimis sometimes stumbles through his own enthusiasm, making him endearingly clumsy and prone to comedic mishaps. His bumbling attempts to demonstrate his prowess often result in inadvertently knocking things over or getting tangled in his own wings, earning him a reputation as a lovable doofus among his kin and companions.

## Notes
---
Aurimis's father, Argentius the Magnificent, is attempting to usher Aurimis out of the family lair to find one for himself.
