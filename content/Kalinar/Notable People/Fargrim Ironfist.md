## Description
---
Fargrim Ironfist is the King of [[Den Fatum]]. He took the throne 10 years ago following the death of his father, Ardonin Ironfist. Keeping in line with Den Fatum's isolationism, he is untrustworthy of outsiders. He has heard of the growing threat of the Blight, but he has avoided any significant engagement. He fears that allying his city with the other realms may eventually result in the taking advantage of his city and its resources once again, so he is reluctant to join the fray, hoping that the storms in the [[The Forgotten Sands]] will keep his city and people hidden.

Ultimately, King Ironfist agreed to send his army to assist the allied armies led by Emperor [[Christopher]] to fight back against the Blight.
