A bounty hunter. He and his partner, Liris, travel throughout Kalinar taking contracts. They had accepted a contract to track down [[Red Thorn]] members and gain what information they could about its head so that it could be rooted out. They captured an Orc [[Red Thorn]] member and turned him over to the authorities, who placed him on display in the town square as a warning to other members. One evening, during the course of their separate information gathering sessions, Liris returned to their room in [[Bilell]] and told Deekek that she found an odd structure out on the beach, near a cliff. It was glowing and guarded by creatures she could not quite make out. Deekek instructed her to keep her motivations toward the contracts. They were making progress in finding out more about the [[Red Thorn]], including its hideout in Bilell, and its leadership. Unfortunately, when he awoke the next morning, Liris was gone. He believed she went to go get a closer look at the structure. He went looking for it and found it, but saw that it was heavily guarded, as Liris said. He could not quite make out what the creatures were, as he did not want to get too close and place himself in danger. He left and came back, expecting Liris to return, but she had not. Believing she had been captured, Deekek saved the party in an effort to ask them to find Liris. They accepted and found a portal to the Underrealm, where they saved Liris. Upon returning to Deekek, Deekek gave the party information about the [[Red Thorn]] and its leader. He noted that information about Bahamut might be obtained from an information broker, [[Lovel]], in Hornsmeet.
