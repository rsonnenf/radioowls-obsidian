## Description
---
Eldrid is a [[Justicars|Justicar]] sent by Emperor Christopher in order to sway King [[Fargrim Ironfist]] of [[Den Fatum]] to aid against the coming Blight.

Eldrid grew up in a small village on the outskirts of a kingdom. His father was a blacksmith who taught him the ways of the forge, but Eldrid always felt a calling to something more. He left his village as a young man to join the army and quickly rose through the ranks due to his skill in battle. After several years of service, he was asked to join the effort in rebuilding the Order of the [[Justicars]]. Eldrid was tasked with convincing  the leader of Den Fatum to join Theel in fighting against the Blight. During this quest, he began assisting The Works In Progress in their efforts to eliminate a Blighted portal. Eldrid was killed as the group escaped the volatile portal, having kick started it's destruction. Eldrid sacrificed himself to allow the group to escape.

## Appearance
---
Eldrid is a tall and muscular human male with a rugged appearance. He has short, dark hair and a thick beard. He wears well-worn armor and carries a sword at his side.

## Personality
---
Eldrid is a proud and honorable warrior who values loyalty and bravery above all else. He is fiercely protective of those he cares about and is willing to put himself in harm's way to ensure their safety.
