## Notes
---

- Part of a group of about 30 that had been imprisoned at Fort Gloomwind. They were able to escape several weeks ago and have a camp nearby.
- Was instructed by Allana to scout the perimeter—heard rumors of Gloomwind Guards searching the area.
- Can describe Gloomwind

- It is a prison camp masquerading as a city for at least 20 years. It is self-contained and self-sustainable. Has markets, entertainment, medical facilities. It functions like a city, but those citizens are held against their will and prohibited from leaving.
- Under the control and watch of the cruel [Administrator Haera](onenote:#Administrator%20Haera&section-id={670b15c5-4c10-4062-a332-2e9f2beadeef}&page-id={778b08a6-b12c-42cb-9178-52f11bb9cbe7}&end).

- Meets any violation of her laws with severe punishment. Some prisoners have disappeared without being seen again.
- A curfew is in place.

- Not sure why they were all imprisoned there, but from what his group has gathered, they believe that someone thinks the prisoners pose some sort of threat. Don't know how or why. No common traits among prisoners that they can tell.

- He knows of Kalendel's history: The traitor, Counselor Fenn overthrew Empress Hayden and the Justicars. Now his son, Bram is continuing oppression.

- Does not know who the Justicars are exactly. Some sort of royal guard.

- Will not explain why they have not left the province. Allana is the one to explain.
