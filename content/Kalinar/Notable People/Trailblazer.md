## Description
---
Trailblazer is a treant who cannot recall his name. He informs the group that the Forell Forest is cursed and offers to lead them through it if they agree to help him raze the part of the forest that has failed to flourish and assist in regrowing it. However, Trailblazer becomes visibly offended when [[Grum-Grum]] asks him to assist [[Nailo]] and [[Talia]], who fell into a ravine while attempting to cross it. Trailblazer states that assisting those who have failed is not "the will of the forest." Despite this, Trailblazer continues to lead the group through the forest until they reach a particularly dense portion that he believes must be destroyed to permit new growth.

Trailblazer attacked the Awakened after they refused to burn down a portion of the forest he did not feel was "flourishing." The wakened defended themselves and killed Trailblazer. They then learned that he was somehow bewitched by some sort of curse that permeated the forest.
