Human Male

The leader of a troupe dispatched to attack Varnithal in the [[Dire Clan]]'s campaign to gain influence in the region and join the [[Red Thorn]] Syndicate. He was captured by [[The Works In Progress]] during the attack and delerious after significant injury. After questioning--which gave the Works in Progress a general direction of the Dire Clan's headquarters, he was healed, and his mind was altered. He was turned over to [[Ilga]] to be his apprentice.
