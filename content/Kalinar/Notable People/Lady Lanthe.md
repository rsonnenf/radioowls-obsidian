## Overview
---
Lady Lanthe is the current ruler of Courdney, a city in Kalinar. Her rule has faced significant challenges due to rising opposition from a faction of sorcerers.

## Personality
---
Lady Lanthe is known for her stern yet fair leadership. She focuses on maintaining order, fostering economic growth, and ensuring citizen welfare. She is both a skilled diplomat and strategist.
