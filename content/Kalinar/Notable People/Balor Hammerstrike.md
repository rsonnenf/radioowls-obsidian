
## Description
---
The owner of the Anvil and Ale tavern. 

## Appearance
---
Balor stands about 4 feet tall and has blue eyes and a broad, muscular build, with a thick beard and long hair the color of burnished copper. His face is weathered and lined with the deep creases of a lifetime spent working with his hands. He wears a simple leather apron over his sturdy tunic and breeches, and his arms are adorned with bracelets and bangles of silver and gold, marking him as a successful businessman. He is often found smiling and has a contagious grin that's quick to spread across his face.

## Personality
---
Despite his rough exterior, Balor is a kind-hearted soul and is well-loved by his patrons. When he's not tending bar, he can often be found chatting and laughing with his customers or regaling them with tales from his youth. Overall, Balor is the epitome of a warm and welcoming tavern-keeper, and his Anvil and Ale is a place where anyone can feel at home.
