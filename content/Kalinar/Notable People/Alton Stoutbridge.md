Male Dwarf

An organizer for the Festival of Lights in [[Varnithel]]. He hired [[Grubby]] and [[Fax Faertrin|Fax]] to perform their show. After their performance, [[the Works in Progress]] defended the town from an attack by a troupe of [[Dire Clan]] members. Before heading to [[Bilell]], the Works in Progress got the gold they were *supposed* to receive from Alton from [[Alina Fox]]. The group then met with Alton, who did not know that they had already been paid, and paid them a second share.
