## Backstory 
___

Was taken in at a young age by a bugbear named [[Zif]]. He taught me the ways of the druid. He  showed me that there is good in people and to do your best to help others and nature. Being a bugbear he was used to not being looked at in the best light but would endure the awful things that were said to him.... until a point. I learned that do no unnecessary harm, but take no shit. He taught me to always be curious which will get me into trouble. I also have 0 clue on any form of social etiquette or social how tos... I was raised by a hermit bugbear go figure. I started wandering when Zif who was already old when he took me in passed away.
