Zos experienced a vision. She was enveloped in gray mist that began to part. As it did so, the mist turned to tree bark. From the center of the parting bark emerged a moose or deer with eyes that, while initially, were rolling incessantly and wildly, focused on Zos. The deer opened its mouth, and the chirping of thousands of birds erupted before coalescing into a single voice that said, “You won’t forsake me, will you?” 

The Deer/Moose is a sigil for a clan in the Misty Forest? They are holding Zif captive?
