Taught how to live off the land. They stayed away from people. Hard to go to school, learning, etc. He was a good person, even though he was a bugbear. One day, he went out into town and never came back. Zos started going out to look for him. He found Bo. Who was lost in the forest. She helped because she does no harm. She made it to town in Karmir. Met [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]], etc.

Zif has been captured by the [[The T'durn]]. Aralus kept Zif alive after learning of his knowledge of the woods. After capturing Zif, the T'durn have placed him under a spell to control him and, given his deep knowledge of the woods, placed him in command of one of their raiding units to attack nearby towns. Zif's intimate knowledge gave the T'durn strategic advantages when attacking from the woods.
