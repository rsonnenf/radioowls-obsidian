After drinking the Blighted essence. Bo has mirror image spell, creating three illusory duplicates. Blight splits his personality into two worse versions of himself and two better versions of himself.

Two are illusory, and two are corporeal. Party decides what to do with them. One could be killed/become NPC, wouldn't want to keep both in party. Would present other people with options. Opportunity to explore other side of his character (if good) or be extra evil. 

Drinking it, causes

[[RPG Rules/5e SRD/spells/Darkness]] and [[RPG Rules/5e SRD/spells/Mirror Image]]. Bo casts both. Two Bos standing before party. Split personalities. One red eye transmitted to one of them. 

One of them flees.

The Light Bo remains after the party killed the Dark Bo.
