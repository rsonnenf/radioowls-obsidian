## Backstory 
___
The memory I hold dear to my heart is the bedtime story my mother would tell me growing up....

My mother (human) who was a kind and compassionate soul, was foraging further out among the outskirts of town one day when she found my father an Orc, who was unconscious and bloody in the Pandoran forest. She paced for an hour contemplating what to do, but she couldn't just leave him alone to possibly die even if he could kill her when or if he woke up. It wasn't in her nature. She couldn't take him back to the village, so she found a way to drag him into a nearby cave. She took care of his wounds and nursed him back to health over weeks of time. He was angry and temperamental until he realized she wasn't going away. She was so patient that he warmed up to her gentle heart over time and fell in love with her. They couldn't go back to either of their families now so they created their own home, traveling through forests until finding an abandoned bermed home and raising myself in it. I grew up with a weasel named Felix that my mother gave to me. My mother would go to towns and bring me back books and small gifts. I was a happy and confident but quiet child.

I was 12 when my parents disappeared. ==They went out to hunt and never returned home.== They were both loving parents but I never knew what happened to them. I am 24 now and the only memories I have left are the stories they shared. The story my father told me of Bahgtru, the god of strength. My father worshipped him. He taught and trained me how to be merciless, quick and strong. My mother taught me empathy when needed but to be careful when trusting others. She told me many stories of the evil in man. I wear this necklace she gave to me passed down from many generations in my family that has to do with our heritage. It burns bright fire red at times. I'm not fully sure why. I never got the chance to learn any of those stories about my mothers heritage. My father was busy teaching me fighting skills that his father once taught him. He had fought in many battles in his lifetime and was a very powerful Orc.

Ever since they left I grew on my own in the woods with Felix hoping one day they would return. Until I came across a village that had all kinds of creatures I had never seen before. I would study them from a distance until one day I met one of these creatures from the village. He was oddly friendly and I learned to trust him. From there, I learned many different jobs in the town. I still train daily like my father taught me and enjoy hanging out at the local library alone reading more than anything though. I have this burning feeling inside that I will leave this town someday because I am destined for greatness...and I have this hope that one day I will find my parents if they are still alive.

## Style and Personality
___
Female. 6ft. 1in. in height and 180lbs in weight. Muscular and strong. Pale green skin, gold eyes with silvery freckles on face.
Metallic blue and purple pixi haircut with braids representing her Orc heritage. Wears armor, a sword and bow when out on adventures in the forest or further away from town. In town she wears very comfortable and chic clothes. She tends to be a
minimalist. Carries a leather purse tied with intricate knots, contains 4d6 bear's teeth, each dipped in molten gold. Each tooth is worth 1gp...perhaps more to an Orc or collector. Inside the purse she always carries a book and her clever weasel, Felix. He hides out and takes many naps inside along for the ride. He hops out at times to grab things for Shayna or when he's feeling mischievous. He can change his color to match their surroundings when he's sneaking around or feels threatened/scared. Shayna wears gold jewelry and a necklace her mother passed down to her. She has a large tattoo on her back of Bahgtru in memory of her father. She also follows the deity, Bahgtru.

Shayna isn't the most social but she gets her point across in conversation. She has a strong presence and sometimes comes off as intimidating. She has a great sense of humor/wit and can warm up to people but it takes time. She is a strong and valiant fighter. She loves learning anything and everything. She can be helpful to others but also loves her freedom. She loves showing off her strength. She’s blunt where others use hints or lies. She would prefer doing something to talking about it.

## Shayna's Quest 
___
Unbeknownst to [[The Works In Progress]], Grummsh is actually held captive along with his son, Bahgtru, whom Shayna worships, as did her parents. Shayna's father was an incredibly proficient martial artist, and he was so excellent in his artistry that he caught the attention of Bahgtru, who taught him more techniques. He began teaching Shayna what he learned from Bahgtru. Though Bahgtru did not consider Shayna's father a friend, as he was well below Bahgtru's power, he did develop some respect for Shayna's father. On a single occasion during a sparring match, Shayna's father bested Bahgtru, drawing blood. Bahgtru solidified that blood into a red pendant and gave it to Shayna's father as a reminder of his impossible accomplishment.

Ilneval, Gruumsh's second war lieutenant (alongside Bahgtru) secretly resented Bahgtru, though for a long time, he did no more than attempt to undermine Bahgtru. Soon, though, Ilneval was convinced that, in order to obtain his own spot in the pantheon, he would need to eliminate Bahgtru and Gruumsh. Ilneval turned on them and has been fighting against them ever since. He had also been sweeping through Kalinar, eliminating all those who refused to denounce Bahgtru and worship him. This was the subject of [[Shayna's Third Vision]]. Ilneval has since allied himself with [[the Blight]]. He set a trap to capture Gruumsh and Bahgtru for the Blight, which was successful. Despite the Blight's efforts to contain the two, Gruumsh's anger has been causing the barrier between the Underrealm and the Overrealm, which had been hiding them, to come close to breaking. Between the seams, Ilneval was able to reach out to Shayna's father, the only mortal he believed could stand a chance to saving them. He relayed that Shayna, whom he had taught many of the techniques he learned from Bahgtru, could help. (Why can't Shayna's parents help?)
