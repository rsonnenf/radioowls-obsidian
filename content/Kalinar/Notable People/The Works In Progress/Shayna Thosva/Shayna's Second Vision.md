Found herself standing in dark fog. A disembodied voice told her, “If the blind one is made to see, there will be ruin.” The dark fog turned crimson red, and the ground beneath her gave way so that she was floating in nothingness. Despite initially feeling as if she wanted to remain in the peaceful, calm silence,  she decided that she needed to find her way out. She had difficulty, and her heart  began beating faster and faster until eventually, just as it felt as if her heart was about to explode, she returned to consciousness.
