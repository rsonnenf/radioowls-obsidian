[[Shayna Thosva|Shayna]] wakes up in her childhood home while her parents are making breakfast. As they do so, a mysterious force attacks the town. While Shayna hides, her parents draw elegant swords. Shayna's mother's sword has the pendant Shayna now wears around her neck on the pommel. They leave to repel the attackers. One of the attackers bursts through the front door. It is purple with glowing red eyes. It tells Shayna, "Your line has betrayed me, little one."

Shayna's mother reenters the house and stabs at the creature as it raises its hand toward Shayna. The ethereal nature of the creature dissipates, revealing what appears to be just a plain bearded orc, now dead. He wore robes and had an odd symbol etched on his forehead. A neighbor then comes through the door. "They are overrunning the town!" Shayna recognizes the neighbor, a human family friend named Orthil. Shayna has never seen him like this before. A sword of his own in hand, while his other arm appears purplish black, as if it is decaying before Shayna's eyes. 

Shayna's mother approaches, apologetic about having left and placed her in danger.  More creatures are seen outside the house. Are they just like this one was? A man in some sort of magical disguise? As you look closer, you can see that the creatures seem to be acting in an unnatural synchronization. Not necessarily moving at the same time, but with such precision and efficiency as they attack your home town that it seems as if they are controlled as one or share a single consciousness. 

Shayna's father returns to the home, looking exhausted and--for the first time that Shayna can remember--afraid.

They determine that they have to leave behind Shayna with Orthil, who will take her to safety in the forest, while they go and hunt the cultists and aid Bahgtru. After saying their goodbyes, Orthil takes Shayna to the forest, where they encounter cultists who kill Orthil. Shayna goes out on her own

What does this represent?
After Ilneval betrayed Bahgtru and Gruumsh, he began amassing a group of cultists who worshiped and would assist him in becoming a deity. The cultists would raid villages who would kill anyone who did not bow to Ilneval. This is what the creatures were that attacked Shayna's town that day. They regularly perform dark rituals to help make Ilneval grow stronger and to augment their own abilities when raiding. Though Shayna's parents left to stop this, due to their loyalty to Bahgtru, the cult continues to grow. Apparently they were not successful, and Shayna still does not know of their fate. This vison suggests that Shayna must heed Bahgtru and assist him by eliminating the cult and killing Ilneval to ensure that he does not join the Blight and become a deity.
