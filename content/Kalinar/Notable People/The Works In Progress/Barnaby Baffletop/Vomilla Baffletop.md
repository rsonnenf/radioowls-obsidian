A rising Artificer who has published many books. In doing so, she was away from [[Barnaby Baffletop|Barnaby]] for much of his youth. She discovered several breakthroughs in the realm of artificing, including novel ways to infuse constructs with magic. What she believed would be her crowning breakthrough was a discovery to be able to fuse souls with constructs. 

Vomilla left Barnaby and Barnaby's Father many years ago, while Barnaby was too young to remember much, to pursue her experiments, though she regretted doing so.

She sought to write a book on the process, conducting experiments to test her theory. In doing so, she attempted to imbue a construct with an essence of a shadow, but the experiment went horribly wrong. The construct injured many of her team before it escaped her laboratory. Once out in the town, the construct caused untold destruction, leading to the death of an individual before it was destroyed by guards. Horrified of what she witnessed, she turned herself in to the authorities, and after a trial, she was sentenced to X years in prison. She recently was released, but the son of the individual the construct killed is looking for revenge. He hopes to draw out Barnaby so that he can take him away from Barnaby's mother, like Barnaby's mother took his father from him.
