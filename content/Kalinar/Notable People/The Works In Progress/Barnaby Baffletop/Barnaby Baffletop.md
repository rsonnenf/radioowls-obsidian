## Description
---
![[Barnaby 2.jpg|500|right]]Barnaby grew up in mountains near [[Courdney]]. Went to school. Grew up there. Mom left dad 25 years ago. (Barnaby is 50, relatively young) She was a gifted artificer. Like most talented people, she left town, and dad had family so wouldn't go with. Barnaby grew up to be an artificer as well. Vomila Baffletop was [[Vomilla Baffletop|mother]]'s name. She left and went to Hove. Lived a successful life. And so did Barnaby. Working for Artificer's Guild. He "just wants that." Before they are best friends, they are co-workers with a job. It is his mission to finish contract right now. Respects his coworkers right now, but he's tired of putting it off.
