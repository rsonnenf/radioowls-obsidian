## Description
---
Dignitary and Prime Minister of Rhuie. He is a vampire who seeks to obtain influence in Western Kalinar. He sent another vampire, Fabien, to scout that region. [[The Works In Progress]] encountered Ortofola at a gala in Hornsmeet after he attempted to kill [[Lovel]], an information broker, for revealing to one of his clients that Ortofola was a vampire. The works in progress defendant against the undead hordes Ortofola summoned, but Ortofola escaped.

Later, Ortofola made an appearance in [[Borbir]].
