Leader of the Artificer's Guild in [[Bilell]]. Assisted [[the Works in Progress]] with travelin to Hornsmeet.

Began working on airship as a hobby several years ago. Once he discovered how to harness magic as a levitation fuel, he began to dream of building something that could take people across the continent. He enlisted the help of artificers and those looking for work to build the actual ship.

Feril recently broke up with his fiancé, Vel. He let his work become more important than his relationship. Running the Guild, Keeping up with contracts. Working on the airship on the side didn't help. He thought about it, and he thinks things slipped away because he was afraid of the future and didn't know how to deal with it.

Feril is a perfectionist.

He cares for his friends and will do anything for them.

Feril likes to party - he will break out a keg of beer he brought on board as a celebration. He will place the mug next to the steering wheel.

Of the far north and the Frozen Pinewood, he knows that it is a relatively sparsely populated region. Small settlements here and there.
