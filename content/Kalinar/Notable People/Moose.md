## Description
---
Moose wasn't always a high-ranking member of the [[Red Thorn]]. He grew up in a scraggly goblin tribe on the outskirts of [[Borbir]], notorious for petty theft and causing mischief. Moose, however, was different. He wasn't content with stealing trinkets; he craved power and influence. He discovered his knack for deception early on, weaving tales that would leave even the most seasoned goblin elder bewildered. He used this skill to manipulate his tribe, becoming their unofficial leader despite his small stature.

One night, a Red Thorn scouting party raided Moose's tribe. Most goblins scattered, but Moose, ever the opportunist, saw a chance for advancement. He approached the leader, Rilad, a sleek elf with eyes like ice, and offered his services. Impressed by his audacity and cunning, [[Rilad]] saw potential in the small goblin. Moose pledged his loyalty to the Red Thorn, vowing to use his talents for the syndicate.

Moose quickly rose through the ranks, using his silver tongue and knack for deception to pull off elaborate heists and cons. He earned the nickname "Moose the Mouth" for his ability to talk his way out of any situation. Now, a trusted lieutenant under Rilad, Moose finds himself tasked with a mysterious mission at the grand bank of Borbir. He isn't sure what Rilad has planned, but one thing's for certain – Moose wouldn't have it any other way. 

## Appearance
---
Moose stands 3 feet tall. His wiry frame is surprisingly strong, honed from years of navigating tight tunnels and evading capture. His oversized ears swivel constantly, catching every sound, and his large, emerald eyes glint with a mischievous intelligence. Despite his stature, Moose carries himself with a swagger that belies his size. His clothes are a mismatched collection of finery – a silk vest several sizes too large over worn leather armor, a plumed captain's hat perched precariously on his head, and a rapier strapped to his back that seems almost as long as he is tall. Around his neck hangs a silver charm in the shape of a red thorn, the symbol of the Red Thorn Crime Syndicate.
## Personality
---
Moose is fiercely loyal to the [[Red Thorn]] Syndicate. It has given him opportunities that he would not have had otherwise. As a result, he trusts [[Rilad]] by default. That being said, it is his dream to lead the [[Red Thorn]], and he is not above doing so by any means necessary.
