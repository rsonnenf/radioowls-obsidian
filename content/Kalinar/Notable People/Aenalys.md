*A young woman reclines on the cushions, a hookah hose held delicately in one hand. Eyes closed, she sings to herself. Her voice is soft and inviting. She doesn't move when she hears you approach, but slowly opens her eyes with a lazy smile.*

Aenalys is a professional actor who belonged to a troupe that toured the Kalinarian realm. Performing classic works of literature to cheering audience and enjoying good wages amounted to a decent life when things were going well. However, she desired a more dependable audience and so picked up the trade of a courtesan.
