A council of trusted military officers, legal authorities, adjudicators, lorekeepers, medical professionals, political advisors, and religious leaders convened during the reign of King Dalran. The Council's influence and membership increased through her reign and despite Emperor [[Fenn]]'s disappointing reign. The Council expressed criticisms with Emperor Bram's tyrannical rule, and Bram disbanded the Council and eliminated its membership.
