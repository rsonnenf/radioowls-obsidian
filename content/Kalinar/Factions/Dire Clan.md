A gang that has been attempting to obtain influence in western Kalinar since Emperor [[Christopher]] took the throne. They seek to join the [[Red Thorn]] Syndicate, which is the preeminent criminal organization in Western Kalinar.

The Dire Clan's Leader, Egard Elderberry, was killed by [[The Works In Progress]] when they accepted a contract to stop the gang from attacking Varnithel.
