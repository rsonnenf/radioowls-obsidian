## Description
---
A sect of the [[Red Thorn]] Syndicate. They are a more violent sect. [[Rilad]] tended to shy away from the Crimson Fangs given their violent nature, but they are growing more powerful. They have been raiding small villages in the southern Misty Forest searching for information about a Wishing Well.

## Notes
---
The Crimson Fangs are led by [[Garrick the Crimson Claw]], Rilad's counterpart in this sect.

The group's hideaway is called Timberfang.
