## Overview
---
The War of Ash was a brutal five-year conflict that fractured the once-unified Kalinar fought between then-Empress Hayden and loyalists of [[Fenn]].

## **Seeds of Discord**
---
Fenn's ambition for the throne festered under Empress Hayden's rule. His discontent with the limitations of the Counselor position likely fueled his desire to usurp power.

## **A Coup Ignited**
---
Fenn's scheming culminated in a calculated overthrow of Empress Hayden. This treacherous act plunged Kalinar into a bloody civil war.

## **Loyalists vs. Usurper**
---
The War of Ash pitted loyalists to the rightful heir, Empress Hayden, against Fenn's supporters. The conflict resulted in a fracturing of the Kalinarian Empire.

## **A Legacy of Ash**
---
The War of Ash left an indelible mark on Kalinar. Beyond the physical devastation, it ushered in an era of fear and repression under Fenn's iron fist rule. The embers of rebellion burning across the Empire stand as a testament to the enduring cost of the War of Ash.
