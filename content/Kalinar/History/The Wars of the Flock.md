## Overview
___

The Wars of the Flock, a period of unknown duration ending roughly 350 years ago, marked a turning point in Kalinarian history. Prior to this era, Kalinar was a fractured region plagued by warring, oppressive nation-states.

## Unification
___
King [[Emperor Dalran|Dalran]] of Edral, recognizing the need for change, partnered with Lord [[Brondur]] of Kvarth to rally discontented factions. Together, they formed the "Flock," a diverse alliance uniting various kingdoms and freedom fighters. The pivotal role of the Justicars, a multi-faceted organization offering military leadership, legal expertise, historical knowledge, and societal support, proved invaluable.

## Outcomes
___
Through the Flock's efforts, tyrannical rulers across Kalinar were overthrown. A unified Kalinarian Empire emerged, led by Emperor Dalran and Counselor Brondur. The [[Justicars]] became the guardians of the new order.
