The current age began approximately 350 years ago at the conclusion of the Wars of the Flock, which unified Kalinar's several nations, many of which oppressed their subjects and were hostile among one another. King Dalran of Edral approached his friend, Lord Brondur of neighbouring Kvarth, about the prospect of garnering support to overthrow the tyrants of the other territories and unifying Kalinar under a centralized, just ruler. Brondur agreed, and Dalran convened the [[Justicars]], a mystical order of trusted military officers, legal authorities, adjudicators, lorekeepers, medical professionals, political advisors, and religious leaders who fought alongside and advised him, and together, Dalran, Brondur, and the [[Justicars]] unified all of Kalinar under a single empire led by the newly crowned Emperor Dalran, advised by the appointed Counselor Brondur, and protected by the [[Justicars]]. The positions of Emperor and Counselor were deemed hereditary. Kalinar prospered and was peaceful for generations.

Thirty years ago, however, [[Brondur]]'s heir, [[Fenn]], became dissatisfied with his advisory position as Counselor and sought to become Emperor. He arranged a coup and overthrew then-[[Empress Hayden]], seizing power for himself and purging the [[Justicars]] during the course of the 5-year War of Ash between loyalists to Empress Hayden and [[Fenn]]'s supporters. Upon seizing control, [[Fenn]] executed Hayden and implemented an oppressive rule to keep dissidents in check.  Now, a small band of resistance grows throughout the realm seeking to oppose Bram's tyrannical rule.
