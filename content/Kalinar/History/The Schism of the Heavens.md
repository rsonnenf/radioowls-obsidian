# The Schism of the Heavens
___
The event that marked the official breakaway of [[the Blight]] from the pantheon of remaining gods was known as the Schism of the Heavens. It was a brutal and bloody conflict that lasted for years and saw rebelling gods turn on their former allies in a bid for power and control.

The Schism began when the leader of the rebelling gods, a powerful deity of darkness and destruction, declared their intention to seize control of the mortal realm and rule it as their own. The existing gods were shocked and horrified by this declaration, and initially tried to reason with the rebelling deity. However, their attempts at diplomacy were met with violence and aggression, and the Schism was soon raging across the celestial planes.

The battle was intense and devastating, with the rebelling gods, who deemed theselves the Blight, using their powers to unleash destruction upon the world and their former allies. The skies turned red with the fires of war, and the ground shook with the footsteps of the gods as they clashed in battle. Many deities were lost on both sides, and the very fabric of reality was torn asunder by the sheer force of the conflict.

In the end, the remaining pantheon subdued the Blight, which went into hiding and bided their time, waiting for an opportunity to take the Overrealm once more.
