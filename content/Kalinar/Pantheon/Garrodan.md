## Description
---
Garrodan was banished to Ogrodath, the Underrealm, by his sister Gadiel, the Lady of Light, after a failed plot to take control of Eternity, ruling over Eonor (the Overrealm) and Reality. Garrodan has become the Lord of Ogrodath, and he has allied with Emperor Bram, promising him power and fortune. Garrodan aims to take and rule over Reality before directing the forces of his amassed realms against Eonor.
