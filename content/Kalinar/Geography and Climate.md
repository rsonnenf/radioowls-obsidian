Kalinar is a  geographically diverse realm, spanning from tropical coastal regions to frozen glacial plains. Moving westward from the Arondale Sea, forests and grasslands are dominant on the continent, covering most of the central region. To the North, the climate becomes more frigid as the environment shifts to snow-blanketed grounds, and a frozen tundra is found in the North-western region. By contrast, deserts cover the Southern regions.
