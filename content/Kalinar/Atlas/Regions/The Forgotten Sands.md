# The Forgotten Sands

## Description
---
The Forgotten Sands is a desolate desert region in southern Kalinar. It is sparsely populated, given its hostile environment, which itself is attributable not only to its arid climate, but also intense storms that roll through the region. Once a vibrant, green place, the Forgotten Sands is home to little wildlife and vegetation and even fewer humanoids, save those who live in the two largest cities in the region, [[Den Fatum]] and Solitude. Some nomadic peoples, known as stormwalkers, traverse the desert, but most travel into this region is done so from the Nautimore Ocean to the north.

## Encounters
___
| Name                                              | Description                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Sandstorm Over The Forgotten Sands\|Sandstorm]] | The party is engulfed by a sudden sandstorm. Beyond the sandstorm itself, creatures live in the storm and see this as an opportunity to strike. |
| Lost Caravan                                      | The party sees a lost group of merchants traveling through the desert                                                                           |
| [[Desert Sphinx]]                                        | The party trespasses on a sphynx's territory and must solve three riddles                                                                                                                                                 |


1.  Sandstorm: The sandstorm is a massive and unpredictable weather event that suddenly engulfs the party's airship. Strong winds make flying difficult and can even damage the airship. The sand and dust make it hard to see and the party must navigate by instruments alone. The sandstorm can also be home to dangerous creatures, such as sand wraiths or sand scorpions, who take advantage of the chaos to attack. The party must use their wits and skills to survive the storm and reach their destination.
    
2.  Lost Caravan: The lost caravan is a group of merchants traveling through the desert to reach a distant city. The merchants are lost, running low on supplies, and in desperate need of help. The party may encounter the caravan while flying over the desert, or they may come across their camp while searching for a place to make an emergency landing. The merchants are seeking a guide to take them to the nearest city, and they offer to pay the party for their services. The journey to the city is filled with danger and adventure, as the party must navigate through the desert, fight off hostile creatures, and avoid treacherous sand dunes. The party must also be careful, as the merchants may have valuable goods that attract the attention of bandits or sky pirates.
