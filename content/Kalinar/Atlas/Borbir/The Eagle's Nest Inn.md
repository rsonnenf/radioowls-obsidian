## Description
---

Perched precariously on the very edge of Borbir's cliffs, the Eagle's Nest Inn offers patrons unparalleled views of the churning Nautimore Ocean far below. Built from the same pale stone as the rest of the city, the inn's exterior is weathered and worn by the constant sea winds, giving it a rugged charm. Inside, the atmosphere is warm and inviting, a stark contrast to the harsh environment outside. The main common room is dominated by a large fireplace, where crackling logs ward off the evening chill. Rough-hewn wooden tables and chairs are scattered throughout the room, occupied by a mix of sailors, traders, adventurers, and locals. The air is thick with the aroma of roasted meats, spiced ale, and the ever-present scent of salt. The walls are adorned with nautical charts, framed portraits of famous sea captains, and trophies of past hunts and voyages. From the windows, patrons can watch the sun sink below the horizon, painting the sky in vibrant hues of orange and purple, while the waves crash against the cliffs far below. For those seeking a more private setting, smaller rooms are available upstairs, each offering stunning views of the sea. The inn is known for its strong ale, hearty meals, and lively atmosphere, making it a popular gathering place for those seeking respite from the dangers and hardships of life on the edge of the world.
