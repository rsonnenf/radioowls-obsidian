# The Bank of Borbir

## Description
---
![[The Bank of Borbir.jpg|right|700]]The Bank of [[Borbir]] stands as a monolithic testament to stability and security in the often-chaotic world of Kalinar. Located in the Merchant District, its imposing structure of polished granite and reinforced steel contrasts sharply with the surrounding buildings of weathered stone. Guards clad in gleaming armor stand watch at the entrance, their presence a constant reminder of the wealth contained within. Inside, the grand hall is a hushed sanctuary of polished marble floors and vaulted ceilings. Tellers work diligently behind ornate counters, handling transactions with practiced efficiency. Discreet meeting rooms line the perimeter, offering privacy for clients discussing sensitive financial matters. The air hums with a subtle magical energy, a byproduct of the powerful wards that protect the bank from intrusion.

The true marvel of the Bank of Borbir, however, lies hidden deep beneath the grand hall: the planar vault. This ingenious security measure utilizes advanced planar magic to shift the contents of the vault to a secure demiplane, accessible only through a complex ritual performed by the bank's most trusted mages. This system ensures that even the most skilled thieves or powerful magic users cannot breach the bank's defenses. The very existence of the planar vault is a closely guarded secret, known only to a select few within the bank's hierarchy. This air of mystery and impenetrable security has cemented the Bank of Borbir's reputation as one of the most secure and trusted financial institutions in all of Kalinar. Even whispers of its planar vault are enough to deter would-be robbers, leaving the bank a silent fortress amidst the bustling city.
