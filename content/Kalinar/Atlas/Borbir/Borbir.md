## Description
---
Borbir, a magnificent city perched atop towering sea cliffs on Kalinar's western coast, is a testament to human ambition and architectural ingenuity. The city's buildings, crafted from a pale, locally quarried stone, rise from the clifftop plateau, their foundations seemingly defying the dizzying drop to the Nautimore Ocean below. Intricate carvings depicting mythical creatures and scenes of the sea adorn the structures, adding an artistic touch to the otherwise practical architecture. A network of sturdy bridges spans the chasms and ravines that bisect the plateau, connecting the various districts. The constant presence of the salty air, the cries of gulls, and the distant rumble of the waves serve as a constant reminder of Borbir's precarious position. Sunsets are particularly spectacular, painting the city in hues of orange and gold as the light reflects off the vast expanse of water.


## Government
---
Borbir is governed by a council of seven known as the Cliff Lords. Each Lord represents a different district of the city and holds equal power within the council. Elections are held every five years, and the position of Head Lord rotates annually amongst the seven. The current Head Lord is Elara Stonehand, known for her pragmatic approach and dedication to strengthening Borbir's defenses against pirate raids. While the council system generally functions effectively, disagreements between Lords representing districts with competing interests can sometimes lead to political gridlock. A well-trained city guard maintains order within Borbir and protects it from external threats.
