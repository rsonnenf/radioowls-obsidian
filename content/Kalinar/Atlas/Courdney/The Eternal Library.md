## Description
---
One of the three largest libraries in Kalinar. It is a gargantuan stone building. A large stairway leads to the front entrance of the library, which is lined with massive marble columns. Inside, the Grand Hall is a massive room, standing several stories high. A female orc librarian (Dulen) sits at the front desk writing in a ledger book. She wears a white robe adorned with a blue sash.
