## Description
---
The Rusty Compass in [[Theel]] isn't known for its grandeur, but for its worn and welcoming embrace. The air hangs heavy with the scent of spilled ale, woodsmoke, and a hint of pipe tobacco. Sunlight struggles to penetrate the grime-coated windows, casting the interior in a warm, dim glow.

Rough-hewn wooden beams crisscross the low ceiling, adorned with an eclectic collection of nautical trinkets – chipped sextants, dusty model ships in glass bottles, and a dented spyglass perpetually pointed towards the distant corner.

Mismatched tables and chairs, some with more legs than strictly necessary, sprawl across the uneven flagstone floor. A worn tapestry depicting a kraken battling a galleon hangs crookedly behind the bar, its vibrant colors faded with time.

Behind the bar itself, a burly barkeep with a perpetually raised eyebrow and a well-worn leather apron dispenses tankards of ale and mugs of mead. In the corner, a flickering fireplace throws dancing shadows on the wall, where a group of adventurers, their armor dented and cloaks patched, huddle over a map, their hushed voices punctuated by the rhythmic clinking of dice. The Rusty Compass isn't fancy, but it offers a haven for weary travelers, a place to share stories, drown sorrows, or celebrate victories – a true adventurer's haven.
