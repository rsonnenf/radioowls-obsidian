
## Description
---
Once a vibrant city before the Breaking, Skuggahvelfing is now a dead and decaying city. A shadow has descended upon it, brought about by dark and undead creatures that have now made it their home. Few buildings remain standing, but one of the few intact structures, a tower, reaches into the sky.

The tower is Myrkur. It was once a palace. It houses the entrance to a labyrinth of catacombs that stretch underneath the city. In a massive vault in the catacombs lie many treasures including, what some believe, is the sole remaining [[Orbs of Dragonkind|Orb of Dragonkind]] in existence. The dark creatures were gathering powerful items as offerings to their leader.
