# Den Fatum

## Description
---
![[Den Fatum Descriptive 1.webp|right|500]]Den Fatum is a large Dwarven realm under the mountains in [[The Forgotten Sands]]. Once known as the "Forge of Kalinar," Den Fatum had served as an exporter of the finest ironwork on the continent. The city became more isolationist after other realms began taking advantage of its work and goods, demanding more without appropriate compensation. The dwarves of the city became distrustful of outsiders, eventually shutting itself off nearly completely with the outside world for the last 40 years. 



The city is a model of Dwarven architecture. Built into a vast chamber, great forges and fires light the stone streets, which stand atop a deep cavern. Its robust economic infrastructure, centered around its mining, and food sustainability have allowed the city to exist in isolation, where its distrust of outsiders has festered and grown. The dwarves of Den Fatum are very protective of their home and are very reluctant to allow outsiders in. They are especially reluctant to allow outsiders into their mines, which delve deep into the mountain.  

## Government
---
Den Fatum is led by a king, [[Fargrim Ironfist]]. He is advised by a ruling council of seven dwarves, one from each of the seven wards in the city.
