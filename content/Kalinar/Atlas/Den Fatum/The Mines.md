# The Mines

## Description
---
The mines under [[Den Fatum]] are legendary among dwarves, known throughout the land for their vast wealth and unparalleled mineral deposits. The mines are said to be located deep beneath the mountain Den Fatum is carved in, which is known as [[the Cornerstone]], accessible only through a series of treacherous tunnels and caverns. The dwarves have been mining these tunnels for generations, using their expertise and skill to extract the precious metals, gems, and minerals that lie hidden within the earth.

The mines are vast and complex, with tunnels and caverns stretching for miles in every direction. The dwarves have mapped out these tunnels meticulously, marking the most valuable mineral deposits and keeping track of any shifts or changes in the earth that could affect their mining operations. The mines are divided into several different sections, each with its own unique challenges and rewards.

One of the most revered sections of the mines is the Great Vein, a vast deposit of pure silver that runs deep beneath the mountain. The silver from this vein is said to be the purest and most valuable in the land, and the dwarves have guarded it fiercely for generations. Another important section of the mines is the Crystal Caverns, a network of tunnels and caverns filled with sparkling crystals and rare gems of every color.

The extensive mining has made parts of the cavern incredibly unstable. Less-explored sections are prone to collapse at a moment's notice. Despite the dangers and challenges that come with mining these treacherous tunnels, the dwarves consider it a sacred duty to uncover the riches hidden beneath the earth. The mines under Den Fatum are a source of great pride and tradition for the dwarven people, and they will stop at nothing to protect and preserve this valuable resource for future generations.

## Encounters/Quests
---
1.  [[The Guards of the Mines]] : The entrance to the Mines is heavily guarded by dwarven soldiers who are responsible for keeping outsiders out. The party might need to persuade or deceive the guards to let them pass, or they could try to sneak past them. However, the guards are highly trained and observant, so getting past them won't be easy.
   
2. [[Collapsing Chamber]]: The Mines are carved deep into the mountain, and the tunnels can be unstable. The party might encounter a section of tunnel that has collapsed, forcing them to find another way in. Alternatively, they might witness a collapse as they are trying to navigate through the tunnels, putting them in danger.
   
3. Trap: The dwarves have set up a variety of traps and alarms throughout the Mines to deter intruders. The party might trigger a trap, such as a falling rock or a pit trap, or set off an alarm that alerts the guards to their presence.

4. [[Finding Grummsh]]: [[The Works In Progress]] delve deeper into [[The Mines|the Mines]] in the hopes of finding a portal leading to the Underrealm and to Grummsh.

5. [[The Dwarven Ghosts]]: [[The Works In Progress]] encounter several Dwarven ghosts asking for help.

6. [[The Unstable Explosives]]: A room containing several unstable explosives will go off if they are disturbed.

7. [[The Secret Brewery]]: A secret brewery in the Mines was run by several renegade dwarves who were unhappy with the [[Anvil and Ale]].
