# The King's Hall

## Description
---
As you enter the great hall of the dwarven king, you are immediately struck by the grandeur and magnificence of the space. The walls are made of solid stone, intricately carved with images of great battles and heroic deeds. The floor is made of polished marble, reflecting the flickering light of torches and lanterns that hang from the ceiling.

The hall is spacious, with high vaulted ceilings that reach up to the rafters. The air is thick with the smell of ale and roasted meat, and the sound of merriment echoes off the walls.

The floor of the throne room is made of worn stone, with intricate patterns etched into its surface. Dwarven artisans have worked tirelessly to create a space that is both functional and beautiful, and the throne room is a testament to their skill and dedication.

At the far end of the hall, there is a raised dais where the king sits upon a throne of solid stone and precious gems.
