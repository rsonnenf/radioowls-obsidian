# Anvil & Ale
## Description
---
The Anvil and Ale tavern is a popular spot located in the Market Quarter of Den Fatum. It's known for its hearty food, frothy beers, and lively atmosphere, making it a popular gathering place for merchants, miners, and travelers.

The tavern has a rustic, warm feel with low-hanging wooden beams and a cozy fire pit in the center of the room. The bar is made from a single block of polished granite and the walls are adorned with weapons and tools forged by local smiths.

The menu is a mix of traditional dwarven dishes and exotic fare from far-off lands, served with mugs of locally brewed ales.
## Notable People
---
### [[Balor Hammerstrike]]
The owner of the Anvil and Ale tavern. Balor stands about 4 feet tall and has blue eyes and a broad, muscular build, with a thick beard and long hair the color of burnished copper. his face is weathered and lined with the deep creases of a lifetime spent working with his hands.

He is often found smiling and has a contagious grin that's quick to spread across his face. He wears a simple leather apron over his sturdy tunic and breeches, and his arms are adorned with bracelets and bangles of silver and gold, marking him as a successful businessman.

Despite his rough exterior, Balor is a kind-hearted soul and is well-loved by his patrons. When he's not tending bar, he can often be found chatting and laughing with his customers or regaling them with tales from his youth. Overall, Balor is the epitome of a warm and welcoming tavern-keeper, and his Anvil and Ale is a place where anyone can feel at home.

### Drogath
A grizzled veteran of the mines, Drogath can often be found regaling anyone who will listen with tales of his exploits deep in the earth. He has a love for good drink and a hearty meal and is a regular at the Anvil and Ale.

### [[Grunhilda]]
A renowned blacksmith, Grunhilda is a fixture of the Market Quarter. She's known for her work creating beautiful and functional pieces of armor and weapons, and is always happy to chat with fellow artisans and admirers.

### Darin
A master brewer and one of the city's most respected artisans, Darin is known for his unique and flavorful ales. He's a regular at the Anvil and Ale and is always happy to chat about the finer points of brewing and share a taste of his latest creation.

### Dwalin
A talented craftsman and sculptor, Dwalin is known for his intricate and beautiful works of art. He's a frequent patron of the Anvil and Ale and is always happy to discuss his craft and show off his latest creations. Whether it's carving intricate figurines or creating stunning jewelry, Dwalin is a true master of his craft.
